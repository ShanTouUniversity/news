Zhicheng College and the "Digital Red Tide" Counselor Studio have announced the winners of the 1st STU Dormitory Culture Festival's "Digital Red Heritage, Dormitory Original Aspiration" contest — a digital creation competition centered on Chaoshan red culture.

Students submitted digital works including short videos and electronic posters inspired by red culture themes such as the Maozhou Meeting, Seven Days of Red Chaoshan, Fenghuang Mountain Anti-Japanese Base, and Wartime Overseas Chinese Remittances. The submissions showcased both the results of dormitory group reading and students' creative talents.

## Winners

| Prize | Work Title | Creator (Dorm) |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| First Prize | *Sparks of Southern Guangdong, Youth Long March* | Hongyi 615, Huang |
| Second Prize | *Mountain Stories from the Dorm* | Dexin g442, Zhu |
| Second Prize | *Southern Guangdong Hot Land, Chaoshan Red Soul* | Building E 531, Lin |
| Third Prize | *Chaoshan Flames of War* | New Building 3-424, Yang |
| Third Prize | *Surging Tide* | New Building 3-713, Yang |

Winners will receive certificates of honor and prizes. Collection details will be announced separately.
