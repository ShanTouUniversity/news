During National Disaster Prevention and Mitigation Month, Zhicheng College and the College of Science are jointly hosting the **7th edition of the "Film and Ideology" series**, themed "Earthquake Safety, Safeguarding Growth." The event commemorates the May 12 Wenchuan earthquake and responds to recent seismic events with practical safety education through three activities: DIY peace sachets, a disaster-themed film screening, and group discussion.

## Schedule

- **Theme**: Guard Safety Through Disaster Prevention, Empower Growth Through Film
- **Date**: Saturday, May 23
- **Location**: Room E104, East Coast Campus

### Part 1: Peace Sachet DIY (6:00–7:00 PM)

Using natural herbal ingredients such as agarwood, rose, lavender, and lemongrass, participants will grind, blend, and fill persimmon-shaped sachets symbolizing peace and well-being — combining traditional craftsmanship with safety awareness.

### Part 2: Film Screening & Discussion (7:00–9:30 PM)

A curated disaster and rescue film will be screened to teach earthquake preparedness, emergency response, and mutual aid through compelling storytelling. Candidate films include *The Peak*, *Shock Wave 2*, *The Wandering Earth*, *The Rescue*, and *Aftershock*.

After the screening, participants will discuss topics such as "What to do when an earthquake strikes," "Earthquake safety on campus," and "Youth responsibility in disaster prevention."

## Participation Notes

- Open to all students and faculty of Zhicheng College and the College of Science
- Scan the QR code in the original OA notice to join the activity WeChat group for registration
- Full participants earn credits through the Dao Meng Kong Jian platform (sign-in and sign-out required)
- Seating is limited — first come, first served
