## Overview

The Admissions Office is recruiting multiple student assistants from the 2024 and 2025 cohorts.

## Responsibilities

- Daily office tasks (document management, phone, email)
- Admissions promotion events and school visits
- Writing, editing, and designing promotional materials
- Data collection and analysis
- Social media operations

## Requirements

- Proficient in office software, strong communication skills
- Responsible and professional
- Photoshop/Illustrator skills preferred
- Writing or social media experience preferred

## How to Apply

Send your resume (PDF) to **o_zsb@stu.edu.cn** by **June 3 (Wednesday), 12:00 PM**, with the subject: Name + Application for Admissions Office Student Assistant. Join the WeChat group by June 2.
