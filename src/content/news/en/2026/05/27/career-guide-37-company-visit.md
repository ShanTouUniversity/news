## Overview

The College of Science and Zhicheng College are hosting the 37th "Career Guide" event, organizing faculty and student representatives from the Physics Department to visit three leading companies.

**Time**: May 27–29

**Destinations**: Desay Battery (Huizhou), Bambu Lab (Shenzhen), ML Optic

## Activities

- **Factory tours**: Visit exhibition halls, smart manufacturing lines, and R&D labs to see physics applications in new energy, smart manufacturing, and precision optics
- **Industry discussions**: In-depth talks with HR and technical leads on industry trends, talent standards, job opportunities, and internship channels
