## Results

From May 22–24, the 15th "Challenge Cup" Guangdong College Student Entrepreneurship Competition concluded at Guangdong Technical Normal University. STU won **20 provincial awards**: 5 silver and 7 bronze in the main competition, and 1 silver and 7 bronze in the Heyuan special track.

## Featured Projects

- **Gastric Ultrasound AI Diagnostic System** (Medical College): Achieves 89.5% accuracy in automated gastric wall layer segmentation using U-net models.
- **Intelligent Brain Puncture Assistance System** (Medical College): Combines deep learning and 3D printing with 89.09% accuracy; 3 software copyrights and 5 patents filed.
- **Yangming Culture + Bamboo Sea Tourism** (Engineering College): An immersive cultural tourism model for Heping County's bamboo sea (350,000+ annual visitors).

## Participation

A record **242 campus entries** from **1,361 students** were submitted, up 132 from the previous competition.
