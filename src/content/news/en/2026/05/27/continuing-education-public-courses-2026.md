## Course Topics

Guangdong Province's 2026 public courses for professional and technical personnel feature two topics:
- **AI Empowering High-Quality Development**
- **Accelerating the Development of New Productive Forces**

## Requirements

Annual requirement: minimum **90 total hours** (public ≥30, professional ≥42, elective ≥18). Choose 1 of the 2 topics and complete 30 hours to satisfy the public course requirement.

Deadline: **April 30, 2027**. No make-up sessions after the deadline.

## Platform

Faculty access the courses via the Guangdong Teacher Continuing Education platform (jsglpt.gds.edu.cn). New hires should contact the Continuing Education Center for account setup.
