## Overview

As part of STU's 45th anniversary, the College of Math & Computer Science and the Alumni Affairs Office are hosting the 7th Alumni Forum and 3rd "Alumni Talks" lecture.

**Time**: May 31 (Sunday), 3:00–4:30 PM

**Venue**: Room E128, Building E, Donghai'an Campus

## Guest Speakers

**Liu Boyang** — Principal Engineer/Architect at Meta. 2006 STU CS graduate, PhD from Rutgers University. 11+ years at Meta Reality Labs, developing real-time voice translation for Meta smart glasses. Previously worked on Alexa at Amazon.

**Li Yeqing** — Senior Staff Engineer at Google, leading the CoreML multimodal ML team. 2006 STU CS graduate, PhD from UT Arlington. 10 years at Google, leading image/video generation model development, multiple Core Tech Impact Awards. Previously at Tencent.

## Topics

- Liu Boyang: **"Development and Applications of Smart Assistants and Smart Glasses"**
- Li Yeqing: **"Current Progress and Engineering Practice of AI Agents"**

Register via the "Dream Space" app for credit hours.
