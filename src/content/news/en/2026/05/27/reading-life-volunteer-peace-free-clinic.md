## Overview

Volunteer recruitment for the "Reading for Life, Volunteer for Peace" themed free clinic has concluded. The selected volunteer list and group assignments are detailed in the attachment.

**Time**: May 30, 8:00–11:30 AM

**Venue**: Tuojiang Street Elderly Service Center

## Organizers

- Hosts: University Youth League, Medical College Youth League, First Affiliated Hospital Hospice
- Co-organizers: Student Club Management Center, Tuojiang Street Elderly Service Center, STU Hospice Volunteer Team

Selected volunteers should attend on time and notify staff 24 hours in advance if unable to attend.

> Source: STU OA Notice (University Youth League, Medical College Youth League, First Affiliated Hospital Hospice)
