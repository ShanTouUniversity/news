## Results

Following college nominations and rigorous evaluation by the Li Ka Shing Foundation Excellence in Teaching Award Committee, **Zhang Cuina** (Engineering College) and **Gu Junfeng** (Law School) have been selected as the 2025-2026 academic year award recipients.

Objections should be submitted in writing to the Academic Affairs Office (Admin Building West 312) by **June 2, 5:00 PM**. Tel: 86502736.
