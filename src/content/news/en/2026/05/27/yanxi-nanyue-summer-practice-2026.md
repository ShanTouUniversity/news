## Overview

The Guangdong Education Department, together with provincial agencies, has launched the 2026 "Yanxi Nanyue" summer education practice program. Open to all full-time STU students, the program runs from **May to August**.

## Three Themes

**Following the General Secretary's Footsteps** — Six routes including Reform Pioneer, Sci-Tech Innovation, Lingnan Culture, Green Guangdong, Greater Bay Area, and Common Prosperity.

**Hundred-Thousand-Ten Thousand Project Frontline** — Village research, volunteer service, tech assistance, and local history documentation.

**Passing on Red Genes** — Field-based ideological education at patriotic bases and revolutionary sites.

## Team Requirements

Each team consists of **8–13 students** and **1–2 advisors**.

## Submission

Colleges should submit reports, summaries, videos, and supporting materials to o_xssw@stu.edu.cn by **September 1, 11:00 AM**.
