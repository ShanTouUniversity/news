## Overview

To review progress in college-academy collaborative education, the university will hold a promotion meeting on **June 3 (Wednesday) at 3:00 PM** at the Sports Park 1F多功能厅, Sangpu Campus.

## Attendees

- University leaders, collaborative education领导小组 members
- College and academy deans and vice deans
- All counselors

## Agenda

1. Progress report on collaborative education
2. College dean appointment ceremony
3. Collegiate research think tank appointment ceremony
4. Future Learning Center (Round 2) unveiling
5. University leader speech

Departments should submit attendee lists to o_xsc@stu.edu.cn by June 1, 12:00 PM.
