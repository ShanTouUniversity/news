## Overview

The Youth League Committee and Student Union are launching the "Youth Marxism Engineering" FLY Leadership Training Program, themed "United for Jiexi Development, Dedicated to City Building," to promote rural revitalization through the Double Hundred Action.

## Field Research

**Time**: May 31, 8:10 AM – 7:00 PM

**Locations**: Shanhu Village, Jinhe Town; Hepo Street, Jiexi County

**Participants**: Student cadres from university-level organizations and college student unions

##成果 Sharing Session

**Time**: June 3 (venue TBD)

Register via the "Dream Space" app for credit hours. All faculty and students are welcome.
