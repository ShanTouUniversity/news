The Academic Affairs Office has published the evaluation results for the **2026 College Student Innovation Training Program**. A total of 375 university-level projects are proposed for funding, with select projects recommended for national and provincial recognition.

## Funding Breakdown

- **University-level projects**: 375 proposed
- **National-level recommendations**: 36 (including 1 national key support project)
- **Provincial-level recommendations**: 77 (including 7 provincial key support projects)

The national key support project is "Intelligent Annotation System Construction and Social Promotion for Chaoshan Overseas Chinese Remittances." Provincial key support projects include "Collaborative Governance of Agricultural Waste Under Carbon Inclusion Mechanism" and six others.
