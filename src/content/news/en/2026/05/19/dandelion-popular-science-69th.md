The College of Science and Zhicheng College, in collaboration with Shapu Primary School, are hosting the 69th edition of the **"Dandelion Pop Science"** series — a Digital Red Heritage Experience Camp on **Wednesday afternoon, May 20**. The event is organized by the faculty and students of the "Digital Red Tide: Chaoshan Red Culture Digital Practice and Communication" course.

## Theme

Digital Empowerment for Red Education, Nurturing Patriotism in Young Hearts

## Three Main Activities

### "Dialogue Across Time — A Letter to Revolutionary Martyrs" Classroom

Students watch digitized thematic videos, participate in red-history dubbing interactions, and write letters to revolutionary martyrs, creating an emotional connection across generations.

### "Road to National Salvation — Passing the Torch" Simulation

A customized Monopoly-style board game themed around the spirit of overseas Chinese remittances. Students roll dice, complete challenges, and accumulate "patriotic wealth" while simulating the arduous journeys of revolutionary predecessors. A transport simulation based on the route of comrade Yi Guangyan adds another layer of historical immersion.

### "Red Imprints — Fun Learning" Experience Booths

Themed booths on campus feature revolutionary history quizzes and digitized red culture exhibits, creating an immersive learning environment in a relaxed atmosphere.
