## Overview

On May 24, led by faculty members including Associate Director Wu Ming, over 130 students from the bridge and intelligent construction programs visited the Zhangshan-Shanwei HSR (Guangdong section) construction site for a practical session of the Bridge Engineering course.

## Activities

Students toured the project's party-building exhibition hall, smart construction control center, continuous beam construction site, and precast beam yard, learning about cantilever casting, box girder production, and intelligent construction technologies.
