## Overview

The "Eaton Cup" Laser Gimbal Electronic Design Competition was held at Sangpu Campus. Seven teams tackled challenges in precision beam control, dynamic target tracking, anti-interference, and multi-scenario adaptation.

Results: 1 first prize, 1 second prize, and 2 third prizes were awarded. The event provided an interdisciplinary platform integrating optics, electronics, and mechanical control.
