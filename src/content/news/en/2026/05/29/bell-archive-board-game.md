## Event Overview

According to the STU Youth League Committee notice, the Literature Club and Board Game Club have jointly organized the "Bell Archive" campus culture narrative exploration board game event.

## Event Details

- **Time**: May 30 (Saturday) – May 31 (Sunday), 2026
- **Venue**: Room 330, Building E, East Coast Campus
- **Participants**: All STU students
- **Organizer**: STU Youth League Committee
- **Co-organizers**: Student Club Management Service Center, Board Game Club, Literature Club

## Activity Description

Participants select preset investigator character cards and, guided by a game master, enter a high-freedom open-ended story set on the STU campus. Players navigate the plot through their actions and skill checks, working together to uncover the truth behind the story.

For more details, follow the "Luming Literature Club" WeChat Official Account.
