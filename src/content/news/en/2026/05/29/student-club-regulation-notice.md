## Overview

The STU Student Affairs Department and the Youth League Committee jointly issued a notice on further regulating student club work, in accordance with the Ministry of Education, Communist Youth League Central Committee's university club management regulations and university-level rules.

## Key Requirements

**Registration and Approval**: All student clubs must obtain formal approval and register before operation. Unapproved groups are prohibited from conducting any activities and must be disbanded, with the organizing unit or individual bearing full responsibility.

**Activity Rules**: Club activities require collective decision-making, advisor consent, and approval from the supervising department. All promotions and event information must be reviewed by the advisor. Clubs may not conduct commercial activities, sign contracts with external parties, or accept external funding.

**Management Guidelines**:

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Membership fees | Not permitted |
| Membership limit | Maximum 2 clubs per student |
| Eligibility | Must be currently enrolled, register periodically, and attend activities |
| Club records | Constitution, structure, membership, finances, activity logs submitted each semester |
| Advisor limit | Maximum 2 clubs per advisor |
| Advisor duties | Political education, daily management, member training, issue identification and correction |

**Supervising Department Responsibilities**: Departments bear primary responsibility for club development, daily supervision, member education, and evaluation of advisor performance.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Department, Youth League Committee)
