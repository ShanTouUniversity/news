## Overview

According to the College of Science, on May 20, the College of Science and Zhicheng College, together with Shapu Primary School, organized the 70th "Dandelion Science Popularization" — a Red Digital Experience Camp activity on campus, involving students from the service-learning course "Digital Red Tide: Digital Practice and Dissemination of Chaoshan Red Culture."

## Activities

**"National Salvation Journey: Passing the Torch"**: A custom Monopoly-style board game based on the story of patriotic overseas Chinese leader Yi Guangyan, where students advanced by rolling dice and completing missions to accumulate "patriotic wealth."

**"Dialogue Across Time: A Letter to Revolutionary Martyrs"**: Students watched videos about revolutionary martyrs, participated in voice-dubbing activities, and wrote letters to the martyrs from a contemporary youth perspective.

**Red Digital Experience Booths**: Interactive quiz boards and digital red culture作品 displays created an immersive red culture learning space.
