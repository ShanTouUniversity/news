## Competition Results

According to the College of Journalism and Communication, at the 8th STU Young Faculty Teaching Competition final held on May 27 at the Sangpushan campus, college faculty members **Liu Zhonghui** and **Li Qiulin** won **second prize** and **third prize** respectively — the college's best-ever result in this competition.

## Course Highlights

Liu Zhonghui's *Public Service Advertising* course covers the characteristics, functions, communication models, and operational frameworks of public service advertising, spanning topic selection, creative expression, media integration, and效果 evaluation. The course integrates AIGC and human-computer interaction technologies, adding interactive teaching modules while incorporating social issues such as fire safety, integrity culture, and urban development into the classroom.

Li Qiulin's *Introduction to Journalism* is a required course for first-year journalism and international journalism majors. It combines core journalism knowledge with cutting-edge topics including international communication and media convergence, enhancing students' social science literacy through an interdisciplinary lens.

## Competition Overview

The competition was jointly organized by the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment, the Trade Union, and the Academic Affairs Office. It was divided into Group 1 (Liberal Arts & Ideological-Political) and Group 2 (Sciences, Engineering & Medical), with each group awarding 1 grand prize, 2 first prizes, 3 second prizes, and 9 third prizes.
