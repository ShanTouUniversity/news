## Final Results

According to the STU Youth League Committee and Student Union, the 33rd STU Top 10 Singers Competition final concluded successfully on May 16 at the East Coast campus. The judging panel announced the following results:

| Rank | Name | College | Major |
|------|------|---------|-------|
| Champion | Yan L. | Engineering | 2023 Communications Engineering |
| Runner-up | Zhao Z. | Medicine | 2022 English |
| Third Place | Chen D. | Medicine | 2023 Clinical Medicine |

For more details, follow the "STU Student Union" WeChat Official Account.
