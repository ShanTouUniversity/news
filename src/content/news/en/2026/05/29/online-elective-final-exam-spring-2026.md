## Exam Schedule

According to the Academic Affairs Office notice, the spring 2026 online general education elective final exam will be held in Week 14 as follows:

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Date | June 7, 2026 (Sunday, Week 14) |
| Time | 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM (submit by 4:00 PM) |
| Duration | 60 minutes per exam (overtime = invalid) |
| Eligibility | ≥80% course completion by June 1 |
| Mode | Self-service login to respective learning platform |

## Important Notes

**Login**: Students should verify their platform account and password in advance. Follow the操作指南 to access the correct platform — logging into the wrong platform will result in no score.

**Proctoring**: Exam behavior monitoring will be enabled. Any irregularities will be handled according to university regulations.

**Deferral & Make-up**: Online courses do not qualify for deferred exams or make-up exams.

For questions, contact the course teaching assistant.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
