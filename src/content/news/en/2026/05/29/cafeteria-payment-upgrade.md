## Upgrade Notice

According to the Resource Management Office, Network and Information Center, and East Coast Campus Management Committee, the POS payment terminals in all campus cafeterias have been upgraded to support QR code payments. Starting **June 1, 2026**, third-party payment devices such as "Shouqianba" will be discontinued.

## Accepted Payment Methods

| Campus | Accepted Methods |
|--------|-----------------|
| Sangpushan Campus | Campus card (physical) or STU campus WeChat code |
| East Coast Campus | Campus card (physical) or WeChat Pay |

Students and staff are recommended to use the campus card for traceable transactions, fund security, and linked benefits.

## Top-Up

Recharge online via the Alipay app: search for "campus card," select the university, and enter your name and card number.

## Contact

For lost, damaged, or balance issues with campus cards, contact the Network and Information Center: 86503400 ext. 2, or 13556307566.
