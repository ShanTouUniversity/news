## Recruitment

According to the STU Bidding Center notice, the university is expanding its bid evaluation expert database for independent procurement projects in accordance with university procurement regulations.

## Expert Categories

The expert database covers the following categories:

| Category | Scope |
|----------|-------|
| Goods Technical Expert | Instruments, equipment, materials, books, textbooks, medical supplies |
| Engineering Technical Expert | New construction, renovation, expansion, demolition, repair, design, supervision |
| Services Technical Expert | Property management, asset auctions |
| Economic Expert | Participates in bidding evaluation across goods, engineering, and services |

## Basic Requirements

- At least intermediate professional title or equivalent, with five years of relevant experience
- Familiarity with bidding laws and regulations
- Ability to perform duties with integrity and impartiality

## Application

Applications may be submitted via **individual application** or **departmental recommendation** (with prior consent). Materials include the registration form and copies of degree and professional certificates.
