## Awareness Campaign

According to the Resource Management Office (STU Hospital), tobacco use remains a major public health threat, with smoking and secondhand exposure linked to respiratory diseases, cardiovascular conditions, and malignancies. To support the Healthy China initiative and promote a smoke-free campus, the hospital is conducting a tobacco control awareness campaign.

The 2026 **World No Tobacco Day** theme is **"Youth Without Tobacco, Unlimited Future."** The hospital has compiled information on tobacco hazards and cessation methods for all students and staff, urging everyone to firmly refuse that first cigarette and远离 tobacco for a healthier future.
