## Public Consultation

According to the STU Discipline Inspection Committee notice, to strengthen collective leadership and enhance democratic and scientific decision-making in committee meetings, the Committee has revised and improved the trial version of the "STU Discipline Inspection Committee Meeting Decision-Making Rules" and drafted a revised version for public consultation.

## How to Provide Feedback

Comments and suggestions should be sent by email to **o_jjs@stu.edu.cn** before **June 5, 2026 (Friday)**.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Discipline Inspection Committee)
