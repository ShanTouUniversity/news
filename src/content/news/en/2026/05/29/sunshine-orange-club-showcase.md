## Overview

According to the STU Youth League Committee notice, the "Sunshine Orange" Student Club Showcase for the 2025-2026 academic year will be held to enrich campus cultural life and promote club development.

## Event Details

- **Time**: May 30, 2026 (Saturday), 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM
- **Venue**: Main Auditorium Entrance, Sangpushan Campus
- **Organizer**: STU Youth League Committee
- **Co-organizer**: STU Student Club Management Service Center

## Activities

The event will feature booth games hosted by multiple student clubs, allowing different organizations to showcase their unique characteristics and interact with attendees.

## Notes

Please follow staff guidance to participate in an orderly manner. For more details, follow the STU Student Club Management Service Center WeChat Official Account.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Youth League Committee, Student Club Management Service Center)
