## Overview

According to the STU Graduate School notice, the 2026 Outstanding Graduating Graduate Student selection has commenced to recognize graduates who have excelled academically, ethically, and in extracurricular activities.

## Eligibility and Quota

Applicants must be 2026 graduating graduate students. The award is limited to **no more than 3%** of graduating graduate students per college, rounded to the nearest whole number (minimum one per college).

## Basic Requirements

- Average score of **80 or above** in degree courses
- Prior university-level honors preferred, or outstanding performance in a specific area
- Academic integrity and compliance with university regulations
- Active participation in campus culture, research, social practice, and volunteer activities
- Preference given to graduates volunteering for western China, grassroots, or border region service

## Selection Process

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Application & Advisor Recommendation | Submit application form and personal事迹 report |
| College Review | Evaluate candidates and publish results for 3 working days |
| Graduate School Approval | Final list published university-wide for 3 working days |

## Deadlines

Colleges must complete candidate evaluation by **June 8**. Electronic materials must be sent to chxwang@stu.edu.cn by **June 9**. Late submissions will be disqualified.
