## Database Overview

According to the STU Library notice, the Wanfang Local Gazetteers Database is now available for trial. This database integrates GIS technology to provide a digital, visualized, spatiotemporal interactive platform covering Chinese local gazetteer resources across historical periods and social domains.

The database currently includes **over 60,000 new local gazetteers** and **approximately 136,000 volumes of historical gazetteers** (including about 42,000 text-digitized volumes).

## Trial Access

- **Access URL**: https://www.lib.stu.edu.cn/database/302
- **Trial End Date**: August 28, 2026

Feedback and suggestions can be sent to the Library Subject Services (Phone: 86503247, Email: zhzeng@stu.edu.cn).
