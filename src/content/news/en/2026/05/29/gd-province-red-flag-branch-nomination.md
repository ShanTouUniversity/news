## Nomination Notice

According to the STU Youth League Committee notice, in response to the Guangdong Provincial Committee's call for the 2026 "Two Reds and Two Excellents" evaluation, the following nomination has been approved after departmental application, college recommendation, and committee review:

The **Dental Hospital Directly-Affiliated Youth League Branch of STU Medical College** is nominated to compete for the **"Guangdong Province May Fourth Red Flag League Branch"** award.

## Objection Period

Any objections should be submitted in writing or by phone to the Youth League Committee before **12:00 PM on Thursday, June 4, 2026**. Departmental submissions must bear an official seal; individual submissions must include the reporter's real name.
