## Call for Submissions

According to STU Youth, a graduation message wall short poem collection has been launched under the theme "The Last Stroke for STU." All graduates, current students, and faculty are invited to express their gratitude to the university, farewell to friends, reflections on youth, and hopes for the future through short poetry.

## Requirements

- **Format**: Short poems,微型 poems, or three-line verses
- **Eligibility**: All STU graduates, current students, and faculty
- **Deadline**: **June 5, 2026 (Friday), 11:59 PM**

Selected works will be featured in the 2026 Graduation Special Issue print edition.

## How to Submit

Send your work to **25qliu@stu.edu.cn** with the subject "Graduation Message Wall Submission." Submissions must be original and unpublished. Contributors may use pseudonyms for publication but must provide real names and contact information for coordination.
