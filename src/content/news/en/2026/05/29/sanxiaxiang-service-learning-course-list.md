## Student List

According to the STU Youth League Committee and Young Volunteers Association, the recruitment for the 2026 "Three Rural Areas" (culture, technology, health) service-learning course has been completed. A total of 67 students participated in interviews, and **41 students** were selected as course participants.

Selected students are advised to prepare for the upcoming activities.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Youth League Committee, Young Volunteers Association)
