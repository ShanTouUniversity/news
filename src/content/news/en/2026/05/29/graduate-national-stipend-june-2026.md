## Stipend List

According to the STU Graduate Affairs Office notice, the June 2026 national graduate stipend list has been finalized after individual applications, college-level review, and university-level approval.

A total of **2,845 graduate students** will receive the stipend, including **147 doctoral candidates** and **2,698 master's candidates**.

## Objections

Any objections should be submitted in writing to the Graduate Affairs Office (Room 403, Xingxi Building) before **5:30 PM on Thursday, June 4, 2026**. Departmental submissions must bear an official seal; individual submissions must include the reporter's real name.
