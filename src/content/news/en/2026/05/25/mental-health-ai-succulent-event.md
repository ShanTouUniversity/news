## Overview

The College of Mathematics and Computer Science, together with Shanhai College, is hosting a mental health event themed "Green Minds, Heal with Wisdom," combining AI technology with nature experiences. It will take place on **May 27 at 3:00 PM** in Rooms 316–318, Building E, Donghai'an Campus.

## Activities

**Board Games for Joy** – Participants choose from strategy, language, and logic games in a relaxed atmosphere.

**AI Heals with Succulents** – AI reads emotional states and recommends succulent combinations; students craft personalized succulent art under AI guidance.

**Flower Language with AI** – AI suggests flowers based on mood; students select, trim, and arrange flowers to create unique bouquets.

## Organization

The event is co-organized by the Math Department Undergraduate Party Branch, the 2025 Math and Statistics League Branches, and the 2024 Computer Science Graduate League Branch.
