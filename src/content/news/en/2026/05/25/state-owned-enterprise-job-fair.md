## Fair Details

The "Employment Benefits, Warm Hearts" Shantou 2026 State-Owned Enterprise Job Fair will be held on **May 29 (Friday) from 2:30–5:30 PM** at **Library Room 30A, Sangpushan Campus**.

Participating employers include Shantou Investment Holding Group, Shantou Ultrasonic Display, Shantou Ultrasonic PCB, Shantou Jian'an Group, Guangdong Shanliang Industrial Investment, Shantou Guolian Pharmaceutical, Shantou Yuehai Water, among others.

Positions cover R&D process engineer, chemical engineer, general management, project management, HR, live-streaming specialist, food management, etc., targeting majors in applied chemistry, materials science, physics, mechanical, civil engineering, accounting, business administration, marketing, law, Chinese language, CS, and electronic science.

## Shuttle Service

Donghai'an Campus students can book a shuttle at **1:40 PM** to Sangpushan (return at 5:00 PM). Register by **May 28 at 2:00 PM** via the QR code.

## External Participants

Graduates from other universities must apply for campus access by May 29 at 12:00 PM via the mini-program.
