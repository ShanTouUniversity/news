## Construction Overview

According to a notice from the Infrastructure Department and Donghai'an Campus Management Committee, the original contractor of Phase II of Donghai'an Campus will carry out balcony drainage pipe renovation at dormitory buildings C, D, and E from **May 25 to May 30, 2026**.

Construction will take place on the ground floor and second-floor elevated areas. **Brief water outages** may occur during the work. Students are advised to follow dorm management notices and avoid entering construction warning areas for safety.
