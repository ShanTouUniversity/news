## Overview

The Engineering College Party Committee is recruiting approximately **30 members** for the 2nd "Red Star Navigation Group," including 1 leader, 2 deputy leaders, and four functional departments.

## Requirements

- Open to 2024/2025 undergraduates and 2025 master's students
- Party members,预备党员, or Youth League members preferred
- Must serve for one full academic year with no failing grades

## Structure

| Department | Responsibilities |
|------------|------------------|
| **Organization** | Recruitment, evaluation, internal training |
| **Training** | Navigation lectures, role model events, party training |
| **Practice** | Research leadership, community service, surveys |
| **Publicity** | Event promotion, posters, news writing |

## Timeline

**Registration**: May 26–30

**Interviews**: Early June
