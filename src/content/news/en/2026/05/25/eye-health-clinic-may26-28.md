## Overview

Ahead of the 31st National "6·6 Eye Love Day," STU has partnered with the Joint Shantou International Eye Center to bring eye health lectures and free clinic services to students, faculty, and their families.

The event is guided by the STU Youth League Committee and organized by the Eye Center's Youth League Branch.

## Expert Teams

- **Sangpushan Campus**: Chief Physician Zhang Riping's team (PhD, master's supervisor, Lingnan famous doctor), specializing in复杂 refractive cases and personalized myopia prevention
- **Donghai'an Campus**: Dr. Zeng Jinhui's team (Lingnan famous doctor)

## Schedule

| Campus | Date | Time | Venue |
|--------|------|------|-------|
| Sangpushan | May 26 (Tue) | Clinic 4:00–6:00 PM, Lecture 4:30–5:00 PM | Hongyi College 1F Multi-function Hall |
| Donghai'an | May 28 (Thu) | Clinic 4:00–6:00 PM, Lecture 4:30–5:00 PM | Teaching Building E, Room 105 |

## Activities

**Lecture**: Focusing on common eye health issues among college students, prevention strategies, and scientific management.

**Free Clinic**: Vision test, computer refraction, comprehensive eye exam, and individual report interpretation.

## Perks

Participants receive:
- Free eye exam and refraction vouchers from the Eye Center
- 15% discount coupon for glasses
- Discount coupon for corneal refractive surgery pre-op package

The event is free and open to all students and staff.
