## Dinner Details

According to the Student Affairs Office, registration for the 84th Leader-Student Dinner (Engineering College & Jingyi College session) has reached full capacity. The list of successfully registered students has been announced.

The dinner will be held on **May 27 (Wednesday) at 6:00 PM** at **Private Room 2, Academic Exchange Center, Sangpushan Campus**. Successful registrants should arrive 10 minutes early for check-in.
