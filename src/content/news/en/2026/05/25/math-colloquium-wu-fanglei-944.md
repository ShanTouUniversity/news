## Abstract

The talk establishes a sharp off-diagonal pointwise upper estimate for the Bergman reproducing kernel associated with a radial weight on the unit disc that satisfies a natural one-sided doubling condition on its moments. The standard kernels demonstrate that this estimate is sharp, up to a multiplicative constant, at every point. The estimate in fact characterizes the class of radial doubling weights under consideration.

## About the Speaker

Dr. Wu Fanglei received his PhD from both Shantou University and the University of Eastern Finland. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher at UEF, working on analytic function spaces and operator theory. His research has been published in *Adv. Math.*, *Math. Z.*, *J. G. A.*, among others.
