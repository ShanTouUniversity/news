## Overview

The 2026 National Digital Literacy Improvement Practice Activity (30th Teacher Activity) is now open for submissions from all STU faculty members, as announced by the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment.

## Submission Categories

**Regular Projects** (courseware, micro-lectures, IT-based courses): 5 entries max per school. Submit to o_fdea@stu.edu.cn **by Aug 5**.

**Higher Education Special Projects**:
- Virtual simulation experiment teaching cases: 5 entries max, up to 5 authors
- Virtual simulation lab management cases: 5 entries max, up to 3 authors
- Large instrument innovation & application cases: 5 entries max, up to 5 authors
Submit **by Jul 28**.

**Educational Technology Papers**: up to 3 authors, 1 paper per person. Submit **by Jun 31**.
