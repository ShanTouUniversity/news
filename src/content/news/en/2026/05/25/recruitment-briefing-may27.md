## Overview

According to the People's Armed Forces Department, the Guangdong Provincial People's Government Recruitment Office is organizing province-wide military recruitment briefings featuring outstanding veteran college students. The STU session will be held on **May 27 (Wednesday) at 9:30 AM** at the **Library Lecture Hall, Sangpushan Campus**.

## Agenda

- **Recruitment briefing by the provincial team**
- **Policy Q&A**: registration process, medical standards, preferential policies
- **Military career development sharing session**
- **Interactive discussion on recruitment policies**

## Attendance

Colleges are requested to organize 2026 graduates to attend. Students from other grades who are interested in enlistment are also welcome. Participants should arrive 15 minutes early.

> Source: STU OA Notice (People's Armed Forces Department)
