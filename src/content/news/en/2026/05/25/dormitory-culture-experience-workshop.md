## Overview

The 1st STU Dormitory Culture Festival presents "Crafting Dorms, Tech & Elegance" — a dormitory culture experience workshop to be held on **May 29 (Friday) from 7:30–9:30 PM** at the **Jingyi College Building D Courtyard, Sangpushan Campus**. All students are welcome.

## Activities

**Tech & Innovation Exhibition**: Featuring outstanding works from the Science & Technology Festival, including 3D printing, smart robotics, engineering imagery, and architectural design exhibits.

**Warm Dormitory Bonds**: Interactive stations including dormitory smiley face drawing, message boards, yarn crafts, and a dormitory culture micro-lecture. Complete tasks to redeem cultural merchandise.

## Organizers

Host: Student Affairs Office | Organizers: Jingyi College, "智创π" Counselor Studio | Co-organizers: Jingyi College Student Association, Engineering College Student Union
