## Overview

The "Blue Sky Encounter" creative study service course at the Law School is now recruiting students. The course offers multiple projects in the spring practice week and fall semester. Students must reside at Donghai'an Campus; cross-campus enrollment is not accepted.

## Spring Practice Week Course (Early July)

| Project | Description | Slots |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Jiexi Fengjiang Town Cultural Co-Research Summer Camp | Based in Fengjiang Town, Jiexi County, conducting research on local culture with primary and secondary school students. Recruiting students skilled in photography, social research, or with teaching experience | 30 |

## Fall Semester Courses (Wednesday PM; Saturday for Tianxi)

| Project | Description | Slots |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Primary School Sports & Arts Teaching | Conducting sports and arts extracurricular activities at primary schools. Recruiting students with美术, sports, or music talents | 60 |
| Xuekang Autistic Children Art Therapy | Accompanying autistic children at Xuekang Autism Rehabilitation Center through趣味知识科普, crafts, and智力 games | 30 |
| Middle School Reading Promotion | Themed "Reading Changes Life, Reading Changes Destiny" — sharing and discussing好书 with middle school students in small groups | 60 |
| Tianxi Community Cultural Exchange | Organizing Chinese classics reading and Chaoshan intangible cultural heritage experiences in Tianxi Community, Longhu District | 40 |

## Experience Event

To help students learn about the course, an experience event will be held on **May 27 (Wednesday) at 2:30 PM** in **Teaching Building E103, Donghai'an Campus**. Participants can receive priority for course selection.
