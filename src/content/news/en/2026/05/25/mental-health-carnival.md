## Overview

The Engineering College and Jingyi College will host a Mental Health Carnival themed "Sunshine in the Heart, Health Together" on **May 27 (Wednesday) from 7:30–9:00 PM** at the **front square of Jingyi College Phase II Building 1, Sangpushan Campus**. All Engineering and Jingyi College students are welcome.

## Activities

Six interactive booths with stamp collection for prizes:

| Booth | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| **Clay Creation** | Freely shape clay art pieces to take home |
| **Wind Chime DIY** | Assemble and paint your own wind chime |
| **Bookmark DIY** | Create personalized bookmarks with dried flowers |
| **Psychological Board Game** | Roll dice, answer psychology questions, complete challenges |
| **Emotional Tree Hole** | Anonymously share worries or write encouraging messages |
| **Yarn Art** | Wind yarn around themed words to create happiness art |

The event counts toward the aesthetic education credit for Engineering College students' comprehensive evaluation.
