## Overview

The Graduate Affairs Office and the Youth League Committee, hosted by the STU Graduate Student Union, have launched the "Youth Is for Reading" — 2026 Graduate Book Review Competition.

**Participants**: All graduate students

**Timeline**:
- Submission: May 25–29
- Judging: May 29 – June 1
- Offline sharing & awards: June 3

## Requirements

Choose books from five categories: Party theory, Chinese traditional culture, revolutionary classics, tech frontiers, and literary works. Write **300–500 word** reviews. One entry per person.

Submit to: 25szlin@stu.edu.cn (Format: College-Grade-Major-Name-Phone)

## Judging

- Preliminary screening, then online voting (30%) + expert review (70%)
- Scoring criteria: content value, writing quality, emotional authenticity
