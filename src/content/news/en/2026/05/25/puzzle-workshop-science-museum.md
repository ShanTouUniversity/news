## Overview

The Engineering College Youth League Committee, in collaboration with Shantou Science Museum, will host a "Puzzle Workshop: Decoding the Science Museum" event on **May 30** at **Shantou Science Museum**, targeting local primary school students and their parents.

## Activities

Family teams will explore:

- Exhibition on Chaoshan scientists
- Shantou science & technology development history exhibition
- Science discovery zone
- Simulated human anatomy lab
- AI experience hall

Participants solve science puzzles to learn scientific principles.

## Organizers

Host: Engineering College Youth League Committee, Shantou Science Museum

Organizers: Engineering College "科启青锋" Practice Team, Technology Creation Association
