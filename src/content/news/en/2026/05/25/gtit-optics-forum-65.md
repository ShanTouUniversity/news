## Forum Info

The 65th STU-GTIIT Optics Forum will be held on **June 8, 2026 (Tuesday) at 9:00 AM** in **Lecture Hall 306, Physics Department**, featuring two professors from the University of the Free State, South Africa.

## Talk 1

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Title | The use of surface characterization techniques in phosphor materials |
| Speaker | **Prof Hendrik C. Swart** (Dept. of Physics, UFS) |

Prof Swart is a senior professor and B1 NRF-rated researcher who introduced luminescent materials research to South Africa. He has published over 990 journal papers with an H-index of 80, ranking 7th worldwide in the Phosphors discipline (Lifetime). His research focuses on luminescent materials for displays, solar cells, solid-state lighting, dosimetry, and thermometry.

## Talk 2

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Title | Morphology-Controlled Multilayer Deposition for CZTS Solar Cells and Quantitative ToF-SIMS Analysis of Indium Segregation from Copper |
| Speaker | **Prof J.J. Terblans** (Dept. of Physics, UFS) |

Prof Terblans is Head of the Physics Department and Director of the Centre for Microscopy at UFS (NRF C1-rated). His research focuses on solid-state physics, thin films, diffusion, segregation, and advanced materials characterization using XPS, AES, SEM, TEM, and ToF-SIMS. He has authored over 150 peer-reviewed publications.
