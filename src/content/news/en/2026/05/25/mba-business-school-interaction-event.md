## Overview

On May 21, the MBA Education Center of the Business School, supported by the college labor union, held a themed business-school exchange event at Administrative Building C315, Donghai'an Campus. The event was supported by alumni enterprise **China Minsheng Bank Shantou Branch**.

## Activities

The event featured a **succulent plant DIY experience** and casual tea break networking, attracting active participation from faculty members. Teachers engaged in hands-on crafting while exchanging ideas on teaching, work, and daily life.

Representatives from Minsheng Bank discussed talent cultivation and校企 cooperation with faculty, laying the groundwork for future collaboration in MBA talent development, corporate management training, and resource sharing.
