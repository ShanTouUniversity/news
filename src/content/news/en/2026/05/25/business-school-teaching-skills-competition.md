## Overview

According to a notice from the Business School, the college will hold its 2026 Young Teacher Teaching Skills Competition to enhance teaching quality and promote experience exchange among new faculty.

## Participants

Newly hired teachers at the Business School within the past year.

## Format

The competition consists of **materials review** and **on-site lecture demonstration**.

## Schedule

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Time | May 29, 2026 (Friday), 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM |
| Venue | Room 328, Administration Building, Donghai'an Campus |

Faculty members are welcome to attend.
