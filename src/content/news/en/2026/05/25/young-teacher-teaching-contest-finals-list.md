## Overview

According to a notice from the Labor Union, Academic Affairs Office, and Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment, the final shortlist for the 8th STU Young Teacher Teaching Competition has been confirmed after departmental recommendation, qualification review, expert online evaluation, and public notice.

## Finalists

- **Group 1** (Humanities, Ideological & Political Education): **15 teachers**
- **Group 2** (Sciences, Engineering, Medicine): **15 teachers**

A total of 30 teachers have advanced to the on-site finals.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Labor Union, Academic Affairs Office, Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment)
