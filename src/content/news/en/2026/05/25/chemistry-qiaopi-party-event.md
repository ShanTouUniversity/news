## Overview

The College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering and Zhi Xing College will hold a themed party event titled "Qiaopi Remembers初心, Gratitude Leads Forward" on **May 29 (Friday) at 7:30 PM** at **Shantou Wanda Cinema**, for student party members and 2026 graduating representatives.

## Activities

**Movie Screening**: Sponsored by a 2023 Materials Science and Engineering alumnus, the event will screen "Love Letter to Grandma" (给阿嬷的情书), a film centered on qiaopi (overseas remittance letters) culture, exploring family bonds, patriotism, and integrity.

**Sharing Salon**: Discussions围绕 qiaopi culture, family and national sentiment, gratitude, and youth responsibility, inspired by the film and the alumnus's generosity.

## Registration

Limited spots available. Eligible students should scan the QR code to join the group and complete the registration questionnaire.
