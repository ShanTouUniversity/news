## Overview

The Guangdong Provincial Education Department has launched a project to develop original, high-quality textbook series on Greater Bay Area tourism. STU is organizing participation in the selection process.

The project aims to promote the "Vibrant Guangdong, Fashionable Bay Area" cultural tourism brand, focusing on Guangdong's mountain and sea ecology, history, culture, intangible cultural heritage, and urban development.

## Project Formats

Approximately **50 projects** will be selected:

| Type | Quantity | Requirements |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Cases** | ~30 | Min. 5 cases each, ~20,000 words |
| **Lecture Notes** | ~20 | Thematic format, ~50,000 words |

All projects must be in print format with supplementary digital resources.

## Requirements

- Applications must be submitted through departmental units (individual submissions not accepted)
- Cross-institutional and cross-disciplinary collaboration encouraged
- Project leads must have strong political integrity and professional competence

## Timeline

Submit applications to your department **by June 4 (Thursday) at 5:30 PM** for departmental review and forwarding.
