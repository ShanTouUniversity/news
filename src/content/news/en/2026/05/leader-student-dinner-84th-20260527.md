The 84th edition of the University Leadership-Student Dinner, hosted by the Student Affairs Office for Engineering College and Jingyi College students, will take place on **Wednesday, May 27 at 6:00 PM** in Private Room 2 of the Sangpushan Campus Academic Exchange Service Center. The dinner aims to encourage students to share their ideas and participate in university development.

## Event Details

- **Time**: Wednesday, May 27, 6:00 PM (arrive by **5:50 PM** for check-in)
- **Location**: Private Room 2, Academic Exchange Service Center, Sangpushan Campus
- **Guests**: University leadership, department heads, college leaders, and faculty representatives
- **Format**: Opening remarks by university leadership → dinner conversation

## Registration

Interested students should register via the eHall platform (ehall.stu.edu.cn) by **Friday, May 22**. Registration will close automatically once capacity is reached. The list of invited student representatives will be published on the campus intranet.

Participants are asked to prepare at least one question or suggestion related to the development of the university or their college. Dinner is provided at the university's expense.

## Notes

Past dinner summaries are available through the STU Student Affairs and STU Student Union WeChat accounts. Students are encouraged to review them to avoid raising duplicate topics. Feedback about the dinner program can be sent to o_xsgl@stu.edu.cn.
