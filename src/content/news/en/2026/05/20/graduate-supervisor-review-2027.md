Shantou University's Graduate School has announced the launch of the **2027 Graduate Supervisor Qualification Review**. All teachers planning to recruit graduate students in 2027 must submit an application — only those who pass the review will be authorized to supervise.

## Review Criteria

The review is conducted in accordance with the "Regulations on Teachers Applying to Supervise Graduate Students at Shantou University" (STU Document [2022] No. 36) and the detailed implementation rules established by each college. The scope covers all disciplines and professional degree categories in which the university has doctoral or master's degree authorization.

## Timeline

- **Individual Application (May 20 – May 26)**: Eligible teachers must log in to the Graduate Management Information System, complete their personal information, verify research projects and representative achievements, and submit applications to their respective disciplines. Cross-disciplinary applicants should apply to the discipline they intend to supervise in.
- **Committee Review and Public Notice (by June 8)**: Each college's Degree Evaluation Sub-committee will review and determine the list of qualified supervisors, with results publicly posted for at least 5 working days.
- **Materials Submission (by June 16)**: Each unit must submit the finalized list of qualified supervisors to the Graduate School.

## Important Notes

The Graduate School requires all units to ensure that every eligible teacher is notified. Applicants must provide truthful information — any falsification, once discovered, will result in a three-year ban on graduate student recruitment applications.
