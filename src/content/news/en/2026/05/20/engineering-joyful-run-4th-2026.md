To promote a "health first" philosophy and encourage graduate students to stay active, the Engineering College is hosting the **4th Graduate Student "Youth Joy Run"** at Sangpushan Campus.

## Event Details

- **Theme**: Youth Without Limits, Engineering in Motion
- **Eligibility**: All Engineering College graduate students
- **Period**: May 21 – May 31
- **Location**: Sangpushan Campus
- **Organizers**: Engineering College Youth League Committee & Graduate Student Union

## Activities

### Distance Challenge

Track runs using the Keep app. Minimum 3 km per run; valid pace is under 6.5 min/km for men and 7.5 min/km for women.

### Weight Management Challenge

Weigh-ins on May 21 and June 1. Rankings based on weight change combined with running frequency. Participation in both weigh-ins is required.

### Campus Orienteering Race

Form teams of 1–3, navigate through checkpoints across campus, and return to the finish line.

## Awards

| Category | Prizes |
|----------|--------|
| Distance Champion (1st/2nd/3rd) | 1/2/3 winners |
| Weight Management Pioneer (1st/2nd/3rd) | 1/2/3 winners |

## Notes

- Run screenshots must be submitted by 11:00 PM, June 1 with complete data (date, distance, pace, duration, cadence, username)
- Falsified data will result in disqualification
- Participants should assess their own health and run within their limits
