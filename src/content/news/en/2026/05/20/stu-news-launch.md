## Platform Launch

An unofficial Shantou University news and information platform — **STU News** ([news.shantou.university](https://news.shantou.university/)) — driven by the [ShanTou.University](https://shantou.university/) community, went live today. The platform aggregates publicly available campus news, academic updates, student activities, and media coverage from a third-party perspective, serving STU students, faculty, alumni, and the general public.

## Features

According to the community, the platform offers full bilingual (Chinese/English) support, light and dark theme options, category browsing, and date-archived content. The site is built on the Astro framework, fully open source, and hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

## How to Contribute

The platform accepts community submissions and suggestions:

- Email: [news@ShanTou.University](mailto:news@ShanTou.University)
- Open an Issue or Pull Request on [GitHub](https://github.com/ShanTouUniversity/news)

## Disclaimer

STU News is an unofficial, non-profit information aggregation platform and is not affiliated with Shantou University. All content is compiled from publicly available sources and does not represent the official position of the university. For official information, please visit the [STU Official Site (stu.edu.cn)](https://www.stu.edu.cn/).
