The Admissions and Employment Office is recruiting several **2026 Admissions Consultation Assistants** from current undergraduate students to support its outreach and inquiry operations during the peak admissions season.

## Qualifications

- Academically strong undergraduate who understands and identifies with STU's mission
- Excellent writing and verbal communication skills
- Proficient with computers and internet tools; fast typist familiar with social media culture
- Courteous, detail-oriented, patient, and responsible
- Previous experience with STU admissions outreach or hotline work is a plus
- Must be available (no other volunteer commitments) from June 15 to July 4

## Work Details

- **Duties**: Answer admissions hotline calls, respond to inquiries on social media platforms (WeChat, QQ, Weibo), assist with on-site and online consultation events, and help receive visiting high school groups
- **Schedule**: June 15 – July 4, rotating shifts (8:30–12:00, 12:00–17:00, 17:00–21:00)
- **Location**: Room 223, Administration Center, Sangpushan Campus
- **Compensation**: University work-study hourly rate

## How to Apply

Complete the resume form (available from the original OA notice) and submit it to o_zsb@stu.edu.cn by **11:59 PM, Sunday, May 24**. Use the subject line: "Name + Admissions Consultation Assistant Application." Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview.

For questions, scan the QR code in the original OA notice to join the inquiry group.
