Following student applications and university review, the Student Affairs Office has announced the proposed recipients of the 2025 Chen Yuhua Care Bursary. Four students have been selected:

| College | Year / Major | Name |
|---------|-------------|------|
| College of Science | 2025, Physics | Huang |
| College of Math & Computer Science | 2024, Computer Science & Technology | Zhang |
| Chang Jiang School of Journalism & Communication | 2023, New Media | Lai |
| Chang Jiang School of Journalism & Communication | 2023, Journalism | Zhong |

Any objections should be submitted in writing to the Student Financial Aid Management Center (Room 223, Administration Center, Sangpushan Campus) by **5:00 PM, Friday, May 22**. Institutional submissions must bear an official seal; individual submissions must be signed with the respondent's real name.
