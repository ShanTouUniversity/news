The Business School and Mingde College have jointly launched the **"Love Letter to My High School"** admissions campaign, inviting current Business School students to write letters to their former teachers and younger schoolmates — sharing their STU learning experience, expressing gratitude, and offering encouragement.

## Details

- **Period**: Now through June 2026
- **Eligibility**: All current Business School students
- **Content**: Write a heartfelt letter (no word limit) about your STU journey, gratitude toward your high school, and words of encouragement. The college will mail the letters along with STU admissions materials and student-designed branded gifts (including insulated tumblers, tote bags, and umbrellas).

## How to Join

The college will provide commemorative stationery. Interested students should scan the QR code in the original OA notice to join the activity group by **11:59 PM, May 22**. Stationery pickup and mailing address registration will be arranged in the group.

Please include the high school name, recipient name, and phone number in your letter to ensure successful delivery.
