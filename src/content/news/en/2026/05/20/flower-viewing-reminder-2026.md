Newly planted lotus flowers and Kalsang flowers near the Bell of Truth at the Sangpoushan Campus have begun blooming, adding a touch of vibrancy to the campus landscape. The Resource Management Office, however, has issued a gentle reminder for visitors to exercise caution and observe proper etiquette while enjoying the scenery.

According to the office, the ground around the lotus pond is slippery and lacks guardrails. Visitors are advised to keep a safe distance from the pond edge and avoid running or playing nearby. Parents bringing children should maintain close supervision. The office also asks visitors not to trample flower beds or pick flowers, and to take any trash with them to help keep the campus clean.
