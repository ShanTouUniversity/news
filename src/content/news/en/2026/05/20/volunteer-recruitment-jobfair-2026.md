The "Employment for the People" 2026 Shantou State-Owned Enterprises Job Fair will be held on **May 29, 14:30-17:30** at the Sangpoushan Campus Library rooms 30A, 30B, and 30C. The Admissions and Employment Office is currently recruiting **30 student volunteers** campus-wide to assist with the event.

## Volunteer Arrangements

Selected volunteers are required to attend a training session on May 27 (16:00-17:30, Building E Room 401) before the fair. On the day of the event, volunteers will be responsible for greeting and guiding enterprise recruiters, assisting with on-site coordination, and collecting feedback.

Volunteers will receive "i Volunteer" service hour certificates and "Dao Meng Kong Jian" credits, with outstanding performers earning an Outstanding Volunteer Certificate.

## Registration

Interested students can register via the online form before **May 25, 12:00 PM**: <https://v.wjx.cn/vm/m26f8Ff.aspx>

Selection results will be announced via email and the consultation group.
