Results for Shantou University's 2026-2027 Western Region Plan and "Hundred-Thousand-Ten Thousand Project" volunteer service selection have been released. A total of **60 students** have passed the university project office's qualification review and are now in the public comment period, which runs through May 26.

## Selection Process

The selection was conducted through student self-registration followed by university project office review. After the public comment period concludes, the university will match top-scoring candidates based on provincial quotas, determine the list of candidates for medical examinations, establish a standby pool, and submit the final results to the provincial project office for approval.

Any organizations or individuals with objections to the results may submit written feedback to the University Youth League Committee by 17:30 on May 29.
