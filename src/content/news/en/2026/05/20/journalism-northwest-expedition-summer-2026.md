The Chang Jiang School of Journalism and Communication is launching a **summer field research project** retracing the route of renowned journalist Fan Changjiang's 1935 northwest expedition. The project will focus on historical transformation, rural revitalization, ecological governance, and cultural heritage in the region through in-depth research and multimedia reporting.

## Recruitment

- **Eligibility**: Current undergraduate and graduate students of the school (all years; upperclassmen preferred)
- **Positions**: ~10 students
- **Timing**: Expected departure in mid-July, lasting approximately one week, primarily in northern Sichuan

## Qualifications

- Passion for journalism with skills in writing, photography/videography, or new media creation
- Physical fitness for long-distance travel, outdoor hiking, and high-altitude or desert conditions
- Team-oriented, committed to full participation including pre-trip training, field research, and post-trip production
- Field research experience, drone piloting skills, or dialect communication ability is a plus

## How to Apply

- **Email**: dingweimin@stu.edu.cn (Subject: "重走西北角 + Year + Major + Name")
- **Materials**: 1-2 portfolio pieces (text/photo/video) + a brief self-recommendation letter (under 300 words)
- **Deadline**: **9:00 PM, Sunday, June 7, 2026**
- **Selection**: Portfolio review → Interview → Final selection
