To support family culture among faculty and staff and care for the healthy growth of their children, the STU Labor Union, together with the university kindergarten and affiliated school, will host a series of **Children's Day family activities on Friday, May 29**. All age-eligible children of faculty and staff are welcome, including those not enrolled at STU's kindergarten or affiliated school.

## Kindergarten "June 1st Carnival: Little Hands, Big Love"

The affiliated kindergarten will host a charity sale, parent-child interactive activities, creative experiences, and festive food — guiding young children to learn through play and grow through experience while developing compassion, responsibility, and teamwork.

- **Time**: Friday, May 29, 8:45 - 10:40 AM
- **Location**: Kindergarten North Campus, Building 789
- **Participants**: Preschool-age children of STU faculty and staff, with their parents
- **Registration**: Faculty whose preschool children do not attend STU kindergarten should scan the QR code on the original OA notice to register by **12:00 PM, May 23**
- **Inquiry**: 86502088

## Affiliated School Children's Day Fun Fair

The affiliated school will host a Children's Day fun fair across its classrooms, open to children aged 6-14 of faculty and staff.

- **Time**: Friday, May 29, 2:30 - 4:15 PM
- **Location**: Classrooms
- **Participants**: Children aged 6-14 of faculty and staff
- **Registration**: Faculty whose children do not attend STU's affiliated school should scan the QR code on the original OA notice to register by **12:00 PM, May 23**
- **Inquiry**: 86502262 (Director Wang or Teacher Chen)

> Source: STU OA Notice (Labor Union)
