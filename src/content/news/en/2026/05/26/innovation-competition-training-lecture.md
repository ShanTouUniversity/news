## Overview

The Law School, Gezhi College, and the "Chaowan Fazhi" Counselor Studio are jointly hosting a training lecture and project exchange for the China International College Student Innovation Competition.

**Time**: May 27 (Wednesday), 2:30 PM

**Venue**: Room 338, Building E, Donghai'an Campus

## Guest Speaker

**Luo Lie**, lecturer at the STU School of Marxism, China Youth Entrepreneurship Mentor, and Guangdong Outstanding Innovation Mentor. He has led 10+ municipal-level research projects and guided student teams to 30+ provincial-level competition awards.

## Content

- Detailed analysis of competition rules, judging criteria, and key focus areas
- Award-winning case studies for project inspiration
- Interactive project exchange — bring your project proposal for personalized feedback

## Participation

Scan the QR code to join the group before May 26 at midnight. Register via the "Dream Space" app for credit hours.
