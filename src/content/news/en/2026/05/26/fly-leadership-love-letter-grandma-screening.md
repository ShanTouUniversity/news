## Event Theme

**Carry Forward Qiaopi Culture, Forge Youth Responsibility**

## Time and Venue

- **Time**: June 6, 2026 (Saturday), 3:30 PM
- **Venue**: Shantou Wanda Cinema
- **Audience**: Student leaders from both university and college levels

## Film Introduction

*Love Letter to Grandma* tells the story of a Chaoshan grandmother, Ye Shurou, and a family secret that spans more than half a century. As the grandson Xiaowei investigates, a long-hidden truth gradually emerges. Centered on qiaopi culture, the film connects family affection, national concern, and integrity, highlighting profound local sentiment and human warmth.

## Registration

Student organizations at both university and college levels should collect the names of interested students (see the attachment) and have the organization leader send the list to Youth League Committee staff Liang Huoyu at `22hyliang1@stu.edu.cn` by **12:00 PM on June 2**. Relevant students should also scan the code to join the group.

## Notes

1. Participants should arrive at the designated cinema hall entrance by **3:20 PM on June 6** for check-in and unified entry.
2. Please keep mobile phones on silent or turned off and observe cinema etiquette.
3. Once registered, attendance is expected unless there is a special circumstance; in that case, please request leave in advance.
