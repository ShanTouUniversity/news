## Open Positions

The STU Youth League Committee is recruiting instructors for two College Practice (Labor) courses:

- **Sunrise Volunteer Teaching Service Learning**: 1 position, September–December 2026, Wednesdays, Sangpushan Campus
- **Green Sprout Environmental Outreach**: 1 position, September–December 2026, Wednesdays, East Coast Campus

## Requirements

1. Full-time faculty members currently on duty across the university
2. Passion for public service and labor education; alignment with the university’s volunteer-education philosophy; ability to participate throughout both theoretical teaching and off-campus practical guidance
3. Strong communication and emergency response skills; applicants for the Sunrise Volunteer Teaching Service Learning course should also have enough patience and empathy to work in vision-support service teaching scenarios
4. Preference will be given to candidates with experience in labor education courses, volunteer team leadership, special education service, or rural teaching support
5. Must follow university and Youth League Committee course arrangements and comply with teaching management regulations

## How to Apply

- **Deadline**: 12:00 PM, May 27, 2026 (Wednesday)
- **Materials**: Complete the "Application Form for Full-time Instructors of Service-Learning Labor Courses" (Appendix 1) and choose your preferred course
- **Submission**: Send the PDF version to `siyichen@stu.edu.cn`
- **Email subject**: Labor Course Instructor Application - Name - Department

## Additional Notes

- Candidates who pass the initial screening will be notified of the interview in the group chat; interviews will be held on **June 3 (Wednesday)**
- The final assigned position may be adjusted based on actual needs
- For questions, please contact Chen at the Youth League Committee, Tel: 0754-86502255
