## Overview

From May 11 to 20, 2026, PhD students **Qiao P.** and **Liu H.** from Prof. Wang Zhongbo's team at the College of Science participated in the MEET2026 joint scientific cruise. The expedition was co-organized by Tongji University's State Key Laboratory of Marine Geology and Xiamen University's State Key Laboratory of Marine Biogeochemistry.

## Cruise Details

Carried out aboard Tongji University's research vessel "Tongji," the cruise reached a maximum depth of 5,100 meters. The team successfully collected surface and columnar geological samples along with in-situ ocean circulation, biological, and chemical process data. The findings will support research on cross-sphere material exchange, biogeochemical cycles, and national seafloor observatory projects.
