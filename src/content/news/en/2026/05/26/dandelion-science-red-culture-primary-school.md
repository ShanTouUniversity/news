## Overview

The College of Science and Zhicheng College are hosting the 70th edition of "Dandelion Science," themed **"Passing the Torch, Red Fun Together,"** at the STU Affiliated Primary School to promote red culture education through interactive activities.

**Time**: May 27 (Wednesday) afternoon

**Venue**: STU Affiliated Primary School

## Activities

**Red Culture Booths** – Creative booths displaying Chaoshan red stories, revolutionary figures, and cultural products, with quizzes, stamp collections, and fun games.

**"Red Journey" Classroom Monopoly** – A custom Chaoshan-themed game board with revolutionary history checkpoints. Students participate in teams, rolling dice, advancing on the board, and completing challenges through quizzes, role-play, and sharing reflections.
