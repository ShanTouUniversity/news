## About the System

The Knowledge World Think Tank System integrates high-quality educational resources from around the world, bringing together academic forums, courses from top universities, industry research reports, and real-world industry practice cases. With AI-powered tools for in-depth content analysis, it provides a smart learning environment connecting general education and specialized study, theory and practice, for faculty, students, and researchers.

## Highlights

- **Academic forums**: 6,000+ lecture recordings, featuring Nobel laureates, CAS academicians, and executives from Fortune 500 companies
- **Professional courses**: 10,000+ courses from MIT, Stanford, Harvard, and other top universities across engineering, computer science, finance, medicine, humanities, and more
- **Research reports**: 4,000+ reports from McKinsey, Goldman Sachs, and other leading institutions covering macroeconomics, technology trends, and corporate case studies
- **Industry practice**: 40,000+ videos across 452 majors, showing real industry transformation pathways
