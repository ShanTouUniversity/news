## Overview

On May 23, the Jiangsu Alumni Association held its 2nd Council Meeting and alumni exchange in Zhenjiang as part of STU's 45th anniversary celebrations. Themed "Uniting Alumni Strength, Painting a Blueprint for Development," the event was attended by Director Huang Yixiong of the University Party and Administrative Office along with council members.

## New Appointments

The council approved:
- **Xie Linghai** (2000, Industrial Catalysis) and **Wang Jiang'an** (1994, Foreign Trade) as vice presidents
- **Yang Yang** (2000, Business Management) as executive vice president

President Kuang Shu announced plans to add two more vice presidents to strengthen the management structure.

## Alumni Exchange

Director Huang Yixiong emphasized the university's commitment to strengthening connections with alumni networks and leveraging alumni resources for institutional development. Attendees shared insights on industry-academia collaboration and alumni platform building.
