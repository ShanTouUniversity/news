The College of Science and Zhicheng College are hosting the 53rd "Teacher-Student Symposium" (Academician in College special session), featuring Executive President and Academician Wang Quan in face-to-face dialogue with 2026 graduates.

**Theme**: Charting the Future: A Dialogue on Growth, Planning, and Advice

**Time**: May 29 (Friday), 3:00–4:00 PM

**Venue**: Room 130, Zhicheng College, Sangpu Campus

**Audience**: 2026 graduates of the College of Science and Zhicheng College
