## Overview

On May 17, the Chemistry College and Zhixing College held a lab safety knowledge competition themed "Promoting Quality Through Competition, Safeguarding with Integrity." Sixteen teams participated.

## Format

The competition featured three rounds: preliminary (quiz), semi-final (rapid response), and final (hands-on scenarios). The final round tested teams on hazardous chemical spill response, injury treatment, and accident simulation.

Results: 1 first prize, 2 second prizes, and 3 third prizes.
