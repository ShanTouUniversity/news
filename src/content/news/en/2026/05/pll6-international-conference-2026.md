The **6th International Conference on the Psychology of Language Learning (PLL6)** is being held at Shantou University from **May 20 to 24, 2026**. The conference brings together scholars and educators from around the world for keynote speeches, symposia, workshops, paper presentations, and poster sessions.

## Conference Theme

**Positive Vis-à-Vis Negative and Beyond**

## Conference Scope

The program features **7 keynote speeches** and **over 200** presentations, posters, workshops, and symposia covering:

- Psychology of language learners and teachers (cognition, emotion, motivation, personality, behavior, and well-being)
- Positive language education
- Dynamic interaction between individuals and contexts
- Building positive institutions for language education
- Technology-enhanced language teaching and its psychological impact
- Innovative research methods in teacher and learner psychology
- Bridging theory, research, and teaching practice
