## Event Details

- **Theme**: Fun "Lithium" Beads, Chemistry Meets Art
- **Time**: May 23, 2026 (Saturday), 3:00 PM
- **Venue**: Multi-function Hall, 2nd Floor, Zhixing Academy
- **Capacity**: 30 students from the Chemistry School and Zhixing Academy

## Activities

**Perler bead science mini-lesson**: Student Li J. from the 2024 Applied Chemistry cohort will explain the thermoplastic principles behind Perler bead materials, basic techniques, and color coordination tips.

**Dopamine creative session**: Participants choose their favorite bead colors and pattern templates to create their own Perler bead art in a relaxed atmosphere.

**Showcase and keepsake**: Finished works can be displayed and shared on-site, and all creations are yours to keep.

## Registration

Interested students should scan the event QR code and join the activity group by **May 22** to confirm registration.

## Notes

- Participants can earn second-class credit and aesthetic education module points by registering through the "DaoMeng KongJian" (到梦空间) app
- Registered participants should attend the full event. If unable to attend, please email 23yhwang3@stu.edu.cn at least 1 day in advance
