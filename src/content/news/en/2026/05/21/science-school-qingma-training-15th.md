## Overview

From April 11 to May 23, 2026, the STU School of Science Young Marxist Training Project (Qingma Project) conducted its 15th Party Activist Training Program. The training used diverse formats including specialized lectures, film discussions, group deliberations, and organizational life meetings to help participants strengthen their ideological foundation and sense of responsibility.

## Key Training Sessions

**Opening Ceremony** (April 11): Held at the Library Lecture Hall, with Lin Jianqing, Deputy Director of the University Party Committee Organization Department, encouraging participants to uphold their ideals and actively align with the Party organization.

**Lecture 1** (April 11): Teacher Liu Yingbo presented on "National Security, Youth Stepping Up," providing an in-depth interpretation of the comprehensive national security concept and helping participants understand contemporary security challenges.

**Lecture 2** (April 26): Focused on understanding the "Two Sessions" spirit, exploring the relationship between new quality productive forces and Chinese-style modernization, and encouraging students to integrate personal growth with national strategy.

**Lecture 3 & Group Discussion** (May 10 AM): Teacher Chen Zegong covered fundamental Party and Youth League knowledge, history, and traditions. Groups then discussed "Responsibility of Young Activists" in relation to national security, technological innovation, and rural revitalization.

**Organizational Life Meeting** (May 10 PM): Participants delivered individual ideological reports to their respective Party branches, reflecting on their training experience and motivation for joining the Party.
