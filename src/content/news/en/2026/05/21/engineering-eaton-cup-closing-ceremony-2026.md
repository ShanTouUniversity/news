## Event Details

- **Time**: May 26, 2026 (Monday), 2:00–3:45 PM
- **Venue**: Library Lecture Hall, Sangpushan Campus
- **Host**: College of Engineering, Jingyi Academy

## Program

1. 2026 "Eaton Cup" Science & Innovation Festival video highlights
2. Announcement of winning teams
3. Awards ceremony
4. Leadership remarks
5. 8th "Scientific Spirit and Practice" Lecture & 23rd "Research Navigation" Session

## Registration

- **Deadline**: May 25 (Sunday), 12:00 PM
- **How to register**: Scan the QR code to complete the survey; accepted participants will be notified via class groups
- Please arrive 20 minutes early for check-in. Attendees will receive comprehensive assessment points for intellectual activities.
