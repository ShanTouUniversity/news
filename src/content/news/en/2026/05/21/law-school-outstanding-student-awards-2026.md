## Four Awards Open for Application

The Law School has announced the opening of four award categories for the 2025-2026 academic year: Outstanding Student Leader, Outstanding Communist Youth League Member, Outstanding Publicist, and Outstanding Youth Volunteer. Nominations are voluntary, combining self-nomination and organizational recommendation on the principles of fairness and transparency.

## Eligibility

Student leaders in the Law School Student Union (including Graduate Student Union), Youth League branches, Youth Volunteer Association, class committees, media center members, youth volunteers, publicity workers, and Communist Youth League members.

## Application Timeline

- **Individual application deadline**: May 24 (Sunday), 11:59 PM — apply through your organization or class
  - Outstanding Publicist: send application to 24yxluo@stu.edu.cn with subject "优秀宣传员报名表+Name+Major/Year"
  - Outstanding Volunteer: send application to 24qffan@stu.edu.cn with subject "优秀志愿者报名表+Name+Major/Year"
- **Organization submission deadline**: May 31 (Sunday), 11:59 PM — send compiled materials to o_fxytw@stu.edu.cn

## Selection Process

Individual submission → Organization-level preliminary selection → College review → Public announcement of results.
