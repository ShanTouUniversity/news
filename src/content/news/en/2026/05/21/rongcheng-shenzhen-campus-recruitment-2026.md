It is reported that on May 21, Rongcheng Certified Public Accountants (Special General Partnership) Shenzhen branch held a campus recruitment session in Room E119, Donghaian Campus. The event aimed to deepen university-enterprise cooperation, broaden employment channels for graduates, and help students understand industry trends and clarify their career plans.

## Industry Status and Talent Development

Ou Changxian, a partner at Rongcheng's Shenzhen branch, opened the session with an overview of the accounting industry. He noted that as AI deeply empowers the accounting sector, market demand for composite accounting talent continues to rise, encouraging students to build upon solid professional foundations and keep pace with the industry's digital transformation.

Li Cheng, an HR specialist, then detailed Rongcheng's talent development policy. Through a mentor system and hands-on project training, Rongcheng helps fresh graduates quickly adapt to business workflows and transition into professional roles.

## Job-Seeking Experience Sharing

Chen HS, a current employee and STU alumnus, shared his growth journey. Since joining, he has traveled to over a dozen cities with his team, treating each project as an opportunity for growth. He has maintained a strong passion for learning, continually strengthening his professional knowledge and expanding his career horizons.

## Interactive Q&A

Following the presentations, students actively asked questions about certification exam preparation, role fit, and internship-to-full-time conversion. The three guests answered each question in detail, helping students further clarify their job-seeking strategies and career planning.
