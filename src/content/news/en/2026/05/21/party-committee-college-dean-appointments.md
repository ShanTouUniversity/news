## Seven Deans Appointed

According to a document issued by the STU Party Committee (Document No. 35 [2026]), the Standing Committee of the Party Committee at its 10th meeting of 2026 has approved the following concurrent appointments:

| Name | Position |
|------|----------|
| Yang Qingjie | Dean of Siyuan College |
| Wang Hui | Dean of Zhicheng College |
| Deng Jianguang | Dean of Gezhi College |
| Liang Qiang | Dean of Mingde College |
| Xia Oudong | Dean of Xiuyuan College |
| Huang Xiaochun | Dean of Zhixing College |
| Tan Zhong | Dean of Shanhai College |

The document was issued on April 23, 2026.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Party Committee)
