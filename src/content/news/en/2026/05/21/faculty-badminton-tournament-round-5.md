## Round 5 Results

On May 20, the fifth round of the 2026 STU Faculty Badminton Team Tournament took place, wrapping up the group stage. Results:

**Group A**
- Administration Team 2: 4–1 Arts & Public Affairs Joint Team
- Medical School: 3–2 Engineering College

**Group B**
- Math & CS: 2–3 Law & Business Joint Team
- Science School: 0–5 Chemistry School

## Final Group Standings

| Rank | Group A | Group B |
|------|---------|---------|
| 1st | Medical School | Administration Team 1 |
| 2nd | Sports/Arts/New Media Joint Team | Chemistry School |
| 3rd | Engineering College | Law & Business Joint Team |
| 4th | Administration Team 2 | Science School |
| 5th | Arts & Public Affairs Joint Team | Math & CS |

## Semifinals Preview

- **Time**: May 22 (Friday), 4:50 PM
- **Venue**: Sports Park Badminton Courts, Sangpushan Campus
- Medical School vs. Chemistry School
- Administration Team 1 vs. Sports/Arts/New Media Joint Team
