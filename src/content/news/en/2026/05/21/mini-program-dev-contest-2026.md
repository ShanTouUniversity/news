## Two Development Tracks

The School of Mathematics and Computer Science and Shanhai Academy are co-hosting a mini program development contest aimed at promoting practical innovation and advancing the school's "ZhiXin" initiative. Participants may choose one of two tracks:

**Volunteer Service Mini Program** — Build a smart volunteer talent database enabling precise matching between volunteer activities and personnel, streamlining campus volunteer event management.

**Zhanglin Ancient Port Cultural Tourism Mini Program** — Integrate local cultural and tourism resources including red-head ship heritage, overseas Chinese history, and historic architecture, offering smart guided tours, cultural education, and local specialty recommendations in a digital format.

## Participation Details

- **Eligibility**: All students from the School of Mathematics and Computer Science and Shanhai Academy
- **Team size**: Individuals or teams of up to 4 members
- **Registration deadline**: May 24 (Sunday) — scan the QR code to join the consultation group and complete the online form
- **Submission deadline**: June 5 (Thursday) — submit a demo video (under 5 minutes) and a 150-word feature summary to 25mhliu@stu.edu.cn
