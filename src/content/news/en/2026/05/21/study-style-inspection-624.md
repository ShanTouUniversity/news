## Week 9 Inspection Results Released

According to a notice from the Student Affairs Office, the Sangpushan Campus study discipline inspection team conducted two routine checks at Building E during Week 9 of the 2025-2026 spring semester, on April 28 (Tuesday) and April 30 (Thursday) at 2:00 PM.

The inspection found **no violations of university regulations**. The Student Affairs Office encouraged all colleges to continue their efforts in promoting good study habits.
