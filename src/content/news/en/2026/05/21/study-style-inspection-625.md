## Week 10 Inspection Results Released

According to a notice from the Student Affairs Office, the East Coast Campus study discipline inspection team conducted two routine checks at Building E during Week 10 of the 2025-2026 spring semester, on May 7 (Thursday) at 10:00 AM and May 9 (Saturday) at 2:00 PM.

The inspection found **no violations of university regulations**. The Student Affairs Office encouraged all colleges to continue their efforts in promoting good study habits.
