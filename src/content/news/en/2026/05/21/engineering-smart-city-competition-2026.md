## Competition Details

- **Time**: May 22, 2026 (Friday), 3:00 PM
- **Venue**: Room G303, Sangpushan Campus
- **Host**: College of Engineering
- **Organizer**: College of Engineering Graduate Student Association

## Competition Content

Teams design proposals around smart city topics. Each team may have 1–4 members. Cross-disciplinary collaboration is encouraged.

## Awards

| Award | Slots | Prize (per team) |
|-------|-------|------------------|
| First Prize | 1 | ¥1,000 |
| Second Prize | 1 | ¥800 |
| Third Prize | 2 | ¥500 |

## Rules

- Plagiarism and falsification are strictly prohibited; violators will be disqualified and prizes revoked
- Different teams may choose the same topic direction but must not submit identical content
- Participants must compete with integrity; malicious conduct toward other teams is prohibited
