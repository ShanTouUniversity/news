## Event Details

- **Time**: May 22, 2026 (Friday), 7:30 PM
- **Venue**: Multi-function Hall, Zhixing Academy
- **Audience**: All enrolled students

## Theme

This session focuses on civil service exam preparation and position selection guidance, helping students understand recruitment policies, exam strategies, and subject-specific application tips.

## Guest Speaker

Liang Zirui, a senior civil service exam instructor at Huatu Education, specializes in Guangdong provincial exam research. He is well-versed in recruitment policies, exam trends, and preparation systems, with expertise in position selection guidance and exam planning.

## Content

1. **Comprehensive exam analysis**: Recruitment timelines, eligibility requirements, historical competition ratios, and policy changes
2. **Written exam guidance**: Breaking down core topics using past exam questions and outlining answering techniques
3. **Position selection coaching**: Available position types by major, application advantages, restrictions, and smart selection strategies
4. **Personalized study plans**: Tailored preparation roadmaps for different skill levels, with live Q&A

Scan the event QR code to join the group. Materials and exam tips will be shared in the group. Please arrive 10 minutes early.
