## Overview

The Journalism School Party Committee and Xingchuan Academy have announced sessions 26–31 of the "Party Members in College, Original Aspiration Accompanies Growth" series. The program leverages faculty party members' exemplary roles in holistic education and explores a new model of "party building guiding residential college education."
