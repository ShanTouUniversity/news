## Online Evaluation Concludes, Finals Shortlist Published

The online evaluation phase of the 8th Shantou University Young Teachers Teaching Competition has recently concluded. Following nominations from secondary academic units, qualification reviews, and expert online evaluations, the shortlist for the on-site finals was formally published on May 21, 2026, entering a four-day public comment period running through May 24.

## How to Raise Objections

During the public comment period, anyone with objections to the evaluation results may submit written feedback to the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment:

- Phone: 0754-86503165
- Email: o_fdea@stu.edu.cn

Individual submissions must include the person's real name, contact information, and supporting evidence. Organizational submissions must bear an official seal and include contact details and evidence. The university emphasized that all feedback should be factual and objective, and that defamation or false accusations are strictly prohibited.

## Issuing Units

The notice was jointly issued by the Labor Union, Academic Affairs Office, and Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment.
