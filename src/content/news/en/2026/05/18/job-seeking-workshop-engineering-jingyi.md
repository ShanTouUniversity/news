## Overview

On May 18, the Engineering College and Jingyi College co-hosted a career workshop themed "Crack the Campus Recruitment, Empower Your Career," featuring HR veteran **Xu Jialin** with 12 years of experience.

## Highlights

Xu analyzed campus recruitment logic from an HR perspective, covering resume matching, data quantification, the STAR method, and interview strategies. One-on-one resume revision sessions provided personalized optimization for students from different majors.
