Professor Tang Shangfeng from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) will deliver an academic talk titled "Quasi-Experimental Study on the Integration of Chronic Disease Prevention and Treatment" at the invitation of STU's School of Public Health. The talk is scheduled for **May 21, 10:00-11:40 AM** in Room 213 of the Wenbei Building.

## About the Speaker

Prof. Tang is a National Key R&D Young Scientist, Hubei Chutian Scholar, and Global Top 2‰ Scientist. His research spans nursing science and social medicine & health management. He has led multiple national key R&D projects and published over 30 JCR Q1 papers as first or corresponding author. He has also authored 30 policy reports adopted by central and provincial-level agencies.

## Attendance

This talk is part of the School of Public Health Academic Lecture Series (NO.2026-03). It is primarily intended for graduate students of the school, though interested faculty members are welcome to attend.
