## Overview

Xingchuan College is launching a dormitory evaluation competition themed "Meet Civilization, Tell Beauty," assessing dorms on five dimensions: ideology, study atmosphere, property care, cleanliness, and safety.

**Time**: June 1, 10:00 AM

## Process

Self-nominated dorms should submit applications to 23ytlai1@stu.edu.cn by **May 31, 8:00 PM**. The review panel will combine self-nominations with random inspections.

Winning dorms receive comprehensive evaluation加分 and recognition.
