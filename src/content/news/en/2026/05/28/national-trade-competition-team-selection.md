## Overview

The National Business Elite Challenge International Trade Competition (Foreign-Related Legal Services Track), hosted by the CCPIT Commercial Committee, is an A-level national competition. The Law School is now selecting teams.

## Requirements

- Open to all undergraduates and postgraduates (separate teams)
- **3–5 members** per team with 1–2 advisors
- Must be available for full preparation from August to October

## Tracks

**Legal Due Diligence**: Simulate a legal service firm conducting due diligence on cross-border transactions.

**AI+Law Research**: Investigate AI application scenarios or LLM development enterprises.

## Evaluation

Written report (70%) + oral presentation with 10-min video (30%)

## Registration

Submit registration form by Aug 1, 24:00, and report draft + PPT by Aug 15, 24:00, to zhywen@stu.edu.cn.
