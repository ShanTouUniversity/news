## Overview

The Business School and Mingde College organized the "Ink Fragrance Nourishes Integrity, Real Performance Inspires Action" themed calligraphy competition, which received enthusiastic participation and many outstanding works.

## Winners

- **First Prize** (1): *Ode to the Lotus*
- **Second Prize** (3): *Ding Baozhen's Family Motto*, *Pure Breeze and Clean Integrity*, *Advocating Virtue and Honesty*
- **Third Prize** (5): *Ding Baozhen's Family Motto*, *Clear Atmosphere*, *Benefiting the People*, *Upright and Incorruptible*, *Untainted*
