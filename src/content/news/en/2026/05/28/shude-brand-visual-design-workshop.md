## Overview

Shude College is hosting a "Brand Visual and Creative Design" workshop for the "Phoenix Blossom" interdisciplinary project teams, featuring **Prof. Xu Lan** from the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design.

**Time**: June 1 (Monday), 3:00–5:00 PM

**Venue**: Shude College, 2F多功能厅

## Content

- Brand story and positioning
- Logo and visual identity optimization
- Packaging and creative design advice

Teams should bring their existing brand materials for on-site review.
