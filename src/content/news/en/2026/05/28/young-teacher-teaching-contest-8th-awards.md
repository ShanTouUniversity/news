## Overview

The Teacher Development and Education Assessment Center, together with the Trade Union and Academic Affairs Office, held the 8th Young Faculty Teaching Competition. Results have been announced following college nominations, expert review, and on-site finals.

## Awards

Two groups — Group 1 (Liberal Arts & Ideological-Political) and Group 2 (Sciences, Engineering & Medical) — each awarded:

- **Grand Prize**: 1
- **First Prize**: 2
- **Second Prize**: 3
- **Third Prize**: 9

## Feedback

Public feedback period: **May 28 – June 1**. Submit written comments to the Teacher Development Center at 0754-86503165.
