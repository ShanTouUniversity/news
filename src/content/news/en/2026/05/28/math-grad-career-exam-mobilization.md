## Overview

According to the College of Mathematics and Computer Science notice, the Math Department held a mobilization meeting for the class of 2027 graduates to help students understand current employment trends and further study options.

## Event Details

- **Time**: May 29, 2026 (Friday), 4:00 PM
- **Venue**: Room 103, Building E, East Coast Campus
- **Audience**: 2027 graduates of the Math Department (2023 undergraduates)
- **Content**: Career guidance and graduate school planning

All 2027 graduates were required to attend and arrive 15 minutes early.
