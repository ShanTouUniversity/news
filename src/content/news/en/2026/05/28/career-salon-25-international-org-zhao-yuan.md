## Overview

The Law School, Gezhi College, and the International Cooperation Office are hosting the 25th Career Salon, featuring **Associate Professor Zhao Yuan** from the Beijing Foreign Studies University School of International Organizations.

**Time**: June 2 (Tuesday), 4:30 PM

**Venue**: Room 128, Building E, Donghai'an Campus

## Content

The lecture will analyze trends in international organization positions and competency assessment, exploring the shift from general competency training to role-specific pathways and from individual competition to systemic national support.

## About the Speaker

Zhao Yuan is an associate professor at BFSU's School of International Organizations, a Harvard Kennedy School visiting scholar, and a consultant to the UN ECOSOC and ITER. Her course "International Organization Talent Development" has been recognized as a national first-class undergraduate course.
