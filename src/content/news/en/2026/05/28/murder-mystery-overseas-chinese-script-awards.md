## Overview

The Business School and Mingde College organized an original murder mystery script competition themed around overseas Chinese (侨) culture, exploring its contemporary significance and the spirit of integrity and patriotism.

## Winners

- **First Prize**: *Fundraising* (筹饷)
- **Second Prize**: *Shengping 1942* (升平1942)
- **Third Prize**: *The Undelivered Letter - Bloody Silver Bank* (未寄的批-血色银庄), *Oath at the Overseas Chinese Ancestral Hall* (侨祠信约)
