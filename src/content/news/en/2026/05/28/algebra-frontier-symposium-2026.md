## Overview

The College of Mathematics and Computer Science is hosting the 2026 Algebraic Frontiers Symposium from **May 29 to June 1** at the Jinhaiwan Hotel in Shantou. Scholars from Beijing Normal University, Sichuan University, Xiamen University, and other institutions will present academic reports.

Faculty and students are welcome to attend.
