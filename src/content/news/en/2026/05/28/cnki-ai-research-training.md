## Overview

As part of the 23rd Sangpushan Reading Festival, the library is hosting an information literacy training session: "Leveraging CNKI AI to Accelerate Your Research Journey."

**Time**: May 29 (Friday), 3:00–4:00 PM

**Venue**: Room 102, Building E, Donghai'an Campus

**Speaker**: Yang Xueying (CNKI Education Knowledge Service Expert)

## Content

- CNKI resources and functions overview
- Efficient search techniques for research topic selection
- AI tools empowering the full research workflow

The session will be recorded in the "Dream Space" second classroom system. All faculty and students are welcome.
