## Overview

The "Tell the STU Story" creative contest has added an **image-text track** alongside the original short video track. Participants can post native image gallery content on Douyin or Xiaohongshu with the hashtag **#把汕大讲给你听**.

## Rules

Both tracks are **judged independently** with separate prize pools (including DJI devices, Huawei tablets).

- **Douyin**: Score = views×50% + likes×35% + shares×15%
- **Xiaohongshu**: Score = views×50% + likes×25% + saves×25%

## Note

Participants may enter both tracks but **cannot reuse the same content** across both formats.

Timeline: May 20 – June 30; submission window: June 28–30.
