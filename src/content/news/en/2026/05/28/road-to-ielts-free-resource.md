## Resource Overview

The Centre for Independent Language Learning (CILL) has licensed the Road to IELTS online preparation platform for all STU students and faculty, making it available completely free of charge.

## Platform Features

- **300+** interactive activities
- **17** expert insight videos
- **40** IELTS mock tests

Available for independent study on both Sangpu and East Coast campuses.

## Registration

Visit the CILL introduction page to set up your account: https://my.stu.edu.cn/courses/elc/mod/page/view.php?id=261036
