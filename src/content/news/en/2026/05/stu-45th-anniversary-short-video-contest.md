To celebrate Shantou University's 45th anniversary, the Admissions and Employment Office, together with Shantou Luoyin Design Co., Ltd., is launching the **"Tell STU's Story"** short video creation contest, open to all current students, faculty, and alumni.

## Overview

- **Theme**: Tell STU's Story (Official hashtag: #把汕大讲给你听)
- **Eligibility**: All current STU students, faculty, and alumni
- **Period**: May 20 – June 30, 2026
- **Platforms**: WeChat Video Channel, Douyin, and Xiaohongshu (judged independently per platform)

## How to Participate

Create videos showcasing campus scenery, student life, faculty stories, alumni memories, or personal growth reflections. Post with the official hashtag #把汕大讲给你听 on any or all platforms. Each platform has its own awards; cross-platform submissions count only for the highest result.

## Awards (Per Platform)

| Prize | Winners | Reward |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Grand Prize | 1 | DJI Pocket 4 Creator Combo |
| First Prize | 2 | DJI Action 5 Pro |
| Second Prize | 2 | Huawei MatePad 11.5 |
| Third Prize | 5 | Edifier W800BT Free Noise-Cancelling Headphones |
| Excellence Award | 20 | STU Admissions Creative Gift Set |

Outstanding creators may also be appointed as official STU Admissions Ambassadors.

## Submission

Submit via the "STU Admissions" (汕大招生) WeChat official account between **June 28, 00:00 and June 30, 23:59**. Navigate to "Material Submission" in the bottom menu to upload your video link and data screenshots.

## Bonus: Share & Win

Forward the "STU Admissions" WeChat post "Tell STU's Story | A Love Letter to STU" to your Moments and collect likes. Submit a screenshot to the WeChat account by **12:00 PM, May 21** — the top 20 will receive an STU customized gift pack.
