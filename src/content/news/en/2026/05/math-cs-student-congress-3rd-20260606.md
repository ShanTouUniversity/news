Approved by the college Party committee, the 3rd Student Congress of the College of Mathematics and Computer Science will convene on **June 6, 2026** in Room 130, Building E, East Coast Campus.

## Agenda

- Review the work report of the 2nd Student Union Committee
- Elect the 3rd Student Union Committee
- Elect the 3rd Student Union Presidium members
- Collect student feedback and proposals on university affairs
- Decide on other major matters

## Delegates

The congress will have **96 official delegates**, representing approximately 4.3% of the college's total student body. Delegates must be full-time undergraduate students of the college, demonstrating strong civic values, responsibility, and the ability to represent their peers. They are nominated by class Youth League branches through democratic consultation.
