## Position

Director of the Ocean Institute, School of Science, Shantou University (1 position).

## Qualifications

- Firm political stance, strong sense of the big picture, team-oriented, and willing to take initiative
- Doctoral degree in a relevant discipline with at least 2 years of service at the university
- Full professor or above with some management experience
- Born after July 1, 1972, in good health, able to serve 2 full terms
- Satisfactory or above annual performance evaluations for the past 3 years

## Scope

Eligible on-campus personnel within the marine and life sciences disciplines of the School of Science.

## Selection Method

Democratic recommendation, organizational review, and decision by the Party Committee.
