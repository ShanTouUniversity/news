## Provincial Recognition

The Guangdong Federation of Trade Unions has announced its latest list of supported Model Worker and Craftsman Innovation Studios, with 70 studios selected province-wide. The "Zheng Huaiping Model Worker and Craftsman Innovation Studio," led by Professor Zheng Huaiping of the STU School of Science, has been successfully selected and will receive dedicated provincial funding.

## Studio Achievements

Professor Zheng's studio has long demonstrated outstanding results in scientific research, talent development, and public service. This selection represents a strong recognition of STU's model worker innovation efforts and will further enhance the studio's impact in technological innovation, technology transfer, and exemplary leadership.
