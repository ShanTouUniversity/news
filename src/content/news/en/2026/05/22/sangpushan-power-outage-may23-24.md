## Outage Schedule

Due to the construction of a new teaching and laboratory building, two 10KV high-voltage power lines between Switch Station No. 2 and No. 1 will be relocated. Power loads will be switched in two phases:

| Phase | Time | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 1st | May 23 (Saturday), **12:00 PM** (adjusted from 2:00 PM) | Load transferred to backup high-voltage line |
| 2nd | May 24 (Sunday), **1:00 PM** (adjusted from 2:00 PM) | Load switched back to main high-voltage line |

Each switch will cause a **5 to 15-minute power outage** in affected areas.

## Affected Areas

All 16 transformers under Switch Station No. 1 are affected, including:

- **Student dormitories**: Buildings A–F, L, Y, P
- **Dining & shops**: 2nd and 3rd canteens, commercial area
- **Teaching areas**: Teaching Buildings C, D, G; Science Building; Engineering Building; Liberal Arts Building; Technology Building
- **Colleges & arts**: Shude College, Art College
- **Administration**: Administration Center, East/West Administration Buildings
- **Faculty housing**: High-rise faculty apartments, faculty dormitories A1–A14, B1–B16
- **Others**: Network Center, Auditorium, Old Gymnasium, Campus Hospital, Kindergarten, Affiliated Middle/Primary Schools
