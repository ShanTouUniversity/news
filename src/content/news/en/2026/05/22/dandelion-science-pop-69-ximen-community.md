## Event Details

The 69th installment of the Dandelion Science Popularity program, organized by the Science College and Zhicheng College, will visit Ximen Community on May 23, 2026, for an immersive science education event on food nutrition and safety.

- **Theme**: Know Your Food, Eat with Safety
- **Time**: May 23, 2026 (Saturday), 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Audience**: Ximen Community residents
- **Organizer**: Science College Youth League Committee
- **Co-organizer**: Science College Youth Volunteer Association

## Activities

The event integrates eye health, nutritional analysis, food authentication, and healthy diet building into interactive experiences:

### Soak Up Sunshine, Forward for Eyes

A fun spinning wheel game helps community residents, especially young people, learn about eye-protecting foods and the concept of balanced nutrition through dietary supplements.

### Fingerprint Art Plates

Through creative fingerprint painting on plates, parents and children learn to identify healthy ingredients and distinguish unsafe foods, blending nutritional knowledge with hands-on fun.

### Nutrition Flying Chess Challenge

A parent-child board game where children roll dice to advance through challenges, learning to distinguish healthy from unhealthy habits while playing.
