## Consultation

Based on the implementation of three trial management regulations, the Research Office has drafted revised versions and is now seeking feedback from across the university:

1. **"STU Science and Technology Plan Performance Evaluation Management Regulation"** (revised from Document No. 208 [2023])
2. **"STU Research Project Information Internal Disclosure Management Regulation"** (revised from Document No. 76 [2022])
3. **"STU 'Lump Sum' Project Fund Management Regulation"** (revised from Document No. 80 [2022])

## How to Submit Feedback

Please send comments or suggestions by **May 30, 2026** to: o_kyc@stu.edu.cn

> Source: STU OA Notice (Research Office)
> ⚠️ The three revised regulation drafts are available on the OA system.
