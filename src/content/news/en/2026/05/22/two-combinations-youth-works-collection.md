## Overview

The Ministry of Education has launched a nationwide collection of themed works on the "Two Combinations" (integrating Marxism with China's realities and with fine traditional Chinese culture). All STU undergraduate and graduate students may participate individually or in teams.

## Five Creative Categories

| Category | Format | Duration |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Youth Theory Presentation | Lecture video | Under 8 min |
| Red Culture Storytelling | Micro-presentation video | Under 5 min |
| Socialist Core Values Practice | Sitcom / micro-film / documentary | 5–8 min |
| Intangible Cultural Heritage Preservation | Craft demo video / micro-drama | 5–20 min |
| China's Voice & Cultural Exchange | Short video / micro-documentary | 5–7 min |

## Requirements

- Landscape orientation, 1080P+ resolution, MP4 format, with simplified Chinese subtitles throughout
- Student-led creation; AI tools may assist (must be noted)
- Original works only; submitters must hold full copyright

## Submission

- **Deadline**: Before July 1
- **How to submit**: Send works, submission info form, and summary table to o_xssw@stu.edu.cn
- **Email subject**: "College Name + 'Two Combinations' Youth Practice Works"

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office)
> ⚠️ Submission info form and summary table templates are available on the OA system.
