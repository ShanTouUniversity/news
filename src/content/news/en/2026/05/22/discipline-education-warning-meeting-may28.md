## Meeting Notice

The STU Discipline Inspection Commission has scheduled a meeting on studying and practicing the correct approach to performance evaluation, combined with a推进会 on addressing prominent issues in higher education governance.

### Schedule

- **Date & Time**: May 28, 2026 (Thursday), 9:00 AM
- **Venue**: Fourth-floor conference room, Administration Building, Sangpushan Campus

### Attendees

Heads of the Party and Administration Office, Discipline Inspection Office, Human Resources, Finance, Research, Graduate School, Resource Management, Infrastructure, Admissions and Employment, Bidding Center, Continuing Education, and Network and Information Center.

### Agenda

1. Presentation of warning cases
2. Unit reports on governance efforts, digital oversight, and anti-corruption measures (5 min each)
3. Remarks by Discipline Inspection Secretary Feng Xinglei

### Important Notes

- Attendee lists must be submitted by **12:00 PM, May 26**
- Attendance is mandatory; leave requests require written explanation to the Discipline Inspection Office
- Contact: Xu Xiangna, Tel: 86502346, Email: o_jjs@stu.edu.cn
