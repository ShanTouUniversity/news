## Training Concludes

The 15th STU Young Marxist Training Project (Qingma Project) Party Activist Training Program ran from April 11 to May 23, 2026. Approved by the STU Party Committee Party School, **477 participants** met graduation requirements, with **138** named "Outstanding Participants."

## Graduation Ceremony

- **Time**: May 23, 2026 (Saturday), 3:00–5:30 PM
- **Main venue**: Room 105, Building E, East Coast Campus
- **Satellite venue**: Lecture Hall 1, Sangpushan Campus

Temporary League branches will present themed reports during the ceremony.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Party School, STU Youth League Committee, STU League School, STU Student Union)
> ⚠️ The full lists of graduating and outstanding participants are available on the OA system.
