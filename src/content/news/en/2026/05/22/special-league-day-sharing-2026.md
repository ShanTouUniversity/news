## Event Details

- **Time**: May 27, 2026 (Wednesday), 3:00–4:40 PM
- **Venue**: Room 201-2, Building E, Sangpushan Campus
- **Host**: STU Youth League Committee, STU Youth Media Center

## Background

This semester's Special League Day projects focused on themes including the "Hundred-Thousand-Ten Thousand Project," integrity and anti-corruption, artificial intelligence, career perspectives, national reading, and the Long March spirit, with active participation from League organizations across the university.

## Format

Project leaders will present with slides, with 5 minutes per project. Awards based on live scoring:

| Award | Slots | Prize |
|-------|-------|-------|
| First Prize | 1 | ¥200 |
| Second Prize | 3 | ¥150 |
| Third Prize | 5 | ¥100 |
| Excellence Award | 5 | ¥50 |

All winning projects receive certificates of honor.

## Notes

- Nominated League branch secretaries must scan the QR code to join the group by **May 23 (Saturday)**
- Participants should wear League badges and dress appropriately
- Arrive 10 minutes early for check-in; presenters must stay until the end
