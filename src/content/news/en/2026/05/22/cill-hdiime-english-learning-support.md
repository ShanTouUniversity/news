## CILL Opens HDIIME English Learning Support

The Center for Independent Language Learning (CILL) is offering the **How Do I Improve My English? (HDIIME)** room to help students meet their English goals. A trained teacher will provide one-on-one assistance to help organize time and plan study goals for completing end-of-term projects.

The service can help improve individual English skills — listening, reading, speaking, or writing — or assist in developing a comprehensive plan for overall academic success.

Students are encouraged to plan now to improve their English and grades by the end of this term, preparing for a semester of success.
