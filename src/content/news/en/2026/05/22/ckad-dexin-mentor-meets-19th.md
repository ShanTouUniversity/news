## Event Details

- **Theme**: Serious Topics Can Be Fun: Observations on Empathy, Bias, and Interactive Narrative
- **Time**: May 29, 2026 (Friday), 7:20 PM
- **Venue**: Room 103, Building E, East Coast Campus
- **Audience**: Students from CKAD and Dexin College

## Speaker

Wang Liuchuan, Lecturer in Visual Communication Design at the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design, PhD, Master's Supervisor. Research focuses on the impact of art and communication on public perceptions, including game and illustration art, interactive visual novels, visual communication and attitude change, and visual narrative and immersive empathy.
