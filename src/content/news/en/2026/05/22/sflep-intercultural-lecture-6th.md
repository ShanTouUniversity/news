## Lecture Details

- **Topic**: Intercultural Communication Case Writing and Analysis
- **Instructor**: Yang Fan
- **Time**: May 27, 2026 (Wednesday), 3:00–5:00 PM
- **Format**: Online (Tencent Meeting ID: 8730864426)

## Background

This is the sixth lecture in the English Language Center's year-round SFLEP Intercultural Competence Contest coaching program. The series helps students systematically learn intercultural competence theory, analyze typical cases, and develop cross-cultural analytical and critical thinking skills.

## Benefits

- Participants receive extra credit for English courses
- Outstanding students may earn competition nomination
- Open to all students interested in cross-cultural communication, regardless of prior contest experience
