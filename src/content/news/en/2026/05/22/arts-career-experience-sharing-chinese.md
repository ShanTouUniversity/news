## Overview

On the afternoon of May 20, 2026, the School of Arts and Siyuan College hosted a career and study experience sharing session at Sangpushan Campus, featuring eight outstanding students sharing insights on postgraduate study and career preparation.

## Further Study

**Domestic postgraduate exams**: Students Tan X., Tan Y., and Qiu L. shared their preparation experiences, covering phased study plans, subject-specific revision methods, and key professional topics.

**Overseas applications**: Student Wu J. introduced the application process, personal material organization, personal statement writing tips, and practical advice on school selection.

## Career Preparation

**Civil service exams**: Students Tang Y. and Zheng Y. covered full-process preparation planning, aptitude test speed techniques, essay logic training, position selection strategies, and common pitfalls to avoid.

**Teaching qualification exams**: Students Li Z. and Wu W. discussed mindset management, efficient practice methods, exam trend analysis, job posting sources, position selection, demo lessons, and structured interviews — all tailored to the Chinese Language and Literature major.

## Interactive Q&A

Students actively asked questions, with speakers providing patient and detailed answers based on their personal experiences. The School of Arts and Siyuan College announced they will continue hosting such events to support student development.
