## Overview

The Cheung Kong School of Art and Design and Dexin College co-hosted the "Art Views of STU, Dexin Impressions" campus photography contest to encourage students to discover the beauty of campus and cultivate school pride. Following professional review, the winning works have been announced.

## First Prize

| Work | Photographer | Major |
|------|-------------|-------|
| "Wandering Light and Shadow" | Xiao L. | Art Design |
| "Dexin Accompanying the Study Journey" | Chen S. | Chinese Language and Literature |

## Second Prize

| "Geometric Poetics" | Zhang Y. | Environmental Design |
| "Corner of Architecture, Blue of Chenghai" | Zhao B. | Electronic & Computer Engineering |

## Third Prize

Eleven works received third prize, including "Floating Light, Distant Green," "Journey Through Emptiness," "Interwoven Campus Rhythms," "Coastal Geometry," "Twilight by the Sea," "When Monet Paints Again," and more, from diverse majors spanning art design, journalism, communications engineering, and clinical medicine.

Additionally, 18 works received excellence awards.
