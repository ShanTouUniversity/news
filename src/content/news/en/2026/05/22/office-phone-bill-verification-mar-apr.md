## Verification Instructions

Electronic phone bills for March–April 2026 (including both China Telecom and China Mobile landlines) have been imported into the Phone Management System. Unit verifiers should review them promptly.

## Steps

1. Log in at http://lrmo.stu.edu.cn and check "电信固话" and "移动固话" respectively
2. Confirm bills without issues by clicking "确定核实选择的账单"; report discrepancies by **May 26** via email:
   - China Telecom landlines: o_dhs@stu.edu.cn
   - China Mobile landlines: stunic@stu.edu.cn
3. Bills not disputed or confirmed by the deadline will be automatically approved
4. The Finance Office will deduct charges from unit budgets on **May 28**

> Source: STU OA Notice (Network and Information Center, Resource Management Department)
