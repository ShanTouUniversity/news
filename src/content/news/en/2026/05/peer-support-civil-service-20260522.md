The 21st edition of the "Peer Support Program" — titled "Charting the Public Service Path, Building Dreams for the Voyage" — will be held online via Tencent Meeting on **May 22 at 19:30**. The event is co-organized by the Science College and Zhicheng College.

## Guest Speakers

Two alumni who have successfully passed civil service examinations will share their experiences:

- **Student Luo**, a 2021 cohort Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering major, now working at the Xinan Subdistrict Office in Sanshui District, Foshan, Guangdong
- **Student Huang**, a 2022 cohort Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering major, now working at a tax bureau in Guangdong

The speakers will cover topics including career planning, exam preparation strategies, and study methods.

## How to Join

The session is primarily intended for 2025-cohort undergraduate students of the Science College. Students can register and earn second classroom credits by searching for the event on the "Dao Meng Kong Jian" app. The Tencent Meeting ID is 961-240-852.
