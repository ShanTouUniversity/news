## Graduation Ceremony Held

On the afternoon of May 23, the graduation ceremony for the 15th STU Young Marxist Program Party Activist Training Course was held in Room 105, Building E at the East Coast Campus, with a satellite venue at Lecture Hall 1 on the Sangpushan Campus. Lin Jianqing, deputy head of the Party Committee Organization Department, along with Li Liting and other faculty and League organization leaders attended the event and reviewed the training journey with 482 participants.

## Themed Presentations

The ceremony opened with the national anthem. Each temporary League branch then delivered themed presentations on topics including the holistic national security concept, the spirit of the National Two Sessions, and establishing and practicing a correct performance outlook. Groups presented through patriotic songs, stage plays, and poetry recitations, with judges scoring the performances.

## Graduation and Awards

The training course included 482 participants. After recommendation, review, and approval, **477 students** met the graduation requirements and received certificates from Ji Peina as representatives of the graduating class. In addition, **138 students** were named "Outstanding Participants" and received awards from Li Liting.

After on-site evaluation, the 7th, 8th, and 1st temporary League branches were named "Outstanding League Branches." 

## Closing Remarks

In her closing remarks, Li Liting commended the participants for their growth during the program and encouraged them to strengthen theoretical study, build solid professional expertise, and take initiative in responsibility, turning what they learned into real action.
