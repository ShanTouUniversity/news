## Revised Outage Schedule

The Infrastructure Department and Resource Management Department have issued a new notice adjusting the previously announced power outage schedule, as construction progress has not met the requirements for power delivery. The updated times are as follows:

| Phase | Previously Announced | Adjusted Time |
|-------|---------------------|---------------|
| 1st (May 23, Saturday) | Previously adjusted to 12:00 PM | **6:00 PM** |
| 2nd (May 24, Sunday) | 1:00 PM | **1:00 PM** (unchanged) |

Each switch will cause a **5 to 15-minute power outage** in affected areas.

## Affected Areas

All 16 transformers under Switch Station No. 1 are affected, including:

- **Student dormitories**: Buildings A–F, L, Y, P
- **Dining & shops**: 2nd and 3rd canteens, commercial area
- **Teaching areas**: Teaching Buildings C, D, G; Science Building; Engineering Building; Liberal Arts Building; Technology Building; Machinery & Electrical Building; Audio-Visual Building
- **Colleges & arts**: Shude College, Art College
- **Administration**: Administration Center, East/West Administration Buildings
- **Faculty housing**: High-rise faculty apartments, faculty dormitories A1–A14, B1–B16
- **Others**: Network Center, Auditorium, Old Gymnasium, Campus Hospital, Kindergarten, Affiliated Middle/Primary Schools, High-Level Lab, Air Dome
