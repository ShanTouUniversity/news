The STU Youth League Committee is recruiting two current or graduating graduate students from the School of Math and Computer Science, School of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, and College of Science to teach math, physics, or chemistry at Taijiang National Middle School in Guizhou.

Part of the Western Plan Guizhou Special Program, volunteers are covered by Western Plan benefits and must report by end of July. Interested applicants should send their CV to Ms. Huang Jianan (jmhuang2@stu.edu.cn) by June 25 at 17:00, with the subject line "College + Name + Taijiang Teaching Volunteer."

> Source: STU OA Notice (Youth League Committee)
