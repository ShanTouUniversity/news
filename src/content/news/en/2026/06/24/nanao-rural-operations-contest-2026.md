The 3rd Nan'ao County Rural Operations Manager Competition, themed "Shared Mountains and Seas, Cultivating Nan'ao," calls for proposals from university teams, planning firms, and青年 entrepreneurs.

**Scope**

Teams select one of six villages or a cluster to develop a full-chain rural operations project covering planning, design, and implementation.

**Timeline**

| Phase | Date |
|-------|------|
| Preliminary proposal | Due Jul 31 |
| Review | Early Aug |
| Field research | Late Aug |
| Final proposal | Oct 30 |
| Finals & awards | Early Nov |

**Prizes**

1st Prize: 80,000 yuan (1 winner); 2nd Prize: 50,000 yuan (2); 3rd Prize: 20,000 yuan (3); Excellence: 10,000 yuan (2). Each finalist receives 5,000 yuan for research. An additional 50,000 yuan implementation bonus for projects breaking ground within one year.
