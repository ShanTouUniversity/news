The Security Office and Student Affairs Office have jointly launched the 2026 "National Anti-Fraud in Action" 100-Day campaign (mid-June to mid-September), themed "Don't listen, don't believe, don't be greedy."

**Three key risks**: job scams targeting graduates, students lured overseas for telecom fraud, and renting out phone cards/bank accounts ("two cards") to become fraud accomplices.

Activities include: legal education on the Anti-Telecom Fraud Law, screening CCTV anti-fraud documentaries, and class-based summer special sessions warning against overseas job offers. Students are required to report suspicious recruitment info to counselors or the Security Office. Summaries due by September 10 to o_bwk@stu.edu.cn.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Security Office, Student Affairs Office)
