The Admission and Employment Office has announced a 2026 graduate employment coordination meeting on June 29 (Monday) at 10:30 in the Administration Building central hall conference room. The agenda includes a review of recent employment progress, reports from college representatives, and remarks from university leadership. College Party secretaries, deputy secretaries, and career counselors are required to attend.
