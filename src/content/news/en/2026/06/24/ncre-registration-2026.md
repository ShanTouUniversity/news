Registration for the 2026 second-half National Computer Rank Examination (NCRE) is now open. The exam will be held in batches starting September 19 at the STU test center (Building E, Sangpu Mountain Campus), with 1,200 available seats. Social applicants are welcome.

**Registration Period**: June 25 10:00 – July 3 16:00

**Website**: https://ncre-bm.neea.cn or https://ncre-bm.neea.edu.cn

**Fee**: RMB 137 per subject, max 3 subjects per session

**Admission Ticket**: Available for printing from September 15

Payment must be completed within 24 hours of submission. Since 2022, only electronic certificates are issued.

Contact: Xie, xhx@stu.edu.cn, 86502402.
