Following a Party committee meeting, Li Shengkang has been proposed as the head of a department within the College of Science.

Li Shengkang, male, born October 1975, holds a doctoral degree in science and currently serves as Vice Dean of the Belt and Road Research Institute.

The public notice period runs from June 24 to 30. Objections may be raised to the College of Science Party Committee (Science Building Room 354, 0754-86502829, o_lxyb@stu.edu.cn).
