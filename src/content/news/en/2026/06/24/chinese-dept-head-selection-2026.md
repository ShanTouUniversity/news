Following a Party committee meeting, the College of Arts has announced the open selection of one department head for Chinese Language and Literature.

**Requirements**: Strong political awareness; doctoral degree with 2+ years at STU; associate professor or higher with management experience; able to serve at least 2 full terms.

**Scope**: Eligible on-campus staff within the Chinese department.

**Process**: Democratic recommendation, organizational review, and Party committee decision. Contact: 86502214, o_wxy@stu.edu.cn.
