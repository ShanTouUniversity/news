According to the Organization Department of the CPC Shantou University Committee, a decision was made at the 16th meeting of the University Party Committee Standing Committee held on June 24, 2026:

Six comrades — Zhu KY, Huang JY, Guo JQ, Zheng WY, Chen CX, and Tan Y — are approved as probationary members of the Communist Party of China, with a one-year probationary period.

Seven comrades — Cao AJ, Chen JM, Fu JQ, Liu JY, Yan YX, Zheng RB, and Chen TP — are approved for regularization as full members of the Communist Party of China.
