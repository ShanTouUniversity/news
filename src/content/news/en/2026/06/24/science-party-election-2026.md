Approved by the university Party committee, the College of Science held a Party meeting on June 24 at 15:00 in the Science and Technology Lecture Hall on the Sangpu Mountain Campus to elect a new committee. All Party members attended, with preparatory members and non-Party representatives as observers.

Party members were required to wear badges, arrive 15 minutes early, and observe strict electoral discipline.
