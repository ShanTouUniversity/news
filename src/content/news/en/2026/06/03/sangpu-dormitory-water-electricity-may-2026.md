## Billing Information

The May 2026 utility bills for Sangpu Campus student dormitories have been released. The billing period is **May 1–31**, with a per-person allowance of 8 kWh electricity, 1.6 tons hot water, and 2.4 tons cold water.

## Review

**June 3–5**: Log in to the utility billing system to check:

- **Hongyi, Siyuan, Zhixing Colleges & Graduate Building A**: http://192.168.48.49:8080/hpao/main
- **Other dormitories (including New College Phase 2, Building 3)**: http://power.stu.edu.cn/ (campus network account)

For discrepancies, submit the completed verification form to the Utility Center (near the Science & Technology Building). Phone: 86502400, 86502489.

## Payment

If no objections, pay online via the system from **June 6** (ensure sufficient card balance). Accounts not paid by **June 8** will be auto-deducted from the campus card.
