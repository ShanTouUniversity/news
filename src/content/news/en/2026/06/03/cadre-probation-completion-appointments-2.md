It is reported that following probation assessments, the STU Party Committee has formally appointed three cadres:

- **Cai Hao** as Director of the Network and Information Center
- **Huang Guzi** as Discipline Committee Secretary of Shantou University Medical College
- **Fan Jibin** as Party Committee Secretary of the Joint Shantou International Eye Center (Shantou University & Chinese University of Hong Kong)

The appointments take effect retroactively from the start of their probation periods.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Party Committee Office)
