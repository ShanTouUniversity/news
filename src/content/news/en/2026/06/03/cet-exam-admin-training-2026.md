Following the Guangdong Education Examination Authority's requirements, the online training for 2026 first-half CET-4/6 exam staff is now open.

## Participants

All exam administration staff, invigilators, and exam monitors (both on-campus and assigned).

## Schedule

**June 3 – June 12, 4:00 PM** — complete within this window.

## Platform

NEEA Exam Staff Online Training Platform: https://kwstudy.neea.edu.cn/

## Process

1. **Register**: Create a personal account via desktop browser
2. **Course Learning**: Complete mandatory courses (full duration required); electives are optional
3. **Assessment**: Randomized questions, **85/100 passing score**, 25-minute limit, up to 10 attempts

## Notes

- Only trained and qualified staff may undertake exam duties
- On-campus offline training is also required (details to follow)
- Do not screenshot or share system operations
