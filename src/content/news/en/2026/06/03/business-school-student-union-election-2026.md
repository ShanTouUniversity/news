To strengthen student union leadership, the Business School is now recruiting candidates for the 27th Student Union presidium, with **3 seats** available.

## Eligibility

Full-time undergraduate students of the Business School with at least one year of student leadership experience. Applicants must be CCP members or Communist Youth League members, with academic ranking in the top 30% of their major and no failed required courses.

## Process

Voluntary application → Joint review by the College Youth League Committee → 3 official candidates confirmed → Election at the 27th Student Congress.

## Application

Submit the application form to tye@stu.edu.cn by **June 9, midnight**. Contact: Ms. Ye (0754-86500247).
