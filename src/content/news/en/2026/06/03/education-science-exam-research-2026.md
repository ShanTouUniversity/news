## Program Overview

Approved by the Ministry of Education Party Committee, the 2026 National Education Science Planning Education Examination Research Special Project focuses on advancing education examination reform. Funding tiers: Key projects (350,000 yuan), General projects (200,000 yuan), and Cultivation projects (100,000 yuan). Research period: 2–3 years.

## Requirements

- Applicants must hold associate professor (or higher) rank, bureau-level leadership, or a doctoral degree
- Active holders of national or ministry-level projects, or NEEA research projects, are ineligible
- Topics must be selected from the provided guide
- System open: **June 10 (midnight) – June 25 (5:00 PM)**
