## Program Overview

The 2026 National Education Science Planning Lifelong Education System Research Special Project is now accepting applications. Funding tiers: Major (600,000 yuan), Key (350,000 yuan), and General (200,000 yuan). Research period: 2–3 years.

## Requirements

- Major project applicants must hold senior professional title or bureau-level leadership
- Key/General applicants need associate professor rank or doctorate
- Project teams must include current open university faculty or researchers
- Topics must use the exact guide titles; no adjustments allowed
- System open: **June 10 (midnight) – June 25 (5:00 PM)**

## Campus Schedule

| Date | Action |
|------|--------|
| **By June 9** | Register on platform and email Research Office for review |
| **By June 15** | Submit application and proposal to system for review |
| **By June 22** | Submit signed hard copy to Research Office |
| **June 23** | Collect stamped application, scan and upload to platform |
