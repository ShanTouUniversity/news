Following the completion of upgrades at the 110KV Yujing Substation, Sangpu Campus will switch power loads from the 10KV Diexue Line to the 10KV Yuda Line. Some areas will experience **5–15 minute** temporary power outages.

**Switching time**: **June 6 (Saturday), 1:00 PM**

**Affected areas**:

| Transformer | Affected Areas |
|-------------|---------------|
| Switch Station 1 | Station 1, East Pump Room |
| Student Dormitories | Buildings A/B/C/D/L/Y, former boiler room, Canteens 2 & 3, market |
| Professor Building | Dormitories E/F, Building P |
| Exchange Building | Shude College, Art College |
| High-Rise Faculty Apt. | High-rise faculty apartments |
| Faculty Housing | A1-A14, B1-B12, kindergarten, hospital, seismic station, union |
| West Pump Room | West pump room, middle/high school, B13-B16 |
| Auditorium | Auditorium, lake sewage pump |
| Admin Center | Former admin center |
| Science Building | Science Building |
| Engineering Building | Wind tunnel, Engineering Building, East Lecture Hall |
| Former Medical Bldg | High-level lab, Teaching C/D/G, Social Sciences |
| Liberal Arts Bldg | Liberal Arts, AV Building, West Lecture Hall, Physics Lab |
| Science & Tech Bldg | Science & Tech Building, Mechanical-Electrical Building |
| Network Center | Network center, foundation office, expert apartments |
| Old Gymnasium | Old gymnasium |
