## Workshop Details

The Business School and the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment are co-hosting the 4th session of the "AI-Empowered Education and Teaching" workshop series, presented by Prof. Zhou Junjie.

- **Topic**: AI-Empowered Business Courses: Practice and Reflections
- **Speaker**: Prof. Zhou Junjie
- **Time**: **June 10, 2026 (Wednesday), 3:00 PM**
- **Venue**: Room 328, Admin Building, Donghai'an Campus

## About the Speaker

Prof. Zhou Junjie holds a PhD from Renmin University of China and is a professor at STU Business School. He serves as Executive Director of the Guangdong Key Lab for Digital Management and Smart Governance. He has authored textbooks on big data and AI for business, led two provincial teaching research projects, received four provincial teaching awards, and published over 40 papers in journals including *MIS Quarterly*.

## Registration

Faculty interested in attending should register by scanning the QR code before **June 8, 5:30 PM**. The session counts toward continuing education hours.
