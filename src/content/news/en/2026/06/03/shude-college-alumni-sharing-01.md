## Event Details

To celebrate its 10th anniversary, Shude College launches its "Outstanding Alumni Sharing" series. Session I is themed **"Finding Your Own Path, Each Blooming in Their Own Way."**

- **Time**: **June 7 (Sunday), 9:30–11:30 AM**
- **Venue**: Shude College, 2nd Floor Multi-Function Hall
- **Capacity**: 80 students and staff

## Speakers

- **Alumna Zhu** (2018, Marine Science): Cross-disciplinary master's in Global China Studies, former Big Four audit and EY-Parthenon, now in finance and M&A in Hong Kong
- **Alumna Xu** (2019, Clinical Medicine): Top of her cohort, recommended to Shanghai Jiao Tong University for surgery master's; 2 SCI papers, 9 patents
- **Alumna Fang** (2020, Product Design): Built a personal brand from 0 to 100K followers and monthly revenue exceeding 3M yuan in one year
