## Training Details

The university hospital will hold the 3rd Red Cross CPR+AED First Aid certification training on **June 10 (Wednesday), 2:00–5:30 PM** at Room 101, Building E, Donghai'an Campus.

The session covers theoretical instruction, hands-on practice, and certification exams. Registered participants should join the WeChat group by **June 7** and set their nickname to "Name + Department/College + Year."

## Reminders

- Wear loose clothing and avoid accessories for the practice session
- Arrive 10 minutes early for check-in
- Absence or incomplete attendance will result in no certificate
