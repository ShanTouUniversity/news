## Program Overview

Approved by the Ministry of Education Party Committee, the 2026 National Education Science Planning Student Development Research Special Project focuses on graduate employment and student competency development. Funding tiers: Major projects (600,000 yuan), Key projects (350,000 yuan), and General projects (200,000 yuan). Research period: 1–2 years.

## Requirements

- Applicants must hold associate professor (or higher) rank, bureau-level leadership, or a doctoral degree
- Active holders of national or ministry-level projects are ineligible
- Topics must be selected from the provided guide; no self-proposed topics
- System open: **June 10 (midnight) – June 25 (5:00 PM)**

## Campus Schedule

|------|--------|
