The College of Science, following the university's selection process and based on individual applications, supervisor recommendations, and college evaluation, has recommended six master's students as Outstanding Graduates of 2026. The nominees specialize in Optical Engineering, Biology, Bioengineering, and Marine Science.

Objections should be submitted in writing to the College of Science (Room 345) by **10:00 AM, June 6, 2026**. Department submissions must bear an official seal; individual submissions must include the signatory's real name.
