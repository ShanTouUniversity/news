The College of Journalism and Communication has recommended two master's students as Outstanding Graduates of 2026, in the fields of Journalism and Communication (full-time) and Journalism and Communication (part-time).

Objections should be submitted in writing by **5:30 PM, June 5, 2026** to the college office. Department submissions must bear an official seal; individual submissions must include the signatory's real name.
