Following a meeting of the School of Mathematics and Computer Science Party Committee, three faculty members are proposed for appointment as department (center) heads:

- **Wang Dabin** (b. 1978), PhD in Science, Professor, Department of Mathematics
- **Cai Lingru** (b. 1979), PhD in Engineering, Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science, CCP member
- **Xu Jianlong** (b. 1982), PhD in Engineering, Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science, CCP member

Public comments are welcome from **June 3 to June 10, 2026**, via written submission, in-person visit, or phone call to the School Party Committee.
