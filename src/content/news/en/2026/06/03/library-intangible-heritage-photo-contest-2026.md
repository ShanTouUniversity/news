## Competition Overview

The 2026 Guangdong University Library Intangible Cultural Heritage Photography Contest, hosted by the Guangdong Provincial Steering Committee for Library and Information Work, recently announced its results. The competition covered 98 universities across the province with 2,461 entries submitted.

## Results

STU students earned one second prize, one third prize, six excellence awards, and multiple outstanding shortlist entries. Ling Xuemin received the Outstanding Instructor Award, and the library won the Best Organization Award.
