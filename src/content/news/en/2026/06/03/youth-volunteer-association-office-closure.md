Due to the approaching end-of-semester exam period, the STU Youth Volunteer Association office (Room 832, Building 789) will suspend its duty services from **June 12, 2026 (Friday of Week 15)**.

All student organizations and college youth volunteer associations are requested to return any borrowed materials promptly. For urgent material requests after June 12, please contact the designated personnel.

> Source: STU Youth League Committee & Youth Volunteer Association (OA Notice)
