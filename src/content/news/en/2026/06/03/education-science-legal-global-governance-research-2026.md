## Program Overview

Approved by the Ministry of Education Party Committee, the 2026 National Education Science Planning Special Project on China's Education Rule of Law and Global Education Governance Research is now accepting applications. Funding tiers: Major (600,000 yuan), Key (350,000 yuan), General (200,000 yuan), and Cultivation (100,000 yuan).

## Requirements

- Major project applicants must hold senior professional title or bureau-level leadership
- Key/General/Cultivation applicants need associate professor rank, doctorate, or relevant experience with master's degree
- Active national or ministry-level project holders are ineligible
- Major/Key/General projects must select from the guide; Cultivation projects may make minor adjustments
- System open: **June 10 (midnight) – June 25 (5:00 PM)**

## Campus Schedule
