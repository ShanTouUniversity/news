The Research Office has drafted the STU Scientific Research Achievement Transformation Management Measures along with four implementation rules covering achievement transfer, licensing, granting ownership or long-term usage rights to researchers, and equity investment. The documents are now open for public comment.

Feedback should be submitted to **o_cgk@stu.edu.cn** by **June 10, 2026**.
