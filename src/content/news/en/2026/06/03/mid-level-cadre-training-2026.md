As part of the 2026 cadre training plan, the university will hold a mid-level cadre training program hosted by Wuhan University.

- **Participants**: Department-level officials, second-level Party secretaries and deputy secretaries, heads of teaching and support units, and seconded cadres
- **Dates**: **July 5–11** (7 days)
- **Location**: Wuhan University, Hubei

Units should confirm participants and submit registration by **June 9**. Those unable to attend should also indicate their reasons.

Participants must strictly follow training discipline; leave is generally not permitted.
