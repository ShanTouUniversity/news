## Competition Overview

The 2026 Shantou Network Civilization Video Competition (Qinglang "Ju Geili") was organized by the Municipal Cybersecurity Emergency Command Center to advance cyber governance education and telecom fraud prevention. The finals were held on May 31. Six works from Shantou University received awards, and one faculty member won an excellence in instruction award.

## Award List

| Work | Award | Student Participants | Advisor |
|------|-------|---------------------|---------|
| *Under the Fingertips* | First Prize | 7 students | Lan Chun |
| *I'm Still Here* | Second Prize | 9 students | Tang Wanxiang |
| *Online Stories, New Life* | Third Prize | 11 students | Liang Wenxuan |
| *Rhythm Defense* | Third Prize | 7 students | Ma Zexiong |
| *History for a Clearer Web* | Excellence Award | 8 students | — |
| *Clean Fingertips, Kind Hearts* | Excellence Award | 10 students | Zhu Xiaoxue |

In addition, Lan Chun received the Excellence in Instruction Award, and the works *I'm Still Here* and *Online Stories, New Life* received the Outstanding Video Creation Award.
