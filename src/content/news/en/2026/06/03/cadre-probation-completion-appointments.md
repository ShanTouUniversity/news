It is reported that following probation assessments, the STU Party Committee has formally appointed six cadres:

- **Xu Fei** as Director of the Academic Affairs Office
- **Wu Yingfan** as Director of the Student Affairs Department, and concurrent roles including Executive Vice Dean of the College Academy and Director of the People's Armed Forces Department
- **Yao Yi** as Director of the East Coast Campus Management Committee
- **Jiang Yaomu** as Party Committee Secretary of the Second Affiliated Hospital of Shantou University Medical College
- **Chen Yexi** as Deputy Party Committee Secretary and President of the Second Affiliated Hospital
- **Liu Siwei** as Party Committee Secretary of the Cancer Hospital affiliated with Shantou University Medical College

The appointments take effect retroactively from the start of their probation periods.
