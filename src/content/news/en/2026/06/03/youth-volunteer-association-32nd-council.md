## Election Conference

The 32nd election conference of Shantou University Youth Volunteer Association was held on May 31 in Room 101, Building E, Donghai'an Campus. Of the 182 eligible members, 174 attended, including 93 voting representatives.

The conference reviewed and approved the work report delivered by the 31st council president on behalf of the outgoing council.

## Election Results

Five students were elected to the 32nd council of the STU Youth Volunteer Association through representative voting.

At the first meeting of the newly elected 32nd council, one president and four vice presidents were elected.
