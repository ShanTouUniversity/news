The May 2026 utility bills for faculty temporary housing have been released for the billing period of **May 1–31**. Log in to the STU Utility Billing System (http://power.stu.edu.cn/) using your campus network account to check.

For inquiries or disputes, contact the Utility Center before **June 15** during office hours (phone: 86502489, 86502400). After the deadline, the Finance Office will deduct the amount from your salary account. Tenants whose salary accounts cannot be deducted should obtain a payment notice from the Utility Center and pay at the Finance Office.

> Source: Resource Management Office (OA Notice)
