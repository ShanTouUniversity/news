## Billing Information

The May 2026 utility bills for Donghai'an Campus student dormitories have been released. The billing period is **May 1–31**, with a per-person allowance of 8 kWh electricity, 1.6 tons hot water, and 2.4 tons cold water.

## Review

Log in to the STU Utility Billing System (http://power.stu.edu.cn/) using your campus network account. For discrepancies, submit the completed verification form to **Room 915, Building C, Donghai'an Campus** during office hours by **June 5** (phone: 86500175).

## Payment

If no objections, pay online via the system from **June 6**. Accounts not confirmed or paid by **June 8** will be auto-deducted from the campus card.

Ensure sufficient card balance. Disputes raised after the review period may take 3–5 business days to resolve.

> Source: Donghai'an Campus Management Committee (OA Notice)
