It is reported that the International College will organize a group movie screening for enrolled international students on the evening of June 18. The film, *A Love Letter to Grandma*, aims to help international students better understand Chaoshan's Qiaopi (overseas Chinese remittance letters) culture and experience local family stories.

**Event Details**

The film will be shown at Xingyuehui Time Theater Cinema. Participation is limited to 40 people on a first-come, first-served basis. Specific meeting time and location will be announced separately.

**Registration**

All enrolled international students are eligible to register via QR code scan. Given the limited spots, registered participants are expected to attend unless under exceptional circumstances.

**Guidelines**

- Participants must arrive at the designated meeting point on time and travel together with the group to the cinema
- Maintain quiet and orderly conduct during the screening
- A brief random interview will be arranged after the film for participants to share their thoughts
- Stay safe and travel in groups during the trip

The event is free of charge.
