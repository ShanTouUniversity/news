It is reported that the Academic Affairs Office has published three projects recommended for the 2026 National Teaching Achievement Award. Based on the provincial education department notice and the results of STU's provincial teaching achievement awards, three projects have been recommended for national-level selection:

| Project Title | Co-leads |
|--------------|----------|
| Collaborative Iteration, Universal Intelligence: Exploring New Forms of Advanced Undergraduate Education in Local Universities in the Smart Era | Hao Zhifeng, Han Baocheng, et al. (15 members) |
| Boundless, Cross-disciplinary, Diverse: Reconstructing an Interdisciplinary Education Ecology through Residential College-School Collaboration | Tang Rui, Wu Yingfan, et al. (16 members) |
| "Five Dimensions, Three Stages" — A 20-Year Innovation in Smart Clinical Competence Training | Xia Oudong, Shi Chujun, et al. (15 members) |

The public notification runs until June 24.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
