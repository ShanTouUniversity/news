It is reported that the Student Affairs Office, International College, and Resource Management Office have announced summer stay-on-campus arrangements. Students who need to remain on campus for university business must apply through the student affairs system from June 20 to 26 and obtain approval from their supervisor and college.

Dormitories without approved applications will have power cut off on July 20, with power restored on September 3. A 24-hour access control system will be in effect, and utilities will not be subsidized during the summer.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office, International College, Resource Management Office)
