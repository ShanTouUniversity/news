It is reported that the Institute of Mathematics will host the 953rd session of the "Towards Modern Mathematics" academic lecture series on June 25 at 10:00, featuring Professor Sun Wenchang from Nankai University's School of Mathematical Sciences, speaking on "Gabor Phase Retrieval."

**Lecture Details**

- Topic: Gabor Phase Retrieval
- Time: June 25, 10:00
- Tencent Meeting ID: 309905051
- Invited by: Lou Zengjian, Yu Haixia

**Abstract**

Gabor phase retrieval refers to recovering a square-integrable function from the absolute values of its Gabor transform (short-time Fourier transform with a Gaussian window), up to a constant factor. The talk studies Gabor phase retrieval based on discrete sampling points, considering three types of sampling sequences corresponding to square-root lattices, square-root sequences on two intersecting lines, and square-root sequences on three parallel lines, with optimal sampling densities derived for each case.

**About the Speaker**

Sun Wenchang is a professor at Nankai University's School of Mathematical Sciences, specializing in wavelet analysis and harmonic analysis. He has received the National Outstanding Youth Science Fund (2015), the State Council Special Allowance (2010), and the Tianjin Natural Science First Prize (first author, 2008). He has published over 90 papers in journals including *Advances in Mathematics*, *Mathematische Annalen*, and *Journal of Functional Analysis*.
