It is reported that the university will relocate East Coast Campus undergraduates back to Sangpu Mountain Campus in batches from July to September 2026, involving 1,773 students across four colleges.

**Relocation Schedule**

| Date | College | Current Building | Students | Destination |
|------|---------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| Jul 6 | Cheung Kong Art & Design College | East Campus Building H | 243 | Dexin College |
| Jul 10 | Chemistry & Chemical Engineering | East Campus Building B | 231 | Zhixing College |
| Mid-Sep | School of Science | East Campus Building H | 403 | Zhicheng College |
| Sep 5–6 | Engineering College | East Campus Building B | 896 | Phase 2, Bldg 1 & 2 |

**Guidelines**

- Students are expected to clean their dormitories before checking out
- Keep valuables with you during the move; report any loss to your counselor immediately
- Relocation vehicles will be arranged separately for passengers and belongings, organized by college
- Schedules are subject to adjustment per college notice
