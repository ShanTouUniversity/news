It is reported that the Guangdong Market Supervision Bureau, together with Hong Kong and Macao IP authorities and Dongguan municipal government, is hosting the 2026 GBA High-Value Intellectual Property Competition under the theme "IP Bridges Dual Chains, Digital Intelligence Drives Integration." The competition aims to empower industry-service integration through IP and foster high-quality research innovation teams. Registration closes August 31.
