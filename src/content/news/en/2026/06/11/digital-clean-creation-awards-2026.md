It is reported that the Engineering College Party Committee and Jingyi College have announced the results of the "Digital & Clean" Science and Technology Creative Contest, held from April to May to deepen the "Clean Engineering" integrity culture brand and diversify integrity education. The event received an enthusiastic response from students.

Following anonymous expert review of entries based on thematic fit, creativity, and completeness, the winners are as follows:

| Award | Winner | Work Title |
|-------|--------|------------|
| First Prize | Lin JW | Clean Core, Bright Bamboo |
| Second Prize | Zhou BB | AI Education Assistant |
| Second Prize | Chen MX | Scales of Integrity |
| Third Prize | Zhang MH | Growing Toward the Sun, Walking with Integrity |
| Third Prize | Wu QC | Lotus Core, Bright Heart |
| Third Prize | Huang XY, Wang YQ, Sun YR | Where Spring Breeze Passes |
