The course evaluation system for the 2025-2026 spring semester Graduate English course is now open. Full-time graduate students enrolled in the course must log in to the STU Graduate Training Management Information System (http://192.168.48.143/) using their campus network account and complete the evaluation by June 15.

The evaluation period runs from June 10 to June 15. Students are required to rate according to the criteria and provide comments and suggestions. For any system issues, students should contact their respective college's graduate academic staff.
