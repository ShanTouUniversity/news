The STU Admissions and Employment Office, together with the Career Planning and Development Association, will hold the fourth career planning sharing session of the 2026 spring semester on June 14, focusing on 2027 Guangdong civil service examinations.

Two sessions are scheduled: the Sangpushan campus session from 4:00pm to 5:00pm at E-J101, and the East Coast campus session from 7:30pm to 8:30pm at E109, Teaching Building E.

The sharing will cover the latest trends and policy changes in national exams, provincial exams, selection transfers, and public institution exams; a full timeline and key milestones for 2027 exam preparation; position selection strategies by major; salary data and historical admission scores across 21 Guangdong cities; and effective study planning.

All enrolled students are welcome. Attendees will receive exam preparation materials including comprehensive textbooks, past exam papers, and 1,500 practice questions.
