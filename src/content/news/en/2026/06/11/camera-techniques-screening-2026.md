The Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication will hold a public screening of "Camera Techniques" course works by the 2025 cohort of Network and New Media majors on June 12 from 10:00am to 11:40am at Room 133, Building E, East Coast Campus. The event is open to all STU students and faculty.

Under the theme "Dialogue Across Time and Space," the screening will feature documentary works exploring "a profession," with students capturing the dignity and warmth behind ordinary jobs through their lenses. The works reflect the students' growth from observers to recorders, blending professional skills with creative practice.

Students may register via the "Dao Meng Kong Jian" APP, with limited spots available. Participants who attend the full event will receive second classroom credits and德育 module points for the Journalism College's comprehensive evaluation.
