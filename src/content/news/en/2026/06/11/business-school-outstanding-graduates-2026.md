The Business School has launched the selection of outstanding 2026 undergraduate graduates, aiming to recognize academically excellent and morally commendable students and foster a positive learning atmosphere.

The selection is open to all full-time 2026 undergraduate graduates of the Business School. Applicants must meet the following criteria: overall GPA ranking within the top 20% of their cohort and major, no course retakes, at least one university-level scholarship or award, and an excellent thesis or a provincial-level or above award in academic competitions. Priority will be given to candidates with notable achievements in social practice, volunteer service, innovation and entrepreneurship, graduate school admission, or confirmed employment.

Graduates who have enlisted in the military or joined grassroots service programs such as the "Three Supports and One Assistance" or the Western Plan will receive priority consideration in the review process.

Applicants must submit the completed application form and summary sheet along with supporting documents to the Business School Youth League Committee email (o_stw@stu.edu.cn) by 10pm on June 14. The selection quota shall not exceed 10% of the total graduates in each major. The school will organize a review, publicize the results, and confer the honorary title of "Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate."
