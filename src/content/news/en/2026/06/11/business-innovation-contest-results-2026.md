It is reported that the Business School, in collaboration with China Mobile Chenghai Branch, has concluded the "Group+Brand Store" Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition and project recruitment selection. The winners are as follows:

| Award | Project Name | Team Members |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| First Prize | "Chaoyu Coffee" — Exploring New Paths for Chaozhou-Shantou Culture "Coffee+" Communication | Lin YZ, Zhang JK, Peng BB, Wen HX, Wen WC |
| First Prize | Chao Ju Hong Yun — Toys Empowering Chaoshan Intangible Cultural Heritage Communication | Huang JJ, Guo YL, Chen JY, Huang TD, Wu ST |
| Second Prize | 123 Eggplant! Campus Photoshoot Platform | Wang X, Fu ZM, Huang ZJ, Qin RF, Huang YQ |
| Second Prize | Campus Fitness Light Food Store (Wild Health Co-branded) | Ye WH, Liang JH, Hai B, Song J, Pan WJ, Ye JM, Zhang HB |
| Third Prize | Heart Whisper, Wisdom Healing — Personalized AI Therapy Based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy | Cen YY, Feng SM, Huang ZN, Lin F, Chen JL, Wang PS |
