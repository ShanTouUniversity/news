The 33rd annual meeting and the 34th Editorial Board election conference of the STU Youth Media Center was held on the afternoon of June 10 at Room E201, Teaching Building E, East Coast Campus. Of the 57 eligible members, 51 were present.

The conference reviewed and approved the work report delivered by Chen SY, Editor-in-Chief of the 33rd Editorial Board.

Following the vote, seven students were elected to the 34th Editorial Board: Guo MC (24 Business Administration), Yu BB (24 Journalism), Pan NZ (24 Business Administration), Huang HL (25 Journalism), Wang JH (25 Civil Engineering), He ZM (25 Journalism), and Zhang SQ (24 Advertising).

At the first meeting of the 34th Editorial Board, Guo MC was elected Editor-in-Chief, with Yu BB and Pan NZ serving as Deputy Editors-in-Chief.
