The Lafang Oriental Aesthetics Innovation Center, jointly established by the Art and Design program of CKAD and the Lafang Group, is now recruiting interdisciplinary student members from across the university. The project aims to create a "chief content officer" and "experience innovation lab" for the Lafang brand through deep integration of academic think tanks and industrial practice.

The project covers two main areas. The online brand narrative track handles the full chain of planning, copywriting, visual design, and community operations for Lafang's WeChat official account and Xiaohongshu. The offline experience innovation track assists in creating Lafang's first offline experience space, including spatial theme planning, functional zoning, experience flow design, and event planning.

All full-time undergraduate and postgraduate students are eligible, with preference for those majoring in art design, journalism, marketing, or Chinese language. Applicants should possess skills in copywriting, visual design, or event planning. Selected members will gain industry experience, academic resources, employment priority access, a dedicated workspace, and a stable project stipend.

Applications are open until June 15 at 6pm. Candidates should submit their resume and portfolio to liujiefei@stu.edu.cn with the subject line "Lafang Project Application - Name - Major - Year." Shortlisted applicants will be interviewed before June 17.
