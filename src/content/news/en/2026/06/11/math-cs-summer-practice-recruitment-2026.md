The School of Mathematics and Computer Science Youth League Committee is organizing the "Children's Dreams, Digital Voyage" youth development social practice activity for summer 2026, heading to Jiexi County, Jieyang City, Guangdong Province from July 11 to 14. The program features digital science education and public interest classes, with recruitment open to all students.

Four teams are being formed: Publicity Team, Education Team, Social Survey Team, and Entertainment Team. Online registration runs until **June 15 at 12:00** via the questionnaire link: https://v.wjx.cn/vm/mBOv8of.aspx#. Interviews may be arranged depending on application volume, with follow-up notices posted in the consultation group.

Requirements include: strong sense of responsibility, full participation in pre-service training and fieldwork, teamwork spirit, commitment to volunteer service, adequate time availability, and proper conduct representing the university's youth志愿者 image.

Participants will receive volunteer service hours. Detailed team introductions are available in the OA system attachments.
