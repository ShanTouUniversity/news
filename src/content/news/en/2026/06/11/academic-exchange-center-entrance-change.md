## Entrance Relocation

According to the Shantou University Academic Exchange Service Center, effective June 12, 2026 (Friday), the main entrance to the center's area has been relocated from Gate 2 to Gate 3. All students, staff, and visitors are advised to adjust their routes accordingly.

## Service Channels

The Academic Exchange Service Center also provides WeChat customer service and an official account for room reservations and inquiries. Users may scan the QR codes to add the customer service contact or follow the official account for more information.
