It is reported that the Engineering College Party Committee and Jingyi College organized the "Honest Breeze, Clean Heart" clean-themed calligraphy and painting works collection activity from April to May, aiming to deepen the "Clean Engineering" integrity culture brand and diversify integrity education. The event received an enthusiastic response from students.

Following anonymous expert review of entries based on creativity and originality, thematic relevance and value orientation, and artistic expression, the winners are as follows:

| Award | Winner | Work Title |
|-------|--------|------------|
| First Prize | Chen L | Choice and Confidence |
| Second Prize | Wang XQ | Studying Classics for Application, Reading with an Open Mind |
| Second Prize | Gan MY | Upholding Integrity and Honesty |
| Third Prize | Huang Y | Clean Hands, Integrity in Every Step |
| Third Prize | Li JJ | Cultivating Character through Integrity, Refining People through Culture |
| Third Prize | Wu XX | Lotus Breeze, Honest Fragrance |
