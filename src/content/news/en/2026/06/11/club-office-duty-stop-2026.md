Due to the approaching end of the semester, the Student Club Management Service Center offices (Sangpu Campus: Room 731, Building 789; Donghai Campus: Room 1007, Building A) will suspend duty services starting from Week 16 Monday, June 15. Services including material application approvals and poster stamping will be paused. Student organizations with urgent material needs should contact the center staff.

> Source: STU Youth League Committee & Student Club Management Service Center OA Notice
