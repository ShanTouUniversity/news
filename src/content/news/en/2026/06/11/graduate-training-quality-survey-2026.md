STU has joined the 2026 Graduate Training Quality Feedback Survey, an initiative of the Ministry of Education's Degree Management and Graduate Education Department, implemented by the Peking University China Doctoral Education Research Center. The university is required to complete the survey for all graduating graduate students by June 17.

The survey targets master's and doctoral students expected to graduate in June 2026 (including those with deferred graduation), as well as those who graduated in the first half of 2026. It covers full-time, part-time, Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan students, but excludes international students and those with military status.

The survey is conducted via the China Doctoral Education Data Research Center Platform using anonymous online questionnaires. The doctoral questionnaire is at https://cdercwj.zkey.cc/bsyEVt and the master's questionnaire at https://cdercwj.zkey.cc/zsgeaa.
