It is reported that the STU University Hospital will hold the second Red Cross CPR+AED first aid certification training session for the Shantangpu Campus on June 17, from 2:00 PM to 5:30 PM at Lecture Hall 2.

The program includes theoretical instruction, hands-on practice, and certification examination. Registered participants are asked to join the WeChat group by June 14 for detailed instructions.

Attendees should wear loose-fitting clothing and avoid accessories. Arrival 10 minutes early is recommended for check-in. Absences or incomplete attendance will result in no certificate issued.
