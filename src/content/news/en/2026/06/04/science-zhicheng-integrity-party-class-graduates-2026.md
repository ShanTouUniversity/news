It is reported that the School of Science and Zhicheng College jointly launched the counselor-led lecture series on integrity education for graduating student party members. Dubbed the "Final Party Class on Integrity Education," the initiative aims to embed the virtues of integrity, honest conduct, and pragmatic action into the formative journey of soon-to-be graduates, guiding them to establish sound values of integrity before entering society.

Themed "Keep Integrity with a Sincere Heart, Begin a New Journey with the Wind of Youth," the event comprises seven sessions scheduled between **June 8 and June 12, 2026**. It targets student party branch members (including probationary members), development candidates, active applicants for party membership, and members of student clubs and organizations.

#### Schedule

The seven sessions each spotlight an honest exemplar from a different era, weaving their life stories, acts of integrity, and dedication to the public into lessons tailored to graduates' job applications, workplace conduct, and personal development:

| No. | Topic | Speaker | Affiliation |
|:---:|:---|:---|:---|
| 1 | Jiao Yulu: Model of County Party Secretaries | Wang F. | Physics Graduate Party Branch |
| 2 | Yang Shanzhou: Lifelong Service to People, Lifelong Selflessness | Fan Y. | Biology Undergraduate Party Branch |
| 3 | Gu Wenchang: Immortal Monument | Ding Y. | Bio-Marine Graduate Party Branch (1st) |
| 4 | Kong Fanseng: The Highest Love is for the People | Lin J. | Bio-Marine Graduate Party Branch (2nd) |
| 5 | Yuan Longping: Be a Good Seed | Yan D. | Physics Undergraduate Party Branch |
| 6 | Wang Ying: Everlasting Bayan Red Leaf | Guo L. | Bio-Marine Graduate Party Branch (3rd) |
| 7 | Liao Junbo: The Woodsman Who Gave His All | She M. | Bio-Marine Graduate Party Branch (4th) |

Through these sessions, student party members are encouraged to draw lessons from the exemplars' noble character, reflect on their own conduct, and build a solid ideological defense against misconduct before stepping into the workforce.

#### Requirements

The organizers have outlined three requirements: first, **attend seriously and observe discipline**—branches and organizations must ensure timely attendance, active listening, and note-taking, with zero tolerance for unexcused absences, tardiness, or lax classroom behavior; second, **deepen reflection**—participants should discuss and relate the exemplars' stories to their own academic experiences and career plans, identify personal shortcomings, and internalize the values of honest living and professional integrity; third, **compile materials promptly**—each hosting branch must gather event photos, learning summaries, and student reflections after each session and submit them to the college Youth League committee email by **June 15**.
