STU's Law College and Zhicheng College have launched the 24th session of the "Care for Tomorrow, Law First" legal education outreach campaign. The event will take place at **Pengou Middle School in Shantou** on **Thursday, June 11, 2026, at 15:00**, targeting all first-year middle school students.

To advance the cultivation of law-aware, law-study-oriented, law-understanding, law-abiding, and law-utilizing youth, the organizers have assembled a team of graduate students from the Law College's Department of Law. The outreach will cover juvenile delinquency prevention, campus bullying prevention, everyday legal knowledge, and protection of minors' rights and interests. Through accessible explanations and interactive engagement, the program aims to build a solid foundation of legal awareness among students as they grow.

The event is hosted by the Law College and Zhicheng College and organized by the First Graduate Party Branch of the Law College.
