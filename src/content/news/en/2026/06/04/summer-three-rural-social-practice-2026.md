The Communist Youth League Shantou University Committee has issued a notice forwarding the joint announcement from the Provincial Youth League Committee, Provincial Party Committee Publicity Department, Provincial Civilization Office, Provincial Education Department, and Student Federation regarding the **2026 Guangdong Summer Rural Culture, Technology, and Health "Three Rural Areas" Social Practice Activities and Provincial Youth University Students "Hundreds and Thousands" Project Youth Volunteer Team Action**.

All university-level Youth League branches and directly affiliated Youth League branches are required to complete organizational mobilization by **June 15, 2026**.

#### Submission Requirements

Each college Youth League branch must package and submit the following two sets of documents to siyichen@stu.edu.cn by June 15:

- **Attachment 4**: Selection and Assignment Form for the 2026 Guangdong Youth University Students "Hundreds and Thousands" Project Youth Volunteer Team Action (for universities) — both an editable version and a stamped/scanned copy are required.
- **Attachment 5**: Summary Data Table for 2026 College Youth League Branch Reports on "Hundreds and Thousands" Project Youth Volunteer Team Actions and Other Social Practices (for universities).

#### Contact

For inquiries, contact the STU Youth League Committee: Teacher Chen, Tel: 0754-86502256.
