With temperatures rising and rainfall increasing, mosquito activity has grown across campus. While the campus property management company has already stepped up routine mosquito control efforts, some areas still report disturbances. To reduce the impact on students and faculty, the Resource Management Office has launched a **targeted enhanced mosquito control initiative** on the Sangpu Mountain Campus.

## How to Report Pest Concerns

Students and staff experiencing mosquito problems should:

1. Download and complete the form: *Sangpu Mountain Campus Targeted Mosquito Control Registration Form*
2. Send the completed form to: **xywy@stu.edu.cn**

Once received, the property management company will dispatch professional pest control staff to assess the site, develop a targeted treatment plan, and conduct follow-up visits to track effectiveness until the issue is fully resolved.

For questions, contact the Resource Management Office.
