The cadre assessment panel of the School of Science announced that it will conduct an organizational assessment for **Li Shengkang** of the Ocean Institute on **June 5 2026**. The review follows the Party and government leading cadre selection and appointment regulations and incorporates feedback from the broader community.

## Assessment Details

During the assessment period, individuals and units may submit feedback regarding the assessee’s performance and background through mail, phone calls, or in-person visits.

## Feedback Guidelines

- Personal submissions: submitters are encouraged to sign or provide their real name.
- Unit submissions: the submission should carry the official seal of the unit.
- All feedback must be truthful and based on facts; defamation or false accusations are prohibited.
- The panel also welcomes supervision of its own work.
