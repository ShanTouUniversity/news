To ensure orderly and accurate processing of 2026 graduate personnel archives, the Admissions and Career Office is recruiting **12 temporary student workers** for archive-related tasks.

## Position Details

- **Positions**: 12
- **Period**: July 1 – July 24, 2026 (tentative)
- **Location**: Room 223, Administrative Center, Sangpu Mountain Campus
- **Pay**: Standard university work-study hourly rate, paid according to actual working hours

## Qualifications

1. Full-time undergraduate student with sufficient availability during summer; no schedule conflicts; able to serve for the full period and follow assignments.
2. Proficient in Office software, with basic data entry, information verification, and spreadsheet organization skills.
3. Detail-oriented, responsible, able to handle repetitive fine-grained work, with good execution and teamwork skills.
4. Strict confidentiality;严禁私自查看、拍摄、传播或泄露毕业生档案及相关涉密信息。
5. Able to follow unified arrangements from supervisors and cooperate with the team.

## Key Responsibilities

- Receive, count, verify, sort, organize, and bind graduate archives; check material completeness and standardization.
- Cross-check archives with student records; perform system data entry, correction, and updates.
- Maintain and update archive management records, track archive status, organize working forms and documents.
- Assist with archive packaging, verification, packing, registration, mailing, and other related tasks.
- Assist with storage room organization, document copying, and other daily tasks as assigned.

## Application

Submit your application by **24:00 on June 11 2026** to: o_bys@stu.edu.cn

- **Resume naming**: `Level+ Major + Name`
- **Email subject**: `应聘档案临时工作人员`

The office will conduct initial review. Results will be sent back via the application email by **June 15**.
