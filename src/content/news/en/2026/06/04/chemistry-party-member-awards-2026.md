The Party Committee of STU's Chemistry and Chemical Engineering College (also known as the Carbon Neutrality Future Technology College) has launched the **2026 selection of "Excellent Communist Party Members" and "Excellent Party Affairs Workers."** The initiative aims to thoroughly study and implement Xi Jinping's thought, motivate Party organizations and members at all levels, and establish a campus-wide culture of learning from, honoring, and surpassing advanced role models. Through this recognition, the college seeks to showcase the exemplary conduct and spirit of outstanding members, setting a benchmark for others and encouraging Party members to support the university's high-quality development.

#### Selection Scope and Quotas

Up to **15%** of each branch's Party members may be selected as "Excellent Communist Party Members," and each branch may recommend **one "Excellent Party Affairs Worker."** Recipients of these honors within the past two years are generally not eligible for re-nomination.

#### Eligibility

**Excellent Communist Party Members** must be formal CPC members. In addition to core political criteria, candidates must meet:
- Undergraduate Party members: at least one comprehensive scholarship in the past two years;
- Graduate Party members: at least one second-class academic scholarship in the past two years.

**Excellent Party Affairs Workers** must meet the above conditions for Excellent Communist Party Members, and additionally demonstrate dedication to Party affairs work, pragmatism, innovation, solid professional knowledge, and significant achievements in Party building; they must also enjoy high prestige among Party members and the broader community.

#### Selection Process

The process follows a bottom-up approach: **Nomination** → **Review** → **Verification** → **Public Notice** (5 working days) → **Decision**. The College Party Committee will convene a meeting to determine the final award recipients and report the results to the University Party Committee Organization Department for the record.

#### Submission Requirements

Each Party branch must submit the nomination forms and summary tables by **June 9** to: o_chemtw@stu.edu.cn.
