From May 8 to May 28 2026, the library ran a 21-day check-in challenge in partnership with Superstar Company under the theme “Embrace AI, Inspire the Future — One Lecture Every Day for 30 Minutes.” After the event window closed, the system applied preset rules to conduct an automatic prize draw. Sixteen readers were selected as winners.

## Prize Collection

Winners should collect their prizes in person by **June 19, 2026**:

- **Hours**: Monday–Friday, 08:30–11:30 and 15:00–17:00
- **Office**: Subject Services Department, Library (inside the 2F information desk), Sangpu Mountain Campus
- **Requirements**: Present your campus card and have your phone number verified
- **Proxy collection**: Email the contact in advance if someone else will collect on your behalf

For inquiries:

- **Phone**: 0754-8650 3247
- **Email**: zhzeng@stu.edu.cn
