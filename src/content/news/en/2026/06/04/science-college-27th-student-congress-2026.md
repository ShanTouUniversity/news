The **27th Student Congress** of STU's School of Science is scheduled to convene at **15:00 on Saturday, June 6, 2026**, in **Room 102, Building E, Sangpushan Campus**, with the approval of the college's Party Committee.

The congress will: hear and review the work reports of the 26th Student Council and its standing committee; elect the 27th Student Congress standing committee and the 27th Student Union presidium; gather student feedback on university operations; protect student rights and interests; and deliberate on other major matters within the congress's purview.

#### Delegates

The congress will have **49 formal delegates**, representing approximately **3%** of the college's full-time undergraduate population. Delegates must be full-time undergraduates in the School of Science, abide by laws, regulations, and university rules, possess solid political and moral qualities, and be capable of genuinely reflecting and expressing student interests. Delegates are recommended by their respective class youth league branches.

The college hopes to use this opportunity to widely carry out publicity and educational activities, mobilizing all students to contribute concretely to the college's development.
