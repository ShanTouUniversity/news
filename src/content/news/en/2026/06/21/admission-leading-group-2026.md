In accordance with Guangdong Provincial Admissions Committee requirements, STU has established the 2026 Undergraduate Admission Leading Group. The group is chaired by Chen Min, with members including Su Junzhi, Lin Shihong, Qiu Dongyang, Xu Fei, and Wu Yingfan.

The group will be dissolved upon completion of the 2026 admission work.
