It is reported that CILL (Center for Independent Language Learning) at the College of Liberal Arts is offering special summer borrowing. Books checked out after June 22 do not need to be returned until September 10, after the summer break. Students may borrow up to 5 books at a time.

> Source: STU OA Notice (College of Liberal Arts)
