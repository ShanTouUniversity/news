It is reported that the College of Engineering and the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Manufacturing Technology will host a graduate research sharing session as part of the Intelligent Manufacturing academic forum series.

## Event Details

- **Time**: July 1, 2026 (Wednesday), 15:00
- **Venue**: Science & Technology Building Room 521, Sangpushan Campus

## Presenters and Topics

| Presenter | Supervisor | Topic |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Qian ZH | Prof. Hao Zhifeng | Causal Discovery with Linear Partially Non-Gaussian Measurement Error Model |
| Wang QS | Prof. Zhang Jian | High-Order Reconfigurable Design of Wind Turbine Assembly Lines |
| Ding CF | Prof. Ling Siying | Sub-Micron Precision Involute Measurement and Manufacturing Process |

Each presentation lasts 15 minutes followed by a 10-minute discussion. The session counts toward graduate lecture attendance records. Interested students should register via QR code by 17:00 on June 30.
