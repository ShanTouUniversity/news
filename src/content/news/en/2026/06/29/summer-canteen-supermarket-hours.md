It is reported that the operating hours of canteens and supermarkets on both campuses have been adjusted for the 2026 summer vacation.

## Sangpushan Campus

**Normal operation**: 2nd Canteen, West Garden Restaurant, Fanke Supermarket

**Closed** (July 4 – September 4): 3rd Canteen, 4th Canteen, Huayuan Restaurant, Northwest Dining Room

**Xiyangyang Supermarket**: Closed from August 21, reopening TBD

## East Coast Campus

**Normal operation**: 1st Canteen, Muxicheng Supermarket (24hr self-service)

**Closed** (July 4 – September 4): 2nd Canteen, 3rd Canteen

> Source: STU OA Notice (Resource Management Office, East Coast Campus Management Committee)
