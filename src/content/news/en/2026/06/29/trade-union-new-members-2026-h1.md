It is reported that following individual applications and approval by departmental and university trade unions, 44 faculty and staff members including Wang Haoyu have joined the STU Education Trade Union from January to June 2026.

New members are encouraged to fulfill their union responsibilities, actively participate in union activities, and contribute to building a harmonious campus. The full list is available in the attachment.
