It is reported that the College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering will hold the 2025 Sendeli Chemical Scholarship award ceremony to recognize outstanding students and promote academic excellence. The scholarship donor and outstanding alumnus, Mr. Chen Zhaohan, General Manager of Guangdong Sendeli Environmental New Material Technology Co., Ltd., will attend to present the awards.

## Event Details

- **Time**: June 30, 2026, 11:00-11:30
- **Venue**: Library 30A, Sangpushan Campus

## Agenda

1. Award ceremony
2. Thank-you letter presentation to Chen Zhaohan
3. Alumni message from Chen Zhaohan
4. Group photo
