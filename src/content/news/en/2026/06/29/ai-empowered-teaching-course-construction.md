It is reported that the College of Arts has established an "AI-Empowered Education and Teaching Course Construction Project" to accelerate the innovative application of digital and AI technologies in education.

## Project Details

Ten courses will be selected, with funding levels:

| Course Level | Funding |
|-------------|---------|
| Regular course | 8,000 yuan |
| University-level first-class course | 10,000 yuan |
| Provincial-level first-class course | 12,000 yuan |
| National-level first-class course | 14,000 yuan |

The project spans 2 years, with funds disbursed in two installments.

## Application

Faculty should submit their project proposals to **xxhuang@stu.edu.cn** by **17:00, September 25, 2026**.
