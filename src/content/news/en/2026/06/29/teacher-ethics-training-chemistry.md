It is reported that the College of Chemistry will hold a teacher ethics education themed training session on June 30, 2026, aimed at improving teaching capabilities and advancing faculty development.

## Event Details

- **Time**: June 30, 2026, 9:00
- **Venue**: Library 30A, Sangpushan Campus
- **Audience**: All faculty of the College of Chemistry

## Agenda

1. First-class course experience sharing
2. Study of the *Ten Behavioral Guidelines for University Faculty in the New Era*
3. Teaching management norms training
4. Interactive discussion

Other interested faculty are also welcome.
