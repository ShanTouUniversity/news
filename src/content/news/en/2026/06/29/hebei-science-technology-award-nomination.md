It is reported that the Research Office has announced a nomination for the 2026 Hebei Provincial Science and Technology Award (Natural Science Award), in accordance with the Hebei Provincial Department of Science and Technology's nomination guidelines.

The nominated project is titled "Controlled Construction of Functional Biochar and Its Interfacial Adsorption Regulation Mechanism for Environmental Pollutants." STU faculty member Song Zhengguo is listed as the 2nd completer.

The public notice period is **June 29 to July 5, 2026**. Objections should be submitted in writing to the Research Office. Contact: Zheng Xiangli, 0754-86502583, o_kyc@stu.edu.cn.
