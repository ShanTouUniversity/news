It is reported that the College of Chemistry has invited outstanding alumnus Mr. Chen Zhaohan, General Manager of Guangdong Sendeli Environmental New Material Technology Co., Ltd., to share his experience on industry-academia-research transformation and university-enterprise cooperation with faculty who joined in the past five years.

## Event Details

- **Time**: June 30, 2026, 10:00
- **Venue**: Library 30A, Sangpushan Campus
- **Content**: Experience sharing on industry-academia-research transformation and interactive discussion

Other interested faculty are also welcome to attend.
