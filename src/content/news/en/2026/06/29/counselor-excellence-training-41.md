It is reported that the Student Affairs Department will hold the 3rd College Counselor Mental Health Education Training, also the 41st Counselor Excellence Program, on July 2-3, 2026.

## Schedule

**July 2** (Shude College 2F Multi-Function Hall)
- 14:15-15:45 Identification and Intervention of Common Mental Disorders (Prof. Zhou Liang, Guangzhou Medical University)
- 16:30-17:30 Themed Group Discussion

**July 3** (Medical College Teaching Center, Rooms 311S-314S)
- 9:00-11:20 Group Case Study and Assessment
- 11:20-12:00 Graduation Ceremony

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office, Mental Health Education and Counseling Center)
