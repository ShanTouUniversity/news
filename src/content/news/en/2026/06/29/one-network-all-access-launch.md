It is reported that the STU "One-Network All-Access" (一网通办) platform officially went live at 8:00 AM on June 29, 2026, as part of the university's digital transformation strategy to streamline campus service workflows.

The first phase covers eight categories: supervision, administration, student affairs, research management, finance and procurement, personnel affairs, logistics, and IT services.

## Access

**Web**: [https://service.stu.edu.cn](https://service.stu.edu.cn)

**Mobile**: Via the STU WeCom (Enterprise WeChat) workspace (recommended), or through the "STU Service Account" WeChat Official Account menu.

For issues or feedback, contact oa@stu.edu.cn or call 0754-86503401.
