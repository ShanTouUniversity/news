It is reported that the Student Affairs Department will hold the 42nd Counselor Excellence Training Program, featuring Professor Huang Jianrong from the School of Marxism at South China University of Technology.

## Training Details

- **Topic**: From "Investing in People" to "Cultivating Souls" — The Mission, Curriculum Wisdom, and Art of Action for Mental Health Educators in the New Era
- **Time**: July 3, 2026, 15:00
- **Venue**: International Exchange Center (AH) 1F Multi-Function Hall
- **Audience**: All counselors

## About the Speaker

Professor Huang Jianrong is a faculty member at SCUT's School of Marxism, a Guangdong "Excellent Southern Guangdong Teacher," and Chief Professor of the Mental Health Education and Counseling Center at SCUT. She specializes in crisis management, management communication, stress management, and positive psychology.
