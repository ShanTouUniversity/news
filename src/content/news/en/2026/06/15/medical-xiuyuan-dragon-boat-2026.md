It is reported that the Medical School and Xiuyuan College will host a Dragon Boat Festival themed event on June 19 from 10:00 to 12:00 at the Xiuyuan College Building E 1F Lobby, Sangpu Campus.

**Activities**

- **Zongzi Tasting**: Enjoy traditional Dragon Boat Festival zongzi
- **Wormwood Hammer**: Make traditional wormwood hammers for summer wellness
- **Sachet Crafting**: Create mosquito-repelling sachets
- **Clay Zongzi**: Sculpt miniature zongzi with creative clay
- **Bracelet Weaving**: Weave colorful silk bracelets for good fortune

Open to all Medical School undergraduates and Xiuyuan College residents. Register via QR code.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Medical School, Xiuyuan College)
