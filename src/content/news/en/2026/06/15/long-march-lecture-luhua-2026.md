It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee, together with the Shantou Youth League Committee and the School of Marxism, will host the 7th session of the "Long March Spirit" red宣讲 series, featuring Sulang Pengchu, the 4th generation guardian of the Luhua Conference site.

**Event Details**

- Time: June 17, 10:00–11:40
- Venue: East Coast Campus, E222
- Audience: Students taking Modern Chinese History and interested faculty/students

**About the Speaker**

Sulang Pengchu is the 4th generation guardian of the Luhua Conference site, director of the Red Study Base, Party branch secretary, and a military veteran. He has dedicated years to preserving the red cultural resources of the Luhua Conference and telling revolutionary stories to young people.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Shantou Youth League Committee, STU Youth League Committee, School of Marxism)
