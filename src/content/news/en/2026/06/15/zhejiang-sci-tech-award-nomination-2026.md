It is reported that a project led by Professor Yu Ying (with STU as both the completing and working unit) titled "Key Technologies for Intelligent Detection and Toughness Enhancement of Connection Performance in Existing Steel Structures" has been nominated for the 2025 Zhejiang Science and Technology Progress Award. The public notice period runs from June 15 to 21.

Objections may be submitted in writing to the Research Office. Contact: Zheng Xiangli, 0754-86502583, o_kyc@stu.edu.cn.
