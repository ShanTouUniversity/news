It is reported that the "Shantou University Budget Management Measures" have been formally issued, following approval at the 14th President's Office Meeting and the 14th meeting of the University Party Standing Committee on June 3. The measures regulate budget preparation, review, execution, and final accounting.

The previous "Shantou University Budget Management Measures (Trial)" (STU Issue [2017] No. 161) has been simultaneously repealed.
