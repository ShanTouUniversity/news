It is reported that the MOE Social Science Department has launched the 2026 special task project for university counselor research. Each project receives 20,000 yuan with a 2-year research period.

Only full-time university counselors are eligible. Each university may submit up to 2 projects.

**STU Timeline**

- Jun 22: Submit application for format review to o_wkb@stu.edu.cn
- Jul 2: Submit final version
