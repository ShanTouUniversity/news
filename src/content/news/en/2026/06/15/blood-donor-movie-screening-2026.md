It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee and Young Volunteers Association will organize a free movie screening of *A Love Letter to Grandma* for blood donors on June 20 at 15:00 at Wanda Cinema, themed "Light and Shadow Reflect the Heart, Passion Dedicated to Youth."

The film revolves around "Qiaopi" (overseas Chinese remittance letters), telling a heartfelt story of Chaoshan people's overseas journey.

Participants with prior blood donation experience have priority. Registration closes June 19 at 12:00 via QR code. No transportation provided.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Youth League Committee, STU Young Volunteers Association)
