It is reported that the Institute of Mathematics will host the 952nd session of the "Towards Modern Mathematics" academic lecture series on June 17 at 11:00 in East Coast Campus D-209, featuring Professor Deng Yinbin from Central China Normal University.

**Lecture Details**

- Topic: Positive Solutions for Critical Elliptic Equation with Critical Neumann Boundary Condition
- Time: June 17, 11:00
- Venue: East Coast Campus, D-209
- Invited by: Professor Tan Zhong

**About the Speaker**

Deng Yinbin is a "Boyan Scholar" distinguished professor and doctoral supervisor at Central China Normal University, specializing in nonlinear partial differential equations and their applications. He has led multiple NSFC key and general projects and published over 100 SCI-indexed papers. His research has earned him the Second Prize of the Ministry of Education Natural Science Award and the First Prize of Hubei Natural Science Award.
