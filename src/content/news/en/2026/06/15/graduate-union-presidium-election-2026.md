It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee, Graduate Work Department, and Graduate Student Union have jointly launched the selection of candidates for the 19th Graduate Student Union Presidium. Five presidium members will be elected under a rotating executive chair system.

**Requirements**

- Full-time graduate student with at least one year of student leadership experience
- Party or League member
- No failing grades, no disciplinary record

**Process**

College团委 recommend candidates. The university-level团委 will review and interview candidates, selecting up to 6 candidates who will then be confirmed by the university Party Committee and elected at the 19th Graduate Student Congress.

Recommendation materials must be submitted to o_tw@stu.edu.cn by June 24 at 12:00, with a copy to stupgu@stu.edu.cn.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Youth League Committee, Graduate Work Department, STU Graduate Student Union)
