It is reported that the MOE Social Science Department has launched the 2026 special research project for university ideological and political theory course teachers, covering 5 categories:

- Xi Jinping Thought Course Research: ≤100,000 yuan (~20 projects)
- Teaching Reform Project: ≤100,000 yuan (~50 projects)
- Youth Research Project: ≤80,000 yuan (~100 projects)
- Teaching Case Study Project: ≤100,000 yuan (~20 projects)
- Classic Works Study Project: ≤100,000 yuan (~20 projects)

All with a 2-year research period.

**Requirements**

- Full-time ideological course teachers with teaching evaluation ranking in the top 30%
- Youth project applicants must be under 35 (born after July 12, 1991)
- Course research and case study project applicants must be award winners in teaching competitions
