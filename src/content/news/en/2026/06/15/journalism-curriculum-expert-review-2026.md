It is reported that the Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication held an expert consultation meeting on the evening of June 15 for its 2026 undergraduate talent cultivation plan, with two external experts reviewing the Journalism and Network & New Media programs.

**External Experts**

- Liu Tao: Executive Dean of Undergraduate School and Dean of Academic Affairs at Jinan University, Changjiang Young Scholar, National Ten Thousand Talents Plan leader
- Li Huajun: Dean of the School of Journalism and Communication at HUST, National Ten Thousand Talents Plan Young Top Talent

The meeting was held via Tencent Meeting to optimize the talent cultivation framework and align with media industry demands.
