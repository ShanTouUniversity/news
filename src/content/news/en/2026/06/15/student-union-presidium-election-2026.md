It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee and Student Union have launched the selection of candidates for the 29th Student Union Presidium. Five presidium members will be elected under a rotating executive chair system.

**Requirements**

- Full-time undergraduate with at least one year of student leadership experience
- Party or League member
- Academic ranking within top 30% of major, no failing grades
- No disciplinary record

**Process**

College团委 recommend candidates. The university-level团委 will review and interview candidates, selecting up to 6 candidates who will then be confirmed by the university Party Committee. The final 5 members will be elected at the 29th Student Congress planned for October.

Recommendation materials must be submitted to o_tw@stu.edu.cn by June 24 at 12:00.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Youth League Committee, STU Student Union)
