It is reported that the national first-class course "Toward the Ocean" has opened recruitment for the 2026-2027 fall semester, offering three classes of 40 students each.

**Course Content**

Students will explore the Su'ai Bay mangrove forest, Mayu Island biodiversity, Nan'ao Island, and Fujian's Dongshan Island, conducting marine ecology surveys, beach cleanups, and fisherman education.

**Schedule Options**

- Sangpu Campus: Wednesday evening lectures (5 sessions) + 5-day fieldwork
- East Coast Campus: Wednesday evening lectures (5 sessions) + 5-day fieldwork
- Practice Week: September 7–19 (Sangpu Campus)

Registration opens June 15 at 12:00 via the "走向海洋" WeChat account. Successful completion earns 2 college practice credits.
