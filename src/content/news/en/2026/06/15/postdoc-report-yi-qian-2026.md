It is reported that the Mathematics Postdoctoral Research Station will hold Dr. Yi Qian's postdoctoral exit report on June 18 at 9:30 at East Coast Campus D-209.

**Abstract**

The report addresses numerical models for two types of nonlocal PDEs: a fourth-order compact difference scheme for fractional convection equations with second-order in time and fourth-order in space accuracy, and a time-splitting Fourier pseudospectral method for the Wigner(-Poisson)-Fokker-Planck equation achieving spectral accuracy in space.

**About the Speaker**

Dr. Yi Qian graduated from Shanghai University and currently works at Guangxi Normal University. She began her postdoctoral research at the Department of Mathematics in August 2024, supervised by Professor Wen Zhitao.
