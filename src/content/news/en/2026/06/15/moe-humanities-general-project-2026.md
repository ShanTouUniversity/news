It is reported that the MOE Social Science Department has opened applications for the 2026 Humanities and Social Sciences General Projects, covering 29 disciplines including Marxist theory, law, and education.

**Categories and Funding**

- Planning Fund: up to 100,000 yuan
- Youth Fund: up to 80,000 yuan
- Special task projects (separate notices)

All projects have a 3-year research period.

**Requirements**

- Planning Fund: senior title or PhD
- Youth Fund: under 40 (born after July 12, 1986)
- Not eligible if currently leading an active MOE, NSFC, or NSSFC project

**STU Timeline**

- Jun 24: Submit college evaluation list to o_wkb@stu.edu.cn
- Jun 29: Format review
- Jun 30: Upload to application system
