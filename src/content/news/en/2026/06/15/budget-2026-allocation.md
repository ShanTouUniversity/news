It is reported that the 2026 budget for the main campus has been approved at the 15th President's Office Meeting and the 15th meeting of the University Party Standing Committee on June 10, and has been formally allocated.

The notice recommends that teaching units allocate 50% or more of their routine funds to departments and teaching and research sections for instructional activities. All units are required to comply with financial regulations, arrange expenditures reasonably, practice economy, and improve fund utilization efficiency.
