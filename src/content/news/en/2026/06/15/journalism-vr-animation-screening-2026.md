It is reported that the Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication will host a screening of final works from the Virtual Reality Animation course on June 18 from 9:55 to 11:40 at East Coast Campus E133.

Over three months, students explored 3D modeling, virtual scene construction, and dynamic narrative through VR animation technology. Register via the "到梦空间" APP for activity credits.
