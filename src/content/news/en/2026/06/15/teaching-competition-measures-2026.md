It is reported that the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment has drafted the Implementation Measures for STU Teacher Teaching Competitions, aiming to improve the competition management system and enhance teaching quality.

Comments and suggestions can be submitted to o_fdea@stu.edu.cn by June 23.
