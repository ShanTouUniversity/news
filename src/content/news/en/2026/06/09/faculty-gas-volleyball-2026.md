It is reported that the STU Labor Union will host the 2026 Faculty Gas Volleyball Competition starting June 15. Matches will be held at the Sports Park Gas Volleyball Court on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays at 5:10 PM. Teams that fail to arrive by 5:20 PM will forfeit.

Eight teams will compete: the Physics-Chemistry United Team, Math-Computer Team, Engineering Team, Admin Team 1, Admin Team 2, Liberal Arts-Art United Team, Law-Business-Journalism-Public Health-Marxism United Team, and the Physical Education Department Team (excluding professional volleyball instructors). The match schedule will be announced separately.
