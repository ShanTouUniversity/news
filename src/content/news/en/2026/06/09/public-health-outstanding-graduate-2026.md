It is reported that, in accordance with the university's Outstanding Graduate selection process, the School of Public Health has recommended student Zhang LR, a master's student in Public Health, as a 2026 Outstanding Graduate of Shantou University.

The public comment period runs until 16:30 on June 10. Units or individuals with objections may submit written comments to the school. Units must affix their official seal; individuals must provide their real name.
