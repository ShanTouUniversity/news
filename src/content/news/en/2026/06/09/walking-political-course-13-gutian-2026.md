It is reported that, to strengthen Party spirit education for graduating Party members before they leave the university, the College of Science and Zhicheng College will hold the 13th session of their "Walking Ideological and Political Course," leading all 2026 graduating Party members on an educational trip to the Changting and Gutian red education bases in western Fujian from June 17 to 18.

Under the theme "Inherit Red Genes, Build a Solid Value Foundation," the trip will take graduating Party members to visit key revolutionary sites including Zhongfu Village, Songmao Ridge, the Red Army Bridge, Red Army Street, the Guanshougong Ancestral Hall Command Memorial, and the former site of the Fujian Soviet Government, where they will learn about the Songmao Ridge blockade battle and the history of the Red Ninth Army Corps' Long March departure. Participants will also visit the Gutian Conference site to study the core principles of the Gutian Conference Resolution, gaining a deeper understanding of the spirit of "building the Party ideologically and building the army politically."

Transportation, meals, and accommodation will be arranged by the college. All participants are required to wear their Party badges and strictly follow organizational discipline.
