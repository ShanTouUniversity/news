It is reported that, in accordance with the STU Undergraduate Teaching Supervision Measures, the university's Undergraduate Teaching Supervision Group will hold the 2025–2026 academic year teaching supervision work meeting on June 17, aiming to strengthen the supervisory role of teaching oversight in undergraduate education and enhance the quality assurance system.

The meeting is scheduled for 15:00 on Wednesday, June 17, at Room E401, East Wing, Administration Building, Sangpushan Campus. Attendees include all members of the university's Undergraduate Teaching Supervision Group, at least one representative each from the Academic Affairs Office and the Student Affairs Office, and at least one representative from each teaching unit's supervision subgroup.

All units must submit their attendee list via email to o_fdea@stu.edu.cn by 17:00 on Monday, June 15. Any changes to supervision group membership should be accompanied by an updated member information form for record purposes.
