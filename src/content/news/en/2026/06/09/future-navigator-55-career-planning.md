It is reported that, to help students establish clear career goals and master practical job-seeking skills, the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College will co-host the Future Navigator Plan (Session 55): Career Planning and Job-Seeking Skills lecture.

The event will be held at 15:00 on Wednesday, June 10, in Room 303, Building E, Sangpushan Campus, and is open to all students of the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College. Interested students should check class notices for registration; registered students may also log into the "Daomeng Kongjian" APP to earn second classroom credits.

The lecture features alumnus Peng Ling as the keynote speaker. Peng Ling, an outstanding graduate of STU's Applied Physics program, holds a Master of Management from Renmin University of China. He currently serves as Dean of the Chain Talent Research Institute and is a nationally certified career guidance counselor and an ICF-certified coach.
