It is reported that, following an open application and comprehensive evaluation process, STU has announced the proposed recipients of the 2025 Outstanding Correspondent, Outstanding Communication Unit, and Good News awards.

The 10 outstanding correspondents are: Yu Yijing, Huang Jiaman, Zhang Xiao, Lin Zefeng, Wang Jin, Zhang Minxuan, Cai Lin, Huang Zhiqi, Lu Zhishuai, and Yang Xueyu. The 10 outstanding communication units are: College of Engineering, College of Science, STU Youth League Committee, First Affiliated Hospital of STU Medical College, Second Affiliated Hospital of STU Medical College, Business School, School of Mathematics and Computer Science, School of Law, International College, and the Teacher Development and Education Assessment Center. A total of 25 news reports received the "Good News" award (see appendix for details).

The public comment period runs from June 9 to June 16. Units or individuals with objections may submit written comments or emails to the Publicity and United Front Work Department. Contacts: Ms. Ye and Mr. Li, phone: 86502367, 86502368, email: o_pressoffice@stu.edu.cn.
