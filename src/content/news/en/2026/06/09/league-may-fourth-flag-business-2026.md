It is reported that, in accordance with the Guangdong Provincial Youth League Committee's notice on the 2026 "Two Reds and Two Excellences" selection and commendation work, the STU Business School Youth League Committee has been selected as the university's recommended candidate for the "Guangdong Provincial May Fourth Red Flag Youth League Committee" award, following collective applications, college recommendations, and review by the STU Youth League Standing Committee.

The public comment period runs until 12:00 PM on June 15, 2026. During this period, units or individuals with objections may submit written comments, visit in person, or call the STU Youth League Committee. Units must affix their official seal; individuals must provide their real name.
