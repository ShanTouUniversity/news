It is reported that the 2026 Guangdong University Table Tennis Team Competition was held in Foshan from June 4 to 7. Led by coach Du Tong, the STU women's team — composed of Liu YW, Zhou YQ, Zhu MY, Qiu JL, and Ye YQ — claimed the runner-up title in Women's Group A, marking the best result in the university's history in this competition.

The team members fought hard with solid skills and tenacious determination, achieving a historic breakthrough for Shantou University.
