It is reported that the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design and Dexin College will host the 20th session of the "Meeting with the Mentor" series, featuring Associate Professor Ma Tao, Director of the Public Art Program, who will deliver a talk titled "From David to Lamborghini: A Casual Talk on Italian Culture."

The event will be held at 19:20 on Thursday, June 11, in Room 109, Building E, East Coast Campus, and is open to students of the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design and Dexin College.

Ma Tao is an Associate Professor in Public Art, holding a PhD in Art Education and serving as a master's supervisor. His research interests include public art education, space and form design, urban sculpture, and art aesthetic education.
