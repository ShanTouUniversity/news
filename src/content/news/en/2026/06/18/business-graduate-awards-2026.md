It is reported that the Business School launched the "Outstanding Student Leader Dedication Award" and "Outstanding Volunteer Service Dedication Award" for graduating undergraduate students. The Outstanding Student Leader Award recognizes those who have served fellow students with dedication, while the Volunteer Service Award honors those with exceptional volunteer contributions. Applications closed on June 21.
