It is reported that the East Coast Campus Management Committee has published the June-July utility billing schedule for student dormitories. First-batch graduates will pay an advance of RMB 65, while second-batch graduates pay RMB 100, with actual amounts settled after departure. Non-graduates pay as billed. Students can check and pay via the utility management system.

> Source: STU OA Notice (East Coast Campus Management Committee)
