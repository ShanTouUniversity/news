It is reported that the library has extended the 24-hour opening of the Sangpushan Campus first-floor semi-open terrace reading area from the original July 3 to July 18 at 8:00 AM, to accommodate medical students' final exam needs. All STU students may use the facility. The East Coast Campus 24-hour study room (Building 1, Room 101) will revert to regular hours on July 3 as originally scheduled.
