The Party Committee of the College of Science has decided, at its 8th meeting in 2026, to appoint Li Shengkang as Director of the Institute of Marine Biology for a one-year probation period, and to relieve Wang Tieyu of the same position.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Party Committee of STU College of Science)
