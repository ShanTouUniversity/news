It is reported that the "Public Service Graduation Photo" course at the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism offered free cap-and-gown portrait sessions for 2026 graduates from June 24 to 26 at the East Coast Campus Photography Center. The service was available to all undergraduate, master's, and doctoral graduates.

Participants could bring personal items to include in their photos. On-site assistance with gown adjustments and touch-ups was provided, and each graduate received one free print and a photo frame.
