It is reported that the "Public Service Graduation Photo" course at the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism offered free cap-and-gown portrait sessions for 2026 graduates from June 24 to 26 at the East Coast Campus Photography Center in rooms E713 and E716 of the teaching building. The service was available to all undergraduate, master's, and doctoral graduates.

Participants could bring personal items to include in their photos. On-site assistance with gown adjustments and touch-ups was provided, and each graduate received one free print and a photo frame. On June 26 at noon, a special session was arranged for past course graduates from previous years.
