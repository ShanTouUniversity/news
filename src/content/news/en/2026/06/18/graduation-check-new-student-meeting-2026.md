It is reported that STU held a graduation ceremony work review meeting and a freshman orientation coordination meeting on June 23 at the Sangpushan Campus. The graduation ceremony meeting ran from 9:00-10:00 AM, followed by the orientation coordination meeting from 10:00-11:00 AM, attended by university leaders and representatives from all relevant departments and colleges.
