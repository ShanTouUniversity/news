It is reported that the Party Organization Department has published the results of the 2026 "Outstanding Party-Building Cases, Party Secretary Lectures, and Party Member Role Models" selection. Following nominations, expert review, and departmental deliberation, 26 outstanding cases, 10 lectures, and 22 role models were selected. The public notification runs until 5:00 PM on June 26.
