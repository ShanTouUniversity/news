It is reported that STU held its 2026 Admissions Open Day on June 21. The Admissions and Employment Office issued advance tips recommending that faculty minimize driving to campus and that students avoid peak dining hours (suggesting meals before 11:00 AM). With heavy library traffic expected, students were advised to use other study rooms.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Admissions and Employment Office)
