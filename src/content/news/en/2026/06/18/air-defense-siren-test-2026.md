It is reported that Shantou city conducted an air defense siren test on June 21 at 10:15 AM, marking the anniversary of Shantou's fall. All siren points on Sangpushan Campus sounded simultaneously. The test included pre-warning, attack warning, and all-clear signals. Campus operations continued as normal during the test.
