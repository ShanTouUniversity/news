It is reported that the 2026 STU Graduation Ceremony and Awards Presentation was held at 9:00 AM on June 26 at the East Coast Campus indoor gymnasium. Alumni representatives, university administrators, college leaders, and faculty entered from 8:00 AM. Graduates from Sangpushan Campus took shuttle buses from Zhenli Square between 6:20-7:00 AM, while East Coast Campus graduates assembled at the southeast gate at 7:00 AM, led by college advisors.
