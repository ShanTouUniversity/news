It is reported that the Student Affairs Office, along with the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College, is hosting the "Poetry Education China" poetry explanation competition. The competition features explanation and speech categories.

The explanation category requires standard Chinese language covering classical poetry from textbooks. The speech category focuses on Chinese traditional culture, revolutionary culture, and advanced socialist culture. Participants must complete an online assessment by June 30, scoring 60 or above to qualify. The campus selection will be held in early July, and winners will record videos to advance to the provincial semi-finals.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office, College of Liberal Arts, Siyuan College)
