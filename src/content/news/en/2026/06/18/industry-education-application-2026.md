It is reported that the Academic Affairs Office has forwarded a notice from the Guangdong Provincial Education Department and three other departments regarding the first batch of provincial-level city-region industry-education consortia, industry-education integration communities, and open regional industry-education practice centers. Interested faculty should submit application materials to the Academic Affairs Office for review by June 25.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
