It is reported that the rehearsal for the 2026 Graduation Ceremony and Awards Presentation was held on June 25 at the East Coast Campus indoor gymnasium. The rehearsal ran in three sessions: 8:00-12:00 for sectional rehearsals, 14:00-16:00 for combined rehearsals, and 16:00-18:00 for the full dress rehearsal, with university leaders, college deans, honored guests, alumni representatives, performers, and relevant staff in attendance.

> Source: STU OA Notice (President's Office, Student Affairs Office)
