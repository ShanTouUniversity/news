It is reported that the 5th meeting of secondary Party organization secretaries was held on June 23 at the Joint STU-CUHK Shantou International Eye Center East Coast campus, combined with the 3rd working session on establishing a correct view of performance. The university Party secretary, discipline inspection secretary, and all secondary Party organization secretaries attended.
