It is reported that the Student Affairs Office has published the results for the 2025 academic year scholarships, with 50 students recommended for the Spring Fountain Scholarship and 40 students for the Tielin Charity Self-Improvement Scholarship. The public notification period ended on June 23.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office)
> ⚠️ The complete list is available on the OA system.
