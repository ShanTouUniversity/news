It is reported that the STU Digital Governance Ideological and Political Education Research Center has announced the results of its open project review. Following qualification review and expert evaluation, four projects were approved:

| Project Title | Level | Project Leader |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Research on the Synergistic Innovation of Ideological and Political Education and Social Governance in the AI Era | Key Project | Liu Yingbo |
| A Critical Study of Emotional Labor from a Marxist Perspective | Key Project | Zhang Di |
| Content Innovation and Form Expansion of Value Guidance in Moral Education Empowered by Digital Intelligence | General Project | Jiang Yangyang |
| Reshaping Urban Time: The Implementation and Abolition of the "Cherish Time" System in Shanghai's International Settlement in the Early Republic | Youth Project | Xu Wenxin |
