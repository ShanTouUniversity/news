It is reported that the Student Affairs Office, along with the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College, is hosting the "Recite China" classic recitation competition. Categories include university students (including postgraduates), international students, and faculty. Participants may enter individually or in teams (up to 20 people). Recitations must be in Mandarin, with musical accompaniment permitted.

Participants must complete an online assessment by June 30, scoring 60 or above to qualify. The campus selection will be held in early July.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office, College of Liberal Arts, Siyuan College)
