It is reported that the Law College is recruiting one research assistant.

## Requirements

Master's degree or above in public administration, sociology, political science, or related fields, with skills in survey research, data analysis, and academic writing.

## Responsibilities

Manage daily operations of regional and international studies workshops and teams, organize research projects, and write reports and papers.

## Compensation

6-month term, salary 3,000-4,000 yuan/month.

## Application

Send your resume and materials to **sli@stu.edu.cn** (subject: "Application for Law Research Assistant") by **July 7, 2026**. Contact: Ms. Li, 0754-86502586.
