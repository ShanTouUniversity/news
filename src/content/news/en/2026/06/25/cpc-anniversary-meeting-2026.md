STU will hold a conference on July 1 at 15:00 in the Sangpu Campus Library Lecture Hall to celebrate the 105th anniversary of the Communist Party of China and commend outstanding Party organizations and members.

Attendees include Party committee members, discipline committee members, Party secretaries at all levels, and award winners of various Party-building honors. Attendance lists must be submitted to the Party and Administrative Office by June 29 at 11:00. Party members should wear badges and arrive 15 minutes early.

Contact: Zhuang Shaoyang, 86502877, bgshw@stu.edu.cn.
