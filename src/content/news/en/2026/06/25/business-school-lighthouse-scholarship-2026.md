The Business School has announced the proposed recipients of the 2026 Lighthouse Torch Scholarship, with 10 students selected.

**First Prize (1)**: Tian EH (2022 International Economics & Trade)

**Second Prize (6)**: Zou LY (2022 Finance), Zhang JJ (2022 International Economics & Trade), Li WF (2022 Business Administration), Qian YH (2022 Finance), Hu BR (2022 Finance), Xu ZY (2022 Finance)

**Third Prize (3)**: Chen YL (2022 International Economics & Trade), Chen JX (2022 Accounting), Huang JY (2022 Accounting)

Objections may be raised in writing before June 27 at 17:00 to the Business School committee (East Coast Campus, Block D, Room 201).
