A project led by Professor Wang Yanning (with STU as both completing and working unit) on "Key Technologies for Safe Shield Tunneling and Intelligent Construction in Water-Rich Karst Areas" has been nominated for the 2025 Shanxi Science and Technology Progress Award. The public notice period runs from June 25 to July 1.

Objections may be submitted in writing to the Research Office. Contact: Zheng Xiangli, 0754-86502583, o_kyc@stu.edu.cn.
