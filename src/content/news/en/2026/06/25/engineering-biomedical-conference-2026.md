The College of Engineering will host the 2026 Biomedical Engineering Symposium from June 27 to 29 at STU, alongside the inauguration of a discipline advisory committee. Experts from universities and research institutes will discuss two main topics: refining research directions and applying for academic master's degree authorization, and revising the undergraduate curriculum and preparing for professional accreditation.

Schedule: June 27 check-in (Academic Exchange Center), June 28 full-day conference (Shude College, Sangpu Mountain Campus), June 29 morning expert consultation and departure.
