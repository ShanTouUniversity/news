Following recent organizational restructuring, the Resources Management Office has led a revision of the Subsidy Policy for STU Faculty and Staff Hospitalized with Critical Illnesses (formerly Document No. 13 of 2020). Feedback is now being solicited from all faculty, staff, and students.

Comments and suggestions can be submitted by July 2 via email to ybk@stu.edu.cn. Contact: Chen Yanyun, 86503144.
