The School of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering (College of Carbon Neutrality Future Technology) has invited Professor Luo Qiong from South China Normal University to give an academic lecture.

**Topic**: Physics-Driven Structure Prediction and Mechanism Analysis — From Metal Hydrides to 3D Genome

**Time**: June 25 (Thursday), 15:00

**Venue**: High-Level Lab Building B, 1st Floor Lecture Hall

**About the Speaker**: Professor Luo Qiong is a doctoral supervisor at SCNU, recognized as an Outstanding Young Teacher of Guangdong Province and a Hong Kong Scholar. Her research focuses on applying theoretical chemistry to life and materials science, including 3D chromatin structure and gene transcription regulation, and novel high-energy-density materials. She has led two NSFC projects and published over 60 papers in journals including PNAS, Nat Comm, JACS, and Angew.
