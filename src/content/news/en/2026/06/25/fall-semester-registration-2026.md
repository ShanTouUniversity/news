The Academic Affairs Office has announced the 2026 fall semester course registration and textbook ordering schedule. Registration is via the academic system (https://jw.stu.edu.cn) with no VPN required off-campus.

**Registration Timeline**

| Phase | Date | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Initial | Jun 29 10:00–Jun 30 17:00 | Over-capacity allowed, random removal |
| Secondary | Jul 2 15:00–Jul 3 17:00 | First-come, first-served |
| Retake | Sep 7 15:00–Sep 8 17:00 | Max 25 credits |
| Change requests | Sep 9–10 | Online applications |
| Adjustment | Sep 21 10:00–Sep 25 17:00 | Week 3 of semester |

**Key Points**

- Maximum 25 credits (including practice courses), minimum 10
- Retake registration via "Exam Registration → Grade Management" path
- Textbook ordering runs concurrently with initial and secondary phases (Jun 29–Jul 3)
- Ideological textbooks at list price; others at 78% discount
- Avoid duplicate ordering of previously purchased textbooks

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
