The scenic platform on the south side of Riyue Keng Reservoir on Sangpu Campus has fully reopened after renovation work addressed safety hazards caused by deteriorated wooden decking and railings. The repairs replaced the wooden deck with stone flooring and installed stainless steel glass railings.

Visitors are advised to watch their steps and avoid climbing slopes or railings.
