The Resources Management Office and the East Coast Campus Committee have reminded 2026 graduates to complete their water and electricity bill payments promptly. The first batch of graduates is required to settle bills by June 26–27.

Data shows that fewer than half of graduates had completed payment as of June 24. Graduates can connect to the campus network and scan a QR code via WeChat to access the utility management system and pay outstanding bills through the self-service option.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Resources Management Office, East Coast Campus Committee)
