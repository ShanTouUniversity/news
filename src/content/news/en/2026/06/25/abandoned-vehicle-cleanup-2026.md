The Security Office will conduct a centralized cleanup of abandoned non-motorized vehicles on both Sangpu and East Coast campuses.

**Schedule**

- Self-removal: Now to July 10
- Centralized removal: July 11 – Sep 10 (vehicles locked and stored)
- Claim period: Sep 11 – Oct 10 (Saturdays only)
- Disposal: From Oct 11, unclaimed vehicles will be disposed of

To claim a vehicle, bring valid ID and proof of purchase to the Security Office. Sangpu Campus: Gongnan 317; East Coast Campus: Admin Building 907.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Security Office)
