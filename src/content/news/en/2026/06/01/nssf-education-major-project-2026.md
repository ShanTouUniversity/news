## National Bidding Overview

Approved by the Ministry of Education Party Group, the 2026 NSSF Education Major Projects are open for national bidding:

- **Issuing body**: National Education Science Planning Leading Group Office (NESP Office)
- **Eligibility**: Universities, ministry-affiliated institutions, provincial-level research institutes, party schools, etc.
- **Quantity**: 22 bidding topics; generally one winning bid per topic
- **Funding**: **600,000 RMB per project**, with selective rolling funding after mid-term review
- **Duration**: 2–5 years (2–3 for applied research, up to 5 for basic research)

## Eligibility

**Chief experts** must hold a senior professional title or bureau-level position; those leading similar ongoing national-level projects are ineligible. One chief expert per team, one project per expert.

**Sub-topic leads** must hold an associate senior title or above, and may participate in only one bid. Current leaders of NSSF major projects or MOE key projects cannot serve as sub-topic leads.
