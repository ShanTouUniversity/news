## Overview

To help students prepare for final exams, the Library will open **24-hour reading areas** for current STU students.

## Open Areas & Hours

| Campus | Area | Hours |
|--------|------|-------|
| Sangpu Campus Library | 1F Half-Terrace Reading Area | 8:00 AM, June 2 – July 2, 2026, open 24 hours |
| Donghai'an Campus Library | Main Building 1F Room 101 (Self-Study) | 8:00 AM, June 2 – July 2, 2026, open 24 hours |

## Closing Time & Reminders

- Starting at **8:00 AM on July 3, 2026**, access permissions to the Sangpu half-terrace area will be revoked, and Room 101 on Donghai'an Campus will return to regular hours.
- Readers must **collect all personal belongings** from these areas **before July 3**. Items left behind will not be kept.
- The Library reminds readers to plan study and rest schedules reasonably.
