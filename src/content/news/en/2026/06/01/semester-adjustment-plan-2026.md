## Document Release

The **STU Semester Adjustment Plan** (Document No. 2026-129), approved at the 12th President's Office Meeting on May 13, 2026, has been formally issued for implementation across all units.

## Key Changes

As reflected in the recently published summer vacation schedule, the adjustment introduces a **Practice Week (short semester)** mechanism, dividing students into two tracks:

- **Students enrolled in practice-term courses**: Shortened summer break (July 18 – Sept 5), earlier fall semester start (registration Sept 6, classes Sept 7)
- **Other students**: Summer break on original schedule (July 4 – Sept 5), fall semester follows later (registration Sept 19, classes Sept 20)

The revised semester framework is being implemented concurrently for both incoming 2026 students and continuing cohorts.

> ⚠️ The full STU Semester Adjustment Plan is available as an attachment on the OA system.
