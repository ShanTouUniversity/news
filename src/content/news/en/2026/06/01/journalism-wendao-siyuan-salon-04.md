## Overview

The Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication's Digital Humanities and Smart Communication Joint Faculty-Student Party Branch is hosting the 4th session of the "**Wendao Siyuan**" (Seeking the Way, Thinking of the Source) Academic Salon, an initiative to integrate party building with academic development and foster faculty exchange.

## Session Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Speaker | **Dr. Zhou Siyu** |
| Topic | Experience and Strategies for Publishing in High-Level SSCI Journals |
| Time | **June 5, 2026 (Friday), 11:45 AM – 1:00 PM** |
| Venue | **Room C628, Donghai'an Campus** |

## Topics Covered

- Key aspects of publishing in high-level SSCI journals in humanities and social sciences: international journal ecosystem, major database usage, and journal preferences
- Targeted submission strategies and standardized academic writing requirements
- Using AI tools for topic discovery, manuscript polishing, and content refinement
- Theoretical innovation and integrating theory with empirical findings
- Practical experience and mindset management throughout the submission process

## Registration

Faculty interested in attending should register via the college faculty group. Deadline: **June 2, 2026, 2:00 PM**.
