## Overview

Lafang Cosmetics Co., Ltd. (Shanghai A-share: 603630) and STU's Cheung Kong School of Art and Design (CKAD) jointly launch the **"Herbal Paint Orient, Medicinal Fragrance Builds Beauty"** Oriental Aesthetic Creative Arts Festival, calling for student submissions on Chinese herbal medicine themes. Hosted by CKAD and Dexin College, the project is part of a university-industry research collaboration.

Winning works will be permanently displayed in the dedicated art exhibition area of Lafang's new headquarters, reflecting the brand's "Oriental Care, Heritage & Innovation" identity.

## Eligibility

Full-time STU **undergraduate and graduate students**; individual or team entries accepted.

## Four Categories

| Track | Theme | Spec | Highlights |
|-------|-------|------|------------|
| A | Oriental Scrolls (Creative Painting) | A3 (297×420 mm) | Any style — Chinese style, ink wash, abstract, textured, watercolor, oil, etc.; include 100–200 word statement |
| B | Plants as Poetry (Plant-Based Creative Painting) | A3 (297×420 mm) | **Must include real plant specimens**; durable, well-preserved, odor-free; label plant names and medicinal effects |
| C | Natural History (Plant Specimens) | Min 150×200 mm, 1–3 cm thick | Preserve roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruits via drying; label Chinese name, Latin name, medicinal use, date, creator |
| D | Cycle of Aesthetics (Art Installation) | Open | Use Lafang's empty bottles, packaging, and pump heads to create large displays, landscape pieces, interactive or functional installations; emphasize sustainability |

> The organizer provides herbal specimen materials for Tracks B and C, and installation materials for Track D. Selected Track A and B works will be framed by the organizer.

## Schedule

- **Registration**: June 6 – June 10, 2026
- **Submission**: June 10 – September 10, 2026

## Awards

Judged by a joint university–industry panel. All shortlisted entrants receive Lafang's official collection certificate.

| Award Tier | Quota |
|------------|-------|
| Grand — Gold Pistil | 1 |
| Grand — Silver Pistil | 2 |
| Grand — Bronze Pistil | 3 |
| Special — Master Painter / Shennong Naturalist / Cultural Heritage / Innovation / Spiritual Insight | 1 each |
| Merit | 10 |

Each tier comes with prize money, gifts, trophies, or certificates.

## Benefits

The organizer highlights three core benefits for participants:

- **Inclusion**: Official A-share company collection certificate; works become part of Lafang's brand cultural assets
- **Presence**: Awarded works permanently displayed at Lafang's new HQ space with the creator's signature retained
- **Growth**: Outstanding participants enter Lafang's talent reserve pool; top winners receive internship fast-track passes and campus recruitment final-round priority
