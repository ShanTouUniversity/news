## Overview

The STU Youth League Committee and Student Union, in partnership with Guangdong Swire Coca-Cola Huizhou Co., Ltd., are organizing the "**Enjoy Cola, Dream Forward**" career guidance activity to broaden students' career perspectives.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | **June 6, 2026 (Saturday), 8:10 AM – 9:40 PM** |
| Venue | **Guangdong Swire Coca-Cola Huizhou Co., Ltd.** |
| Capacity | 25 total (20 general Donghai'an students + 5 student leaders) |

## About the Company

Guangdong Swire Coca-Cola Huizhou is the only Coca-Cola bottling plant in eastern Guangdong, a nationally certified green factory (MIIT), and a benchmark for smart and green manufacturing within Swire Coca-Cola China. It operates multiple international advanced fully automated production lines covering five cities in eastern Guangdong and the Greater Bay Area.

## Program

1. **Smart production workshop tour**: Observe modern manufacturing and learn about the brand's history and strategy
2. **Executive talk**: Company strategy, corporate culture, and talent selection criteria
3. **Resume & interview workshop**: HR session on job-seeking tips with real cases

## Logistics

The organizers will provide insurance (passenger liability) and cover lunch and dinner for all participants.

## Registration

Interested students must **scan the QR code** to join the consultation group and complete the questionnaire **by June 2, 2026, 24:00**. First come, first served. Results will be announced in the group.
