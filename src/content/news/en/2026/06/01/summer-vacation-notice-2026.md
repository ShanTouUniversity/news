## Schedule Overview

The Party and Administrative Office has issued the 2026 summer vacation notice (Document No. 2026-02), based on the semester system adjustment notice (Document No. 2026-129) and the academic calendar.

### Students

| Category | Summer Break | Fall Registration | Fall Classes Begin |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| With practical term courses | July 18 (Sat) – Sept 5 (Sat) | Sept 6 (Sun) | Sept 7 (Mon) |
| Other students | July 4 (Sat) – Sept 5 (Sat) | Sept 19 (Sat) | Sept 20 (Sun) |

### Faculty & Staff

- **Faculty with practical term duties**: Begin break after course completion; must finish grade submission first
- **Other faculty**: Begin break July 4 (Saturday)
- **All faculty**: Return to campus **Sept 4 (Friday)** for preparations, teaching research activities, and new-student course advising
- Colleges and departments should arrange social practice activities for students not enrolled in courses
- Graduate advisors to arrange teaching, research, and practice activities per training plans

## Safety Education

Colleges, residential colleges, and relevant departments must provide pre-vacation safety education, especially concerning personal and property safety.

## Administrative & Support Staff Arrangements

Based on STU's standing policy (Document 2009-08):

- **Rotation period**: July 6 – September 3, with units arranging duty rosters based on workload
- **Summer work hours** (July 4 – September 3): **8:30–11:30 AM and 3:00–5:00 PM**
- Rotating staff must remain reachable and report to duty as needed
- Normal hours resume **September 4**

Each unit must submit its summer duty schedule (names, office numbers, office and mobile phones) **by end of workday June 22** to the Party and Administrative Office (email: o_jdk@stu.edu.cn, tel: 86502877).

> ⚠️ The administrative and support staff summer duty schedule form is available on the OA system.
> Source: STU OA Notice (Party and Administrative Office)
