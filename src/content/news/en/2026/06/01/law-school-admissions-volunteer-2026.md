## Overview

STU Law School is recruiting student volunteers (undergraduate and graduate) for the 2026 admissions promotion campaign, running from mid-June through early July.

| Role | Quota | Period | Notes |
|------|-------|--------|-------|
| Online consulting & WeChat group maintenance | **20** | Mid-June – mid-July | Flexible schedule; must be regularly available June 24–30 |
| On-site school visits | **40** | June 24–28 | Chaozhou (10), Shanwei (6), **Foshan (24)** (Foshan: 2–3 days during June 25–28) |

## Eligibility

- Priority to students from Chaozhou, Shanwei, or Foshan
- Enthusiastic about STU, strong communication skills, disciplined

## Registration

Fill in the questionnaire (https://v.wjx.cn/vm/rCWWUaf.aspx#) and join the WeChat group **by June 8, 24:00**.

Volunteers must attend training and will receive volunteer service hours. Activity expenses are reimbursable per financial regulations.
