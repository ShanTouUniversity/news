## Public Notice

Per higher-level directives, the "**Glorious 50 Years in the Party**" commemorative medal will be awarded to party members who have maintained 50 years of party membership by July 1, 2026, have demonstrated consistent good conduct, and have not previously received the medal. Three STU party members meet the criteria this year:

Duan Wenyong, Huang Jinghong, Li Chuyin.

Public feedback period: June 1 – June 7, 2026. Any objections may be submitted in writing, in person, or by phone to the Party Organization Department (official submissions must bear a seal; personal submissions must include the full name of the complainant).
