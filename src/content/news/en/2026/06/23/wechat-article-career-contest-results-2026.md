It is reported that the Admissions and Employment Office held the "Let's Meet • Career Path" WeChat article submission event from May 22 to June 15, 2026. After review and selection, works from 44 students were chosen.

Selected students may collect their certificates at the Admissions and Employment Office, Room 223, Administration Center, Sangpushan Campus.
