It is reported that the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism has completed its selection of 2026 outstanding undergraduate graduates. Following student applications, review by the college evaluation committee, and public announcement, four students were awarded the title of "2026 Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate of the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism."

The honorees are: Cen Y, Zhu XX, Wen NN, Chen JX.

The college hopes the awardees will cherish the honor and continue to excel, while encouraging all students to follow their example.
