It is reported that the College of Liberal Arts Party Committee held a themed education event on June 24 at Room 212 of Siyuan College, under the theme "Tempering Party Spirit, Strengthening Work Style, Upholding Original Aspirations, Embarking on a New Journey."

## Event Highlights

The event was designed for all graduating Party members of the College's student Party branches and featured four main segments.

**Final Party Lesson Before Graduation** — Graduating Party members studied General Secretary Xi Jinping's important discussions on establishing and practicing a correct view of performance, with a focus on Party spirit cultivation, work style development, and professional integrity. The session aimed to help graduates develop a clean and ethical approach to their future careers.

**Learning Reflections Sharing** — Graduating Party members shared their insights and commitments based on their career plans and future job realities, reinforcing discipline and rules awareness.

**Oath Reaffirmation** — All participants reaffirmed their Party membership oath and celebrated their political birthday together, strengthening their sense of identity and responsibility as Party members.

**Membership Transfer Procedures** — Staff introduced policies for transferring Party membership relationships, explaining procedures and documentation requirements for different post-graduation paths including employment, further study, and other circumstances. Questions were addressed on site.
