It is reported that the Business School, in collaboration with China Mobile's Shantou campus store, will form a China Mobile Campus Service Ambassador team, recruiting current students. The service period is tentatively set for September 7 to 20, 2026.

## Requirements

All majors are welcome. Applicants should have a strong sense of responsibility, be able to commit sufficient time during the orientation period, and possess good communication and teamwork skills. Experience in student activity organization or volunteer service is preferred.

## Service Scope

Campus Service Ambassadors will assist with communication service guidance organized by the university-enterprise campus brand store, including providing on-site consultation on mobile services and campus card processing for new students and parents, answering questions about campus network and communication plans, and collecting service experience feedback.

## How to Apply

Applications are open from now until July 2, 2026. Interested students can scan the QR code to fill in the registration questionnaire and join the recruitment consultation group.

## Incentives

The campus brand store will provide unified pre-service training. Those who complete the service period and pass the assessment will receive a social practice certificate issued by China Mobile Chenghai Branch. A special subsidy and commemorative gifts will also be provided based on actual service hours and performance.

Outstanding participants interested in continued involvement may join the core operations team of the campus brand store, engaging in daily operations and management. Those who consistently excel and pass comprehensive assessments will have the opportunity to obtain an internship.
