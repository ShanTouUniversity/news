The undergraduate Party branch of the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design will hold a themed Party Day event on June 25 at 16:00 in the Art College's 3rd-floor meeting room, with the theme "Tempering Party Spirit, Forging a New Journey."

The event features: a lecture on correct performance views and integrity in the workplace; learning reflections from graduating Party members; a Party oath reaffirmation ceremony; and an introduction to Party organization transfer procedures.

All graduating Party members are required to wear Party badges and arrive 15 minutes early.
