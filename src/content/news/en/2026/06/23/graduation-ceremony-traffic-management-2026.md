It is reported that the 2026 Graduation Ceremony and Awards Presentation was held on June 26 at the Shantou Sports Center Gymnasium. To ensure smooth traffic for shuttle buses transporting graduates from Sangpushan Campus, the Security Office implemented temporary traffic management on certain sections of the campus.

## Restricted Periods

Traffic management was in effect during two time slots on June 26: 6:00-7:30 and 12:00-13:30.

## Restrictions

During the restricted periods, the road from the main gate entrance to the main gate exit served as a temporary parking and boarding area for graduation ceremony shuttle buses. Other vehicles were prohibited from parking or passing through. Vehicles for faculty, service staff, and affiliated school parents were required to use the southwest gate for campus access.
