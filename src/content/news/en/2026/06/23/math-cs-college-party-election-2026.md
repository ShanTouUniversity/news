It is reported that the College of Mathematics and Computer Science held a Party congress on June 24 at Building E, Room 102 on the Donghai'an Campus, to elect the first Committee of the CPC Shantou University College of Mathematics and Computer Science.

The congress was organized by the preparatory work leadership group and approved by the university Party Committee. All full Party members participated in the election, with probationary Party members attending as non-voting participants.

The congress followed strict electoral procedures, using secret ballot and competitive election to select the College Party Committee members.
