It is reported that the Business School has completed its evaluation of outstanding undergraduate graduates. Following student applications, committee review, and college deliberation, 43 students have been recommended for the 2026 Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate title.

|-------|------------|
| Accounting | Zhou LR, Zhan XQ, Chen JX, Liang WE, Huang JY, Zhang ML, Zheng YL, Wang Q, Fang X, Chen BH, Wang JQ, Zhong YP, Liang ZW, Yang EM |
| Finance | Zou LY, Huang SY, Zhang QY, Lin XH, Chen YY, Xu ZY, Ye JX, Chen JA, Su YY, Qian YH, Chen BW, Niu JJ, Huang XY, Deng SY |
| Intl. Economics & Trade | Huang F, Luo HY, Chen YL, Zhang JJ, Liu JX, Tian EH, Qi WL |
| Business Administration | Wang TY, Liu QL, Li WF, He XL, Lin XH, Chen TT |
| Marketing | Zheng XT, Deng LT |

The public notification period ends at 5:00 PM on June 25. Any objections may be submitted in writing to the committee.
