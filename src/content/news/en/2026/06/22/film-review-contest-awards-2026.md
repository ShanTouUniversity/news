It is reported that the results of the 2026 Guangdong University Library "Xuewenbei" Film Review Competition have been announced, with the theme "Films Inspire, Words Paint the Land." The competition featured traditional text review and AIGC creative tracks, with 138 universities and 5,612 valid entries participating.

## STU Achievements

| Award | Name | Work |
|-------|------|------|
| Text Review Second Prize | Li CL | "Developing History from Negatives, Illuminating Hearts with Flickering Light" |
| Text Review Third Prize | Wu PY | "A Civilian Epic Facing Death" |
| AIGC Excellence Award | Bi ZQ | "Blood Evidence" |

Advisors Huang Jigang and Zhou Jie received Outstanding Advisor awards.

Please follow the library's notice for certificate collection.
