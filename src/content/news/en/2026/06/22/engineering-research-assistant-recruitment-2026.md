It is reported that the College of Engineering is recruiting one research assistant from both on and off campus.

## Requirements

Applicants should hold a master's degree or higher in biology, chemistry, environmental science or related fields, with independent research experience in water/wastewater treatment catalytic material preparation, characterization, and water quality analysis. Laboratory management experience, proficiency in office software, and over one year of relevant work experience are preferred.

## Responsibilities

The position involves conducting research on water/wastewater treatment catalytic materials, writing reports and papers, and other assigned tasks.

## Compensation

The position is a research assistant role without personnel file transfer to STU, with a monthly salary of RMB 3,800-4,200 and other benefits per university policy.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates should send their resume, ID copy, and other relevant materials to o_gxy@stu.edu.cn (subject: "Application for Engineering Research Assistant Position"). Applications close July 1, 2026. Shortlisted candidates will be notified for interviews.
