It is reported that following student applications, college recommendations, and review by the university Youth League Committee, two students have been recommended for the 2026 Guangdong University "Young Marxists" (Qingma Project) Training Program.

The recommended nominees are: Zhou ST (College of Engineering, 2025 Class Civil and Hydraulic Engineering postgraduate) and Ji YS (Law School, 2024 Class Law undergraduate).

The public notification period ended at 12:00 PM on June 27. Objections may be submitted to the university Youth League Committee.
