It is reported that the Party Committee of the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Art and Design has approved 13 individuals as probationary members of the Communist Party of China for a one-year probationary period: Lin JY, Lv ZY, Chen YR, Chen JY, Yang QY, Feng GN, Song ZJ, Qiu DW, Lin RN, Liu XY, Huang QY, You CY, and Li JX.
