It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee and Young Volunteers Association, in collaboration with Shantou Central Blood Station, held a blood donation event themed "Passing on Passion, Saluting Youth with Blood" for the 2026 graduation season. The event took place on June 23 at the Sangpushan Campus flagpole and on June 25 at the East Coast Campus second cafeteria.

Participants were required to meet standard health criteria, including age (18-55), weight, and medical conditions. A photo booth was set up for participants to share on social media.
