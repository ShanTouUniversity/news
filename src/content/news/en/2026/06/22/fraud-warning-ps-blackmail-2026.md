It is reported that the Security Office has issued a fraud warning after several STU staff and students received suspicious letters containing morphed images created through AI face-swapping and Photoshop, used for extortion.

The Security Office advises: stay calm, do not reply or transfer money, protect personal information online, and report any incidents to the police at 110 with evidence screenshots.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Security Office)
