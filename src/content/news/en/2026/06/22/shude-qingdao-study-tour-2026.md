It is reported that Shude College, in collaboration with Southern University of Science and Technology and Zhejiang University, will organize a study tour to Qingdao from July 10 to 16, recruiting 8 female students from STU. The application deadline was June 24.

## Itinerary

The tour includes visits to the Qingdao Beihai Fleet, University of Health and Rehabilitation Sciences, Ocean University of China, Shandong University, and Qingdao Haikong Group, with site visits and seminars on national defense, university education philosophies, marine economy, and public health development.

## Fees

Shude College covers outbound transportation, accommodation, and activity materials. Meals in Qingdao, personal expenses, and return transportation are at participants' own cost. Participants are also required to submit an official certificate of no criminal record.
