It is reported that the College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering held a Party congress on June 23 at the Science and Technology Lecture Hall on the Sangpushan Campus, to elect the first Committee of the CPC Shantou University College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering.

The congress was organized by the preparatory work leadership group and approved by the university Party Committee. All full Party members participated in the election, with probationary Party members attending as non-voting participants. The congress followed strict electoral procedures, using secret ballot and competitive election to select the College Party Committee members.
