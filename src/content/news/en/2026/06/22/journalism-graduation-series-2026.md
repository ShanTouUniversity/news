It is reported that the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism and Xingchuan College jointly hosted the "River to Sea • Passing the Torch" 2026 graduation season activities.

## Events

**Graduate Symposium** — Held on June 24 at Room 628, Administration Building, East Coast Campus, featuring faculty-student exchanges, awards, and farewell messages.

**Graduate Sharing Session** — Following the symposium, senior students shared experiences with underclassmen. Speakers included Cen Y (Advertising) on finding direction amid uncertainty, Wen NN (Journalism) on using oneself as a method, and Lian X (Journalism and Communication) on embracing uncertainty.

**Item Recycling** — Held from June 25 to 27 at Xingchuan College, where graduates donated used books and items, receiving packing boxes in return.
