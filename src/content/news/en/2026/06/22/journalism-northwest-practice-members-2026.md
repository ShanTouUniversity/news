It is reported that the Cheung Kong Kwan School of Journalism has completed recruitment for the "Retracing China's Northwest Corner" (Inner Mongolia) project. Following student applications, portfolio review, interviews, and daily performance assessment, five students have been recommended for participation.

Selected participants (in no particular order): Chen ZX, Luo JK, Wu WH, Zhang YJ, Huang MY.

The project team noted that Inner Mongolia features diverse terrain and complex climate conditions, with grassroots research tasks that may involve challenging field environments. Participants are advised to prepare sun protection and insect repellent supplies, adjust their physical and mental condition, and maintain strong teamwork awareness.
