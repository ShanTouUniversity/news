It is reported that, to promote military recruitment on campus, the College of Engineering and Jingyi College will jointly host the "Dream of Military Service, Build Your Youth" recruitment promotion event on June 12 from 15:00 to 17:00 at Jingyi College Building C, Sangpushan Campus.

The event will include a briefing on 2026 military recruitment policies, a military knowledge quiz, and a face-to-face sharing session with veteran students. It is open to all College of Engineering students and Jingyi College residents. Participants may register via the "Daomeng Kongjian" APP to earn second classroom credits and comprehensive evaluation bonus points.

> Source: College of Engineering and Jingyi College OA Notice
