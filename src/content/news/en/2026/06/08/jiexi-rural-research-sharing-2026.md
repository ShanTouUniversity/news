It is reported that, to advance the "Hundred, Thousand, Ten Thousand Project," the STU Youth League Committee and the Student Union will hold a sharing session on the student leader research trip to Jiexi County on June 10 from 16:00 to 17:00 at Room E201-1, Building E, Sangpushan Campus. The event is open to all faculty and students.

Student leaders from university and college-level student organizations will share their findings on rural cultural tourism, specialty agriculture development, Lei tea culture, and the Three Mountain King culture. Interested students should register via the "Daomeng Kongjian" APP by 20:00 on June 9 and join the group chat. Participants are required to check in 15 minutes before the event.

> Source: STU Youth League Committee and STU Student Union OA Notice
