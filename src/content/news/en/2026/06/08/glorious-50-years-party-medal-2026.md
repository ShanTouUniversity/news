It is reported that, in accordance with the latest notice from the higher-level Party organization, the STU Party Organization Department has identified five additional Party members who will have reached 50 years of Party membership by December 31, 2026, and are proposed to receive the "Glorious 50 Years in the Party" commemorative medal.

The five newly proposed recipients are: Xie Hebiao, Lin Haoyu, Huang Zanfa, Huang Jinzun, and Chen Wenyuan. The public comment period runs from June 8 to June 12. Units or individuals with objections may submit written comments, visit in person, or call the Organization Department during this period.
