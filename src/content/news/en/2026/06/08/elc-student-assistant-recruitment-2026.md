It is reported that the English Language Center is recruiting 6 office student assistants from the Sangpushan Campus to support its daily operations.

Assistants will be responsible for front desk reception, document delivery between departments, office supplies management, and event preparation support. Applicants should be proficient in common office software, possess strong communication skills, and demonstrate a strong sense of responsibility. Those with ample free time and the ability to work stable weekly shifts are preferred. Compensation will be paid according to the university's work-study standards.

Interested students should send their CV to elc@stu.edu.cn by June 15 with the subject line "ELC Student Assistant + Name + Grade and Major," and join the WeChat group via QR code.
