It is reported that the 2026 National Education Science Planning annual project application process has commenced. According to the Guangdong Provincial Education Science Planning Office, STU may submit up to 2 project applications, which will be determined through an internal review organized by the education discipline.

This year's applications are conducted online. Applicants must register on the National Education Science Planning Management Platform (https://202.205.185.227/) by June 10 and notify the university via email at o_wkb@stu.edu.cn for verification. The application form and proposal outline must be submitted to the system for formal review by June 12. A signed paper copy of the application must be submitted to the Research Office by June 18. The final PDF versions (with official seal) must be uploaded to the platform by June 21.

Funding amounts: National Social Science Fund Education key projects (350,000 yuan), general/youth/western projects (200,000 yuan), MOE key/youth projects (80,000 yuan), and doctoral student projects (50,000 yuan). The system will be open from 00:00 on June 10 to 17:00 on June 25.
