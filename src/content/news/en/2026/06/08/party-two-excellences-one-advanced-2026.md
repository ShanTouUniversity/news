It is reported that the STU Party Committee will recognize outstanding Party members, outstanding Party affairs workers, and advanced grassroots Party organizations ahead of July 1 under the 2026 "Two Excellences, One Advanced" campaign. After nominations from secondary Party organizations and review by the Organization Department, the proposed commendation list has now been published for public feedback.

The public comment period runs from June 8 to 5:00 PM on June 12. During this period, units or individuals with objections may submit written comments, visit in person, or call the Organization Department. Units must affix their official seal; individuals must provide their real name.
