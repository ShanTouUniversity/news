It is reported that since the recruitment notice was released on June 5, the call for volunteers for the 2026 Guangdong Provincial City Basketball League Shantou Division has received an enthusiastic response from students. Following the selection process, 46 students, including Xu JH, have been confirmed as official volunteers. The full list is available in the appendix. Admitted volunteers should scan the QR code to join the group chat; any questions may be directed to the coordinator in the consultation group.
