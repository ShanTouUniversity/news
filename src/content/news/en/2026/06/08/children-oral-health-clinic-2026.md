It is reported that, as part of the ongoing "Health Wednesday" series, the STU Labor Union, together with the Medical College Youth League Committee and the Dental Hospital Youth League Committee, will host a children's oral health education and free clinic event on June 10 from 16:30 to 17:30 at the table tennis room on the first floor of the Labor Union building on Sangpushan Campus.

The event will be staffed by the medical team from the STU Medical College Dental Hospital, featuring a children's oral health lecture, free basic dental check-ups, one-on-one consultations, and interactive Q&A with giveaways. All faculty, staff, and students are welcome to attend.
