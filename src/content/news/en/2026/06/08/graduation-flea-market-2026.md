It is reported that, to promote green living and facilitate the recycling of闲置 items from graduating students, the College of Engineering and Jingyi College will host the "Cherish Memories, Trade for a New Journey" graduation flea market on June 16 from 15:00 to 18:00 at the Jingyi College Building D atrium, Sangpushan Campus.

The event features four zones: a free trade zone for vendors to sell or swap items; a charity donation zone collecting books and stationery; an interactive experience zone where participants can collect stamps through trading, donating, or games to win prizes including college-branded merchandise; and a time capsule wall for graduation messages and photo opportunities. The event is specially supported by Huawei Terminal Company.

Interested vendors should register via the questionnaire as a dormitory group and join the consultation group. Tents, tables, and chairs will be provided for vendors, who must sign relevant agreements. Only personal second-hand items such as books, daily necessities, and clothing are permitted; food, beverages, prohibited electrical appliances, and illegal items are strictly forbidden.
