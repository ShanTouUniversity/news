It is reported that, to support the 2026 graduation ceremony, the STU Youth League Committee and the Youth Volunteer Association are recruiting 320 student volunteers from across the university.

The rehearsal is scheduled for June 25 from 14:00 to 18:00, and the official ceremony will take place on June 26 from 9:00 to 11:30. Applicants must be able to commit to their assigned shifts, possess strong communication skills, and demonstrate teamwork spirit. Priority will be given to those with volunteer experience at the 15th National Games handball event.

Interested students should scan the QR code to register and join the consultation group by 12:00 on June 12. The final list will be announced on the university's internal information network and the consultation group. Applicants are advised to confirm that their exam schedule does not conflict with the activity; if uncertain, exams should take priority.

> Source: STU Youth League Committee and STU Youth Volunteer Association OA Notice
