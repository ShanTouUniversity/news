It is reported that the 2026 first-half National College English (and Japanese) CET-4/6 exams will be held on June 13. The Academic Affairs Office is now recruiting exam assistants from across the university.

30 assistants are needed on the Sangpushan Campus, working from 14:00 to 15:30 on June 13 to assist with ID verification and facial recognition; training begins at 13:30. Another 30 assistants are needed on the East Coast Campus, working on June 12 from 16:00 to 18:00 and throughout June 13, responsible for exam room setup and order maintenance; training begins at 15:50 on June 12.

Applicants must be current undergraduates who have not registered for this exam. Medical College students are not eligible. Compensation will be paid at the university's standard work-study rate based on actual hours worked. Registration closes at 17:00 on June 10 on a first-come, first-served basis.
