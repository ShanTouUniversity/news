It is reported that the College of Liberal Arts has initiated the selection of 2026 Outstanding Undergraduate Graduates to recognize exceptional graduating students.

Applicants must have a cumulative GPA ranking within the top 15% of their cohort and major, no course retakes, a university-level scholarship or above, and an excellent thesis grade. Preference will be given to graduates who have actively participated in social practice or volunteer work, achieved outstanding research results, or been admitted to graduate programs at home or abroad. Approximately 5% of graduates per major will be selected, and each recipient will receive a certificate and a 1,000 yuan prize.

Eligible students should submit their application form, personal statement (800+ characters), and supporting materials to the College Academic Affairs Office at xxhuang@stu.edu.cn by 12:00 PM on June 12, with the subject line "Outstanding Graduate Application + Major + Name."
