The Law School has announced the launch of its 2026 Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate selection process.

## Eligibility and Quota

The award is open to 2026 undergraduate graduates of the Law School. The selection ratio is capped at 10% of each department's graduates.

## Selection Criteria

**Basic requirements** include strong political and ideological qualities, excellent academic performance, active participation in social practice and extracurricular activities, and passing the National Student Physical Fitness Standard.

**Mandatory requirements**:
- Cumulative GPA ranking within the top 30% of the same grade and program
- Outstanding graduation thesis, or provincial-level or above competition awards
- No failed or retaken compulsory or restricted elective courses
- No disciplinary violations or sanctions
- Meeting bachelor's degree conferral requirements

**Preferential clauses**: Graduates who served in the military, participated in the "Three Supports and One Assistance" program, or volunteered in western (mountainous) regions may receive relaxed criteria. Those with confirmed employment are given priority under equal conditions.

## Application

Applicants must complete the Law School Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate Application Form, attach supporting materials (images in a Word document), and submit to **o_fxytw@stu.edu.cn** before **June 9, 2026 (Tuesday) at 24:00**. The email subject and document title should follow the format: "Graduate + Major + Name + Outstanding Graduate Application Materials."

A review committee will evaluate all applications and confer the "Outstanding Graduate" title with a certificate of honor.
