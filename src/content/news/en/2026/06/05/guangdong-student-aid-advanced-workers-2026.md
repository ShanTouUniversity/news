Three Shantou University faculty members have been honored with the 2025 Guangdong Province University Student Aid Advanced Worker award: Zhao Yue from the School of Science, Huang Yuanying from the Business School, and Lin Zhanqiao from the School of Engineering.

The Student Affairs Office said it hopes all student aid workers will follow their example, strive for excellence, and strengthen theoretical learning and research to continuously advance the university's student aid efforts.
