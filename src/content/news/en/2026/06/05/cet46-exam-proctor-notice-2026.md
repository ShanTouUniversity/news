According to the Academic Affairs Office, the 2026 first-half College English (and Japanese) Test Band 4 and Band 6 will be held on Saturday, June 13. CET-4 runs from 9:00 AM to 11:20 AM, and CET-6 from 3:00 PM to 5:25 PM.

## Proctor Training

Training will be held on **June 11 (Thursday), 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM**:

- **Sangpu Campus**: Lecture Hall 102, Building E
- **East Coast Campus**: Room 105, Building E (online participation as sub-venue)

Proctors may choose either location and should review the relevant examination documents before training.

## Exam Day Schedule

| Campus | Level | Assembly Time | Assembly Point |
|--------|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Sangpu | Band 4 | 8:10 AM | Lecture Hall 102, Building E |
| Sangpu | Band 6 | 2:00 PM | Lecture Hall 102, Building E |
| East Coast | Band 4 | 7:50 AM | Room 105, Building E |
| East Coast | Band 6 | 2:00 PM | Room 105, Building E |

All proctors must undergo security screening before entering the exam hall. Communication and camera devices are prohibited. Proctor assignments will be announced on the exam day.

Exam hotline: Sangpu Campus 82902402 / 86502623 / 86502402; East Coast Campus 86500161.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
