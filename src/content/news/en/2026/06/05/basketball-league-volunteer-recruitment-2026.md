The 2026 Guangdong Province City Basketball League Shantou leg is coming to the Zhengda Gymnasium. To support the event, the STU Youth League Committee and Youth Volunteers Association are recruiting student volunteers from the East Coast Campus for venue operations, crowd guidance, and ticket inspection.

**Date**: Wednesday, June 10, 2026, 14:00-22:00  
**Venue**: Zhengda Gymnasium, Shantou

**Open Positions (60 total)**:

| Role | Needed |
|------|--------|
| Stand spectator service | 15 |
| Inner entrance order maintenance | 5 |
| Entrance service | 9 |
| Ticket inspection | 12 |
| On-site guidance | 12 |
| Safety inspection | 4 |
| Party-member volunteer service | 3 |

**Basic Requirements**:
- Voluntary participation in the event
- Law-abiding, no criminal record, responsible in work and study
- Competent for the assigned role
- Dedicated to public welfare, with strong social responsibility and good moral character
- Willing to accept organizer guidance and follow work arrangements
- Already registered as a volunteer on the Guangdong Volunteer Platform (www.gdzyz.cn)

**Support Provided**:
- Round-trip bus transportation on event day
- Evening meal on duty
- Volunteer uniform and identification

**Registration**:
Register by **18:00 on Saturday, June 6, 2026** via:  
https://v.wjx.cn/vm/eclUY53.aspx

After registering, join the volunteer consultation group via the QR code. Results will be posted on the campus information portal and in the group.

> Source: STU Youth League Committee and Youth Volunteers Association OA Notice
