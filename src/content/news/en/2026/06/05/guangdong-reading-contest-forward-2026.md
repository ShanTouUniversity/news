The university has forwarded the official notice for the Guangdong Provincial Classic Reading Competition, led by the provincial publicity department, to encourage campus participation.

## Timeline

- **Online preliminary submission**: April 22 – June 30, 2026
- **Preliminary round (online)**: Before July 15, 2026
- **Selection round (online)**: Before end of July 2026
- **Provincial finals (offline)**: Before August 15, 2026
- **Awards and stage performance (offline)**: End of August 2026, during the South China Book Festival

## Participation

- **Open to**: All readers in Guangdong, no age, occupation, or location restrictions
- **Tracks**:
  - Youth (under 18)
  - Adult
  - Family/Team (up to 6 members)
- **Registration**: Download the Nanfang Metropolis N-Video app, enter the competition portal, fill in information and upload works

## Work Requirements

Works should be based on the **"Reading Lingnan"** book list, highlighting Lingnan culture and Bay Area development. Youth participants in the preliminary round may submit images or videos; adults and family/team entries must submit videos. Creative forms such as recitation, drama, role-play, shared reading, and creative interpretation are encouraged.

- Duration: 1-5 minutes
- Resolution: not less than 720P
- File size: not more than 500M
- Original content, no watermark

## Awards

A total of **100 winning individuals/teams** will be selected:
- Youth track: 50 awards
- Adult track: 30 awards
- Family/Team track: 20 awards

Each track will have first, second, and third prizes. Winners will receive **honor certificates + premium book packages**. Outstanding works will be included in the provincial lifelong learning platform resource library.
