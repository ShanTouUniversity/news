The School of Arts and Design has opened the 2026 Outstanding Graduate selection for full-time undergraduate students. The program honors academically excellent graduates and encourages strong academic performance across the student body.

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Firm political stance, sound ideological character, and no disciplinary violations
- GPA ranking in the top 15% within the same grade and major, with no retaken courses
- Excellent graduation thesis/project, or provincial-level (or higher) awards in professional competitions
- At least one university scholarship or other university-level (or higher) award or honor
- Priority will be given to candidates who have actively participated in social practice, volunteer service, or public welfare activities

**Application Process**:
Eligible students must complete the Outstanding Graduate Application Form and submit, by **12:00 on Friday, June 20, 2026**, the following to the Student Affairs Office email:

**o_ystw@stu.edu.cn**  
Email subject format: `参评优秀毕业生＋专业＋姓名`

A selection committee appointed by the school will review all applications and determine the final recipients. Each class will nominate one outstanding graduate, who will receive an honor certificate and a monetary award.
