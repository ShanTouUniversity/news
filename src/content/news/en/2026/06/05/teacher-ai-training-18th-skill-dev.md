## Training Details

According to the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment, the 18th AI literacy and capability improvement training session will be held as follows:

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Time | June 10, 2026 (Wednesday), 2:30 PM – 5:30 PM |
| Venue | Room 204, Lecture Hall, Building E, Sangpu Campus |
| Topic | Hands-on AI-Empowered Teaching Skill Development |
| Speaker | Zheng Yu (Qiuye Group) |

## Training Content

- **Fundamentals**: Core concepts of Skills and their instructional architecture
- **Skill Applications**: Courseware design, classroom teaching, knowledge visualization, academic tools, and chart generation
- **Development Practice**: End-to-end Skill creation from ideation, AI-assisted development, debugging, to publishing

## About the Speaker

Zheng Yu is a lecturer expert (AI direction) under the Chinese Institute of Electronics' Talent Project, a senior AIGC prompt engineer, and a gold medal AIGC instructor at Qiuye Group. He is also a senior AI trainer at Alibaba DAMO Academy and formerly served as a regional training lead for a major retail enterprise.
