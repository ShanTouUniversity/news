## Abstract

This talk discusses the boundedness and compactness conditions for Cesàro-type operators associated with a positive finite Borel measure supported on [0,1), acting on two derivative-type spaces of analytic functions (derivative Hardy spaces and weighted Dirichlet spaces).

## About the Speaker

Dr. Xie Huayou is a lecturer at Guangdong University of Finance. He received his PhD from the School of Mathematics at Sun Yat-sen University in 2025. His research focuses on operator theory in functional analysis and complex analysis, particularly function spaces. His work has been published in *J. Funct. Anal.*, *J. Geom. Anal.*, *Proc. Amer. Math. Soc.*, *Proc. Edinb. Math. Soc.*, among other leading journals.
