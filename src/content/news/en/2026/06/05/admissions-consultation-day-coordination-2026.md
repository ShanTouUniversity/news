To strengthen inter-departmental coordination for the 2026 Admissions Consultation Day, the university will hold a preparation meeting on **Tuesday, June 9, 2026** in Room 401, Administration Building East Wing, Sangpu Mountain Campus. The meeting is scheduled to begin around **11:00**, following the graduation departure coordination meeting.

**Participating Units**:
All undergraduate admissions units, Publicity Department (News Center), Academic Affairs Office, Student Affairs Office, Youth League Committee, International Exchange Office, English Language Center, Shude Academy, Zhi Cheng Academy, Resources Management Office (including campus health services), Security Office, Library, Network and Information Center, and East Coast Campus Management Committee.

Each unit should submit its attendee list to the Admissions and Employment Office by **12:00 on Monday, June 8, 2026** via email: o_zsb@stu.edu.cn. Contact: Chen Xiangyu, Tel: 86503666.

Attendance is strongly encouraged; please arrange work schedules accordingly.
