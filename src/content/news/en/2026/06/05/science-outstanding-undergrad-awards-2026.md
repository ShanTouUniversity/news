The College of Science and Zhicheng College have announced the launch of the 2026 Outstanding Undergraduate Graduate selection process.

## Eligibility Criteria

**Basic requirements**:
- Comprehensive development in moral, intellectual, physical, aesthetic, and labor education
- Cumulative GPA ranking within the top 20% of the same grade and program
- No retaken compulsory courses
- At least one university-level scholarship or above
- Outstanding graduation thesis, or provincial third prize or above in academic competitions

**Priority conditions** (one of the following):
- **Social service**: Active participation in social practice, volunteer work, or public welfare with notable contributions
- **Service to peers**: Held a key student leadership role for one year or more with outstanding performance
- **Grassroots service**: Military service, or participation in "Three Supports and One Assistance" and western (mountainous) region volunteer programs

## Application Process

Applicants must submit the completed application form with a personal statement (800+ characters) and supporting materials to their advisor's email by **June 14, 2026 (Sunday)**. The email subject should follow the format: "Outstanding Graduate Application + Major + Name." Physical copies must also be submitted to the advisor.

A review committee will evaluate all applications. The selection ratio is capped at **5%** of each department's graduates. Selected students will be awarded the "Outstanding Graduate" title with a certificate of honor.
