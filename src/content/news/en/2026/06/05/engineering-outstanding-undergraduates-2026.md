The School of Engineering has opened the 2026 Outstanding Undergraduate selection to recognize exemplary graduates and promote academic excellence across the student body.

**Eligibility**:
- Firm political stance, law-abiding, no disciplinary violations during studies
- Received university-level (or higher) scholarship or honor
- GPA ranking in the top 10% within the same grade and major
- Excellent graduation thesis/project
- Priority will be given to candidates who have actively participated in social practice, volunteer service, or public welfare activities; achieved outstanding results in entrepreneurship or research; or won provincial-level (or higher) awards in subject competitions
- Students who have enlisted in the military, joined the "Three Supports and One Assistance" program, or participated in the Western (Mountain) Volunteer Service program may be considered with relaxed standards

**Application Process**:
1. Graduating students must complete the STU School of Engineering Outstanding Undergraduate Application Form, attach supporting materials and an 800-word personal achievement essay, and submit all electronic materials to the student counselor email by **16:00 on June 11, 2026**
2. A selection committee appointed by the school will review all applications and determine the final recipients

**Award Quota**:
The number of awards is capped at **3%** of the graduating undergraduate cohort. The final quota will be determined based on the total number of applicants.
