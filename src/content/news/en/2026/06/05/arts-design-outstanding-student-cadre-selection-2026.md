The School of Arts and Design has opened the 2026 Outstanding Student Cadre selection for full-time undergraduate and graduate students. The program recognizes student leaders who have actively served their classes and the college community.

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Active class student cadre during studies, with firm political stance, sound ideological character, and no disciplinary violations
- Undergraduates: GPA of 3.3 or above with strong academic performance
- Graduate students: strong academic performance with active engagement in professional practice
- Proactive cooperation with college departments and faculty in teaching and administrative affairs
- Leadership in organizing class thematic and social practice activities
- Dedication to serving peers, integrity, and willingness to innovate

**Application Process**:
Eligible students must complete the appropriate Outstanding Student Cadre Application Form and submit, by **12:00 on Friday, June 20, 2026**, the following to the Student Affairs Office email:

**o_ystw@stu.edu.cn**  
Email subject format: `参评优秀学生骨干（本科生／研究生）＋专业＋姓名`

A selection committee appointed by the school will review all applications and determine the final recipients.

**Award Quotas**:
- Undergraduates: one recipient per class
- Graduate students: one recipient for the entire graduating cohort

Recipients will receive an honor certificate and a monetary award.
