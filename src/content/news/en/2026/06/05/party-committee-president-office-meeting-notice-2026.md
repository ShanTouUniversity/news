Shantou University's General Office has issued a notice further regulating the submission of agenda items for the Party Committee Standing Committee and President's Office meetings, aiming to improve meeting quality and deliberation efficiency.

## Key Requirements

**1. Pre-meeting Compliance Review**
Normative documents submitted for review must first be examined by the General Office for legal and regulatory compliance. The submitting department head must brief the Party Secretary and President **one week in advance**, covering the background, basis, main content, and consultation status of the document. Only after written approval may the item be placed on the agenda.

**2. Regular Meeting Schedule and Deadlines**
- The Party Committee Standing Committee and President's Office meetings are generally held every **Wednesday morning**.
- Submit complete electronic materials to the General Office by **12:00 on the Friday one week before the meeting** for compliance review.
- By **17:30 on the Monday of the meeting week**, submit the signed agenda forms and materials (printed + scanned copies) to the General Office.
- The General Office will compile and submit all materials to university leaders by **12:00 on Tuesday**.
- Items submitted after the deadline will not be scheduled for that week and will be deferred to the following week.

**3. Ad hoc Adjustments**
If meetings cannot be held at the regular time due to special circumstances, the General Office will issue separate instructions on submission deadlines.
