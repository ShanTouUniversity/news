The School of Mathematics and Computer Science has announced the launch of its 2025-2026 Outstanding Individual Awards.

## Eligibility

The awards are open to graduating undergraduate students, key student leaders from Party branches, League branches, student unions, and青年志愿者 associations, as well as class committee members and youth volunteers.

## Award Categories

- Outstanding Undergraduate Graduates
- Outstanding Student Leaders
- Outstanding Youth Volunteers
- Student Activity Participants

## Application Process

Applicants must submit their completed application form along with supporting materials to the designated email address by **June 11, 2026 at 12:00**.

The school's Youth League Committee will review applications based on the published criteria and release a preliminary list for a 3-day public notification period. Final awards and certificates will be issued after the notification period concludes without objections.

## Notes

The selection process must adhere to principles of fairness, justice, and transparency. Any falsified information will result in disqualification. Late submissions will not be accepted. For inquiries, contact the school's Youth League Committee.
