## Program Overview

Approved by the Ministry of Education Party Committee, the 2026 National Education Science Planning Discipline Construction and Graduate Training Research Special Project focuses on supporting major reforms in graduate education. Funding tiers: Key projects (350,000 yuan) and General projects (200,000 yuan). Research period: 1–3 years.

## Requirements

- Applicants must hold associate professor (or higher) rank or a doctoral degree
- Active holders of national or ministry-level projects are ineligible
- Topics must be selected from the provided guide; each topic typically funds only one project
- System open: **June 10 (midnight) – June 25 (5:00 PM)**

## Campus Schedule

| Date | Action |
|------|--------|
| **By June 15** | Submit application and proposal to system for format review |
| **By June 22** | Submit signed hard copy to Research Office |
| **By June 23** | Collect stamped application (PDF), upload with proposal PDF to platform |

Applicants must first register on the National Education Science Planning Management Platform and email o_wkb@stu.edu.cn for account verification.
