This summer, the Center for Independent Language Learning (CILL) is offering all Shantou University students and faculty **free access to Road to IELTS**, the official online IELTS preparation platform developed by the British Council. The initiative is designed to help the campus community make productive use of the summer break and get closer to their target IELTS scores.

**What the platform includes**:
- 300+ interactive learning activities
- 17 expert instructional videos
- 40 full IELTS mock tests
- Mobile-friendly design, supporting phones and tablets for on-the-go study

The license covers all STU students and faculty at no cost, whether on campus or at home.

**Important reminder**: Off-campus access requires the **STU VPN**. Without it, the platform may be inaccessible from outside campus.

**Get started**:  
[Road to IELTS Introduction & Setup Page](https://my.stu.edu.cn/courses/elc/mod/page/view.php?id=261036)

Register today and start your IELTS preparation over the summer.
