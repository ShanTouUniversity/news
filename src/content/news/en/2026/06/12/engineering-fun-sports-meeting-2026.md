It is reported that the Youth League Committee of the STU Engineering College and the Engineering College Graduate Student Union will co-host the "Journey Together, Enjoy Youth" Graduate Fun Sports Meeting on June 13, aimed at easing research pressures among graduate students, strengthening cohesion, and fostering collaboration across different disciplines.

Open to all graduate students in the Engineering College, the event will take place from 6:00 to 8:00 PM at the STU Track and Field Stadium. The sports meeting will feature team competitions, recruiting 3 to 4 teams of 6 members each. Four events will be offered: hula hoop relay, back-to-back ball relay, fun bowling, and balloon stomp battle. Each event will be timed and scored individually, with an overall ranking announced at the end.

Students interested in participating may register through their respective graduate class groups.
