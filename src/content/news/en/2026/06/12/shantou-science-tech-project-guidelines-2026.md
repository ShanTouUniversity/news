It is reported that the Shantou Municipal Science and Technology Bureau is soliciting suggestions for project application guidelines for the 2026-2027 program from enterprises, private non-profit organizations, universities, and research institutes within the city. Suggestions should address key bottlenecks in industrial chains, focus on industrial innovation and development needs, and strengthen market orientation and application demonstration.

Five categories of suggestions are sought:

| Category | Key Focus |
|----------|-----------|
| Key Core Technologies | New energy, new materials, next-generation electronics, toy creativity, textiles, and big health under the "Three New, Two Special, One Major" priority industries; new energy vehicles, low-altitude economy, high-end equipment manufacturing, green environmental protection as strategic emerging industries; bio-manufacturing, brain-computer interfaces, new energy storage, generative AI as future industries |
| Science and Technology Innovation Platforms | Construction of provincial key laboratories and new R&D institutions to gather innovation resources and talent |
| Innovation Collaboration | Linkage with the Greater Bay Area innovation platforms and major scientific infrastructure, promoting resource sharing, platform co-building, and joint achievement transformation |
| Innovation Service Capacity | R&D in healthcare, ecological environment, and public safety; scientific strategy research, innovation statistics, and technology management services |
| Tech-Driven Agriculture | Agricultural technology R&D, application and promotion of advanced technologies, achievement transfer, supporting rural revitalization |

Suggested content includes implementation details, expected objectives, proposed duration, rationale, lists of relevant institutions and enterprises in the province and nationwide, jointly recommending entities, and leading domestic experts in the field.

Submissions are due by June 18, 2026, via the Shantou Sunshine Government Platform (http://xm.gdstc.gd.gov.cn/st).
