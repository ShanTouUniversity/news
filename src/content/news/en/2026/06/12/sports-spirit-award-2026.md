According to a notice from the Student Affairs Office, based on the *Shantou University Sports Spirit Award Selection Management Measures* and the evaluation of the Sports Spirit Award Selection Committee, the 2026 Sports Spirit Award and nomination award results have been announced and are now under public review.

## Award Recipients

### Sports Spirit Award

| Name | College (Residential College) | Year | Major |
|------|------------------------------|------|-------|
| Jiang Zheng | Engineering College (Jingyi College) | 2022 | Civil Engineering |

### Sports Spirit Nomination Award

| Name | College (Residential College) | Year | Major |
|------|------------------------------|------|-------|
| Liu EN | College of Arts (Siyuan College) | 2022 | English |
| Chen YY | College of Chemistry & Chemical Engineering (Zhixing College) | 2022 | Materials Science and Engineering |

The public review period runs from today through 9:00 AM on June 16. Any units or individuals with objections may submit written feedback to the Student Financial Assistance Management Center of the Student Affairs Office during the review period. Institutional submissions must bear an official seal, and individual submissions must include a real name.
