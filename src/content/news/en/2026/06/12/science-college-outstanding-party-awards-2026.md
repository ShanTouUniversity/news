The Party Committee of the School of Science at Shantou University has published the proposed award list for 2026 Outstanding Party Workers and Outstanding Party Members, aiming to showcase advanced role models and inspire the party membership.

Following democratic evaluation and recommendation by party branches, and review by the college party committee, seven comrades are proposed for the title of Outstanding Party Worker, and 24 comrades for the title of Outstanding Party Member.

**Proposed Outstanding Party Workers** (7 recipients)

The seven proposed recipients come from the Physics Undergraduate Branch, Biology Undergraduate Branch, Physics Graduate Branch, and the first through fourth branches of Marine Biology Graduate students.

**Proposed Outstanding Party Members** (24 recipients)

The 24 proposed recipients come from various student party branches of the School of Science, including the Physics Undergraduate Branch, Biology Undergraduate Branch, Physics Graduate Branch, and the first through fourth branches of Marine Biology Graduate students.

**Feedback**

Those with any concerns regarding the proposed list are encouraged to submit written feedback to the School of Science Party Committee by **June 18, 2026**. Organizations should affix an official seal, and individuals should provide their real names.
