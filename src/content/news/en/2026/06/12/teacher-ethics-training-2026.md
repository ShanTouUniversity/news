It is reported that the University's Teacher Work Department will conduct the 2026 Teacher Ethics Training Program for all teaching staff. The training will be delivered online and runs from June 12 to July 12, with training hours credited for completion within this period.

The four-part course covers the following:

| Course Title | Speaker |
|-------------|---------|
| Teacher Ethics Construction — Professional Competence and Character in the New Era | Yao Xiaoling (Professor, Beihang University) |
| Wisdom, Integrity, and Moral Education — Scientific Ethics and Tech Ethics in the AI Era: Challenges and Responses | Yu Xuefu (Professor, Dalian University of Technology) |
| Leading Teacher Ethics in the New Era with Educator Spirit | Gao Xiaoying (Professor, Beijing Jiaotong University) |
| Promoting Educator Spirit: Construction and Practice of Teacher Ethics and Conduct | Zhong Qin (Professor, Nanjing University of Science and Technology) |

Per higher-level requirements, each teacher must complete no less than 12 training hours of teacher ethics education annually. The four sessions total approximately 12 hours (16 credit hours). Participants must complete at least 80% of each course within the given period to qualify for a certificate. Courses must be accessed via the "Shipei Alliance" WeChat public account; using playback speed controls or skipping to the end will not count as valid study time.

Certificates can be downloaded 15 working days after course completion. After the training concludes, the Teacher Work Department will compile backend data and report participation to each teaching unit.
