It is reported that the STU Youth League Committee has circulated a notice regarding the "My Village Chronicle I'll Restore" 2026 Guangdong Province Colorful Rural Practice Series Activities. The call for submissions is open to all faculty and students, seeking village chronicles, micro-videos, photography works, research reports, and special research project proposals.

The activity theme, "My Village Chronicle I'll Restore," aims to preserve the roots, memories, and cultural spirit of rural villages through documenting village conditions, narrating village history, and compiling village chronicles. Research subjects must be rural areas within Guangdong Province (including urban villages), with priority given to historically significant towns and villages, traditional villages, economically strong towns, towns with distinctive industries, "Hundred-Thousand-Ten Thousand Project" model towns and villages, and related zones.

The submission deadline for works is October 5, 2026. Research project proposals are due by June 25. Project leaders must hold a mid-level or higher professional title, a doctoral degree, or a department-head-level position or above. Final deliverables are due by October 25, 2026, consisting of a village chronicle draft of no less than 10,000 words.
