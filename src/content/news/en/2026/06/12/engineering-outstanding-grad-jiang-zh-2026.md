It is reported that the Engineering College of Shantou University has launched an outstanding graduate showcase series. This edition features Jiang ZH, a 2026 PhD graduate from the Department of Civil and Smart Construction Engineering, who also earned a doctoral degree in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Politecnico di Torino in Italy. His research focuses on innovative applications of acoustic emission in engineering structures and earthquake precursor identification.

## Prolific Research Output

During his doctoral studies, Jiang ZH published 17 high-level papers in domestic and international journals, including 14 SCI-indexed papers and one granted patent. He authored 10 SCI papers as first author or second author (with his supervisor as first), including 3 in CAS Zone 1 top journals and 3 in Zone 2 journals. Representative publications appear in Engineering Structures, Structures, Construction and Building Materials, and Natural Hazards and Earth System Science. His doctoral dissertation defense was rated outstanding.

He has also served as a reviewer for multiple international journals including Composites Part B: Engineering, Engineering Failure Analysis, Applied Acoustics, and Construction and Building Materials, completing over 30 reviews to date.

## Active International Engagement

Jiang ZH presented at the 12th International Conference on Fracture Mechanics of Concrete and Concrete Structures (FraMCoS-12) in Vienna, Austria, and the 21st International Conference on Experimental Mechanics (ICEM-21) in Bologna, Italy. He also attended the RILEM 2026 Spring Conference in Ghent, Belgium, and the ARTISTE International Conference on Machine Learning in Civil Engineering and Architecture in Turin.

Supported by a China Scholarship Council (CSC) scholarship, he pursued a dual doctoral degree at Politecnico di Torino under the supervision of Professor Giuseppe Lacidogna, a member of the Academy of Sciences internationally renowned in fracture mechanics and acoustic emission. Jiang ZH is the first student to complete the dual-degree program since the agreement was signed between the two universities, marking a significant milestone in the Engineering College's international talent cultivation efforts.

## Honors and Awards

- China Scholarship Council (CSC) State-Funded Scholarship
- National Scholarship for Doctoral Students
- Guangdong Province Outstanding Young Research Talent International Training Program
- Annual Outstanding Paper Award from Journal of Southeast University (Natural Science Edition)

## Mentorship and Research Platform

Jiang ZH's doctoral supervisor, Professor Zhu Zhiwen, leads the Structural and Wind Tunnel Key Laboratory of Guangdong Higher Education Institutions, which maintains high-level research platforms for wind engineering and structural health monitoring. The team is active in structural wind engineering, seismic engineering, and structural health monitoring, supported by the first-level doctoral program and postdoctoral research station in civil engineering. Jiang ZH credits his supervisor for providing both academic freedom and meticulous guidance, creating a supportive environment for rigorous research.
