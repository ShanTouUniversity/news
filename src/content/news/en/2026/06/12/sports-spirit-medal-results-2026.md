It is reported that the evaluation results for the 2026 Shantou University Sports Spirit Medal have been announced, based on the "Evaluation and Management Measures for the STU Sports Spirit Medal."

The results are as follows:

| Award | Name | College | Grade/Major |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|
| Sports Spirit Medal | Jiang Z | Engineering College (Jingyi College) | Class of 2022, Civil Engineering |
| Nomination Award | Liu EN | School of Arts (Siyuan College) | Class of 2022, English |
| Nomination Award | Chen YY | College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering (Zhixing College) | Class of 2022, Materials Science and Engineering |

The public notice period runs from June 12 until 9:00 AM on June 16. Any objections should be submitted in writing to the Student Financial Aid Management Center, Student Affairs Office (Office 223, Pudong Administration Building).
