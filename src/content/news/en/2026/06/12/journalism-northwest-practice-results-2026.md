It is reported that the recruitment phase for the Changjiang School of Journalism and Communication's "Retracing China's Northwest Corner (Northern Sichuan)" summer practice program has concluded. Following the announcement, students from all grade levels submitted applications, including cover letters and portfolio materials.

After a preliminary review of application materials and interviews, four students were selected based on their professional competence, physical fitness, and teamwork ability: Xia YN, Xu SL, Huang SP, and Tang XT.
