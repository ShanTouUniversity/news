It is reported that the School of Mathematics and Computer Science and Shanhai College will co-host the "Fallen Leaves Return to Roots" Graduation Choir Concert on June 13 from 8:00 to 9:30 PM at the central atrium of the L1 indoor courtyard, No. 90 Changping Road, Suning Plaza, offering a musical farewell for graduating students.

The concert will feature solo performances of classic songs including "Red Bean," "Young and Promising," "Chase," "Loved Like Fireworks," "Ten Years of Spring Breeze," and "The Crossing of Phoenix Flowers." A group chorus segment will invite audience members to join the performers in singing "The Farthest View" and "Fallen Leaves Return to Roots."

Students interested in participating may register via the "Daomengkong" app by searching "'Fallen Leaves Return to Roots' Graduation Concert." Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis and closes at 2:00 PM on June 13. All faculty and students are welcome to attend.
