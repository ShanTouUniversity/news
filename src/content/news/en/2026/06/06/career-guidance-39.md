It is reported that the College of Science, together with Zhicheng College, organized the 39th Career Guidance enterprise visit on June 5, 2026. Faculty representatives from the Biology and Physics departments visited three companies in the Sanshui district of Foshan.

**Companies Visited**

| Company | Location |
|---------|----------|
| Tsingtao Beer (Sanshui) Co., Ltd. | Sanshui, Foshan |
| Guangdong Radio and Television Network Co., Ltd. Foshan Branch | Foshan |
| Guangdong Red Bull Vitamin Drink Co., Ltd. | Sanshui, Foshan |

**Activities**

The event comprised three segments: First, an enterprise tour — faculty members visited production workshops, R&D laboratories, and operation exhibition areas to observe the companies' main business lines, production configurations, technology applications, and job position standards. Second, a seminar on school-enterprise collaboration, covering joint talent cultivation, internship and training base development, targeted graduate recruitment, and industry-academia research cooperation. Representatives from the companies' HR and business departments discussed industry trends, recruitment requirements, and curriculum optimization directions. Third, a resource matching session, where participants identified concrete entry points for biology- and physics-related collaboration and outlined a framework for long-term cooperation to establish sustainable channels for student internships and employment.

The event aims to implement the national action plan for expanding employment channels through enterprise visits, strengthen school-enterprise collaboration, and align talent cultivation in biology and physics with corporate demand.
