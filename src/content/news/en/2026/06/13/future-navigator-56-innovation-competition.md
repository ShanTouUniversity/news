It is reported that, to foster a vibrant academic atmosphere, strengthen students' innovation and entrepreneurship capabilities, and build a platform for exchanging outstanding projects, the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College will co-host the Future Navigator Plan (Session 56): a briefing on the China International College Students' Innovation Competition, featuring competition interpretation and preparation tips.

The event will be held at 9:00 on Sunday, June 14, in Room 603, Siyuan College, and is open to all students and faculty of the College of Liberal Arts and Siyuan College. Interested students should check class notices for registration; registered students may also register via the "Daomeng Kongjian" APP to earn second classroom credits upon completion of the event.

The lecture features Luo Lie as the keynote speaker. Luo Lie is a lecturer at STU's School of Marxism and serves as a China Youth Entrepreneurship Mentor and an Outstanding Innovation and Entrepreneurship Mentor of Guangdong Province, bringing extensive experience in coaching college students for innovation and entrepreneurship competitions.
