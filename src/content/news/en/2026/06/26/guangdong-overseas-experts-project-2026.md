The Guangdong Provincial Department of Science and Technology has launched the 2027 Overseas Distinguished Experts Project, supporting universities and institutions to invite overseas experts for short-term exchanges in science, education, and humanities.

**Funding**

- Experts from abroad or Taiwan: 50,000 yuan per project
- Experts from Hong Kong or Macau: 30,000 yuan per project

**Priority Areas**

Integrated circuits, AI, quantum technology, biotechnology, new energy, and humanities/social sciences.

**Requirements**

Overseas experts should hold associate professor or equivalent positions at recognized institutions. Maximum 10 projects per institution per year.

**Timeline**

Online registration opens July 24, 2026 via the Guangdong Science Business Management platform. Signed documents due by August 12. Project period: January 1 to November 30, 2027.
