"I dare to venture, I will create." The China International College Student Innovation Competition (2026) has officially launched at STU, running from May through September.

**Competition Structure**

Two tracks: the main track and the "Youth Red Dream Journey" track. Selection proceeds through college preliminaries, university semi-finals, and university finals. The finals will award 1 gold, 3 silver, and 6 bronze prizes, with top entries recommended for the provincial competition.

**Eligibility**

- Full-time students (undergraduate and graduate) or graduates within 5 years
- Ages 18 to 35
- Teams of 3 to 15 members, cross-college teams encouraged
- Each student may lead only one project

**Timeline**

| Phase | Date |
|-------|------|
| College preliminaries | May–July |
| University semi-finals | Jul–Aug (online review) |
| University finals | Aug–Sep (pitch + defense) |

Register via the National College Student Entrepreneurship Service website (http://cy.ncss.cn). Contact: 0754-86502255 / 0754-86502256.
