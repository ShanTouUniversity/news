The Current Affairs and Policy Education (Extracurricular) course is a mandatory public course for all 2027 graduates at both campuses, carrying 1 credit (16 class hours).

The course is completed through a thesis project without classroom sessions. Students must register via the course selection system under the School of Marxism during the designated period.

A thesis of 3,000–5,000 words must be submitted in both paper and electronic formats to the assigned instructor by January 22, 2027. This course is offered only once per year in the fall for graduating students. Late registration or late submission will affect graduation. Previous graduates may also enroll.
