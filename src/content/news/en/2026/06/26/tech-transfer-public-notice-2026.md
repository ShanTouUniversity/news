A technology secret titled "A Physiological Feedback-Adaptive Lower Limb Blood Circulation Enhancement System and Method" developed by Gao Dongna has been licensed to Shenzhen BKMinda Technology Co., Ltd. for 200,000 yuan over a 3-year period. The transaction is a know-how license via negotiated pricing.

The public comment period runs until July 10. Contact: Xie Fengshan, 86503271, o_kyc@stu.edu.cn.
