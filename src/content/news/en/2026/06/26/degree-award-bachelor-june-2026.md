The 11th STU Degree Evaluation Committee has awarded bachelor's degrees to 3,546 graduates.

**Breakdown by Discipline:**

| Degree | Count |
|--------|-------|
| Bachelor of Arts | 556 |
| Bachelor of Laws | 262 |
| Bachelor of Management | 344 |
| Bachelor of Economics | 186 |
| Bachelor of Fine Arts | 190 |
| Bachelor of Science | 680 |
| Bachelor of Engineering | 779 |
| Bachelor of Medicine | 549 |

Two students were also awarded dual bachelor's degrees.

> Source: STU OA Notice (STU Degree Evaluation Committee)
