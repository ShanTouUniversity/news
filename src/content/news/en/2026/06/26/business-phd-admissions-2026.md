The Business School has reported strong PhD admission results for its 2026 master's graduates. Seven students have been admitted to doctoral programs at prestigious universities including Sun Yat-sen University, South China University of Technology, Northeastern University, Central University of Finance and Economics, and Jinan University, achieving an admission rate of nearly 20%. Two additional 2024 graduates were admitted to China Agricultural University and Zhongnan University of Economics and Law.
