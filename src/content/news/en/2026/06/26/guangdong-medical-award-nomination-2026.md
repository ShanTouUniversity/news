It is reported that a project led by Professor Zhou Benqing (with STU as both completing and working unit) on "Molecular Mechanisms and Clinical Translation Strategies of New Therapeutic Targets for Breast Cancer" has been nominated for the 8th Guangdong Medical Science and Technology Award. The public notice period runs from June 26 to July 2.

Objections may be submitted in writing to the Research Office. Contact: Zheng Xiangli, 0754-86502583, o_kyc@stu.edu.cn.
