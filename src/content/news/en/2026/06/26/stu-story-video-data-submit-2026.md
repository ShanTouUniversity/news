The "Tell STU Story" short video contest, part of STU's 45th anniversary celebrations, is entering its data submission phase.

**Submission period:** June 28, 0:00 – June 30, 23:59

**How to submit:** Follow "汕大招生" on WeChat and click "材料提交", or scan the QR code.

Judging uses an engagement data + content review model across WeChat Video, Douyin, and Xiaohongshu.

**Prizes (per platform):** 1 Grand Prize (DJI Pocket 4), 2 First Prizes (DJI Action 5 Pro), 2 Second Prizes (Huawei Pad), 5 Third Prizes (headphones), 20 Excellence Awards. Top creators may become official admissions ambassadors.

Contact: 0754-86503666, o_zsb@stu.edu.cn.
