The Guangdong Key Laboratory of Marine Biotechnology invites Professor Cheng Pengfei from Ningbo University, ranked among the world's top 2% scientists for 2024–2025, to deliver an academic lecture.

**Lecture Details**

- Topic: Microalgae Germplasm Innovation and Eco-Health Resource Utilization
- Time: June 29, 9:30
- Venue: High-Level Lab Building A314

**Highlights**

Guided by the One Health concept, the talk covers: mutagenesis techniques (space microgravity, plasma, high ammonia stress) for stress-tolerant, high-yield algae strains; photobioreactor development for livestock and aquaculture wastewater treatment; and bioactive compounds from *Chlorella*, *Phaeodactylum*, and *Botryococcus* for pharmaceutical, functional food, and biofertilizer applications.

**About the Speaker**

Professor Cheng Pengfei has led over 10 national and provincial research projects, published 50+ papers in journals such as *Chemical Engineering Journal* and *Bioresource Technology*, and guided student teams to a gold medal at the China International College Student "Internet+" Innovation Competition.
