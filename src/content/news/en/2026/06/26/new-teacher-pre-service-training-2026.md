It is reported that the Guangdong Education Department will conduct the 2026 provincial pre-service training for new university teachers from July 12 to 19, combining online and offline formats. STU will set up a branch venue at Sangpu Campus for live streaming.

Eligible participants: teachers and counselors who joined in 2026 or have not completed pre-service training.

Registration deadline: June 30, 12:00, via o_szk@stu.edu.cn. Contact: Ni Xiaodan, 86502340.
