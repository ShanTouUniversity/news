The Comprehensive Ideological Practice Course (Part 2) is a mandatory public course for all 2024 undergraduates at both Sangpu and East Coast campuses, carrying 1 credit (16 class hours).

The course is completed through a thesis project without classroom sessions. Students must register via the course selection system under the School of Marxism during the designated period.

A thesis of 3,000–5,000 words must be submitted in both paper and electronic formats to the assigned instructor by January 22, 2027. Passing the course is required for graduation.
