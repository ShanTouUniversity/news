The Arts Education Center will host the 2026 "Mountains and Sea Music Festival" Spring Semester Series — the STU Wind Band Concert, on June 14 at 19:30 in Building B, Room 102 of the Donghai Campus.

Featuring the STU Symphonic Wind Band and the STU Donghai Campus Wind Band, the concert program spans Disney, the Marvel universe, Polovtsian dances, and the fantasy worlds of Hayao Miyazaki, offering a musical journey across genres and eras.

Admission is free with first-come, first-served seating. Attendees are encouraged to arrive 30 minutes before the performance. Please silence mobile devices and refrain from smoking or eating during the event.
