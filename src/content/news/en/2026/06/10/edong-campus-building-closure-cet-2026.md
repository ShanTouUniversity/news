It is reported that Building E at the East Coast Campus will be temporarily closed on June 13 from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM for the national College English (and Japanese) Test Band 4 and 6, as it will serve as an examination area.

All classrooms will be set up for the exam on the afternoon of June 12. Students should remove their personal belongings in advance, as items left behind will be treated as discarded.

> Source: STU Academic Affairs Office, Network and Information Center, and East Coast Campus Management Committee OA Notice
