## Final Review Schedule

With final exams approaching, the SLC Peer Learning Center is offering spring semester review sessions across 38 courses covering various majors. Senior course tutors will provide review materials, answer questions, share study tips, and help students grasp key concepts and difficult points.

The review sessions will be held during **Weeks 15-16** at the following locations:

| Campus | Venue |
|--------|-------|
| Sangpu Mountain Campus | SLC Peer Learning Center Study Rooms |
| East Coast Campus | Classroom E314, Classroom E315 |
| Online | Tencent Meeting |

Students can attend according to the session schedule **without prior appointment**.

Regular SLC consultation courses are also available. Students can register via the mystu platform at: https://my.stu.edu.cn/slc/front/index.jsp
