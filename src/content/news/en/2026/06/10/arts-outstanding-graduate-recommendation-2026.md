It is reported that following individual applications, advisor recommendations, and college review, the School of Arts has recommended three students for the 2026 STU Outstanding Graduate Award: Zhou XQ (Chinese Language and Literature), Chen WN (Chinese Language and Literature), and Liu LJ (English Subject Teaching).

The public notice period runs until June 12. Any objections should be submitted in writing to the School of Arts.
