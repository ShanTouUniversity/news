According to the STU Teaching Research Project Management Measures (Revised), the Academic Affairs Office will conduct the final acceptance review of university-level "AI+" higher education teaching reform projects initiated in 2025.

Faculty must submit project applications, acceptance registration forms (Appendix 3),成果 evidence, and "AI-empowered Higher Education Teaching" cases (Appendix 4) to the teaching project management platform (http://project.stu.edu.cn). Departments will conduct preliminary reviews and submit summary forms (Appendix 5). The submission deadline is **September 4, 2026 (Friday) at 17:30**. Late submissions without approved延期 requests will result in a failing review.

For project延期 requests, submit the "STU Quality Engineering-Teaching Reform Project Change Application Form" (Appendix 6). Only one延期 is permitted per project cycle.
