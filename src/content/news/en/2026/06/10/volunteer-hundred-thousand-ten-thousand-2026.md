## Proposed Admission List

According to the STU Youth League Committee, following independent applications, university recommendations, position matching, medical checks, and psychological assessments, 15 graduating students have been proposed for admission to the 2026-2027 Guangdong University Student Volunteer Service "Hundred-Thousand-Ten Thousand" special campaign.

The public comment period is from **June 10 to June 16, 2026**. Any objections should be submitted in writing or communicated in person or by phone to the Youth League Committee before 17:30 on June 16. Departmental feedback must be stamped with an official seal; individual feedback must include the signatory's real name.
