It is reported that the STU Academic Affairs Office will conduct a conclusion review of provincial and university-level undergraduate teaching quality and teaching reform projects initiated between 2022 and 2024. The review covers seven project categories: specialized talent training programs, industry-education integration practice bases, student social practice teaching bases, course teaching and research offices, virtual teaching and research offices, modern industrial colleges, and higher education teaching reform projects.

Project leaders must submit their review materials through the teaching project management platform by September 4, including project proposals, acceptance forms, supporting documentation, and research reports. The university will organize expert evaluations, and results for provincial projects will be publicly announced.

The review results will serve as a reference for future provincial quality project funding and for teaching achievement award applications at both university and provincial levels.
