It is reported that to celebrate STU's 45th anniversary, the Student Affairs Office and Admissions and Employment Office, together with China Mobile Shantou, are hosting the "Tell STU's Story — Graduation Edition" short video contest from June 20 to 30.

## How to Participate

Participants can post original short videos on WeChat Video Channel, Douyin, or Xiaohongshu with the official hashtags `#TellSTUStory #TellSTUStoryGraduation`. Works must focus on graduation themes, with graduation ceremony footage making up at least 30% of the total duration.

## Prizes

Each platform will have its own awards: 1 first prize (DJI Pocket 4 Creator Combo), 2 second prizes (Huawei MatePad Air), 3 third prizes (Huawei Watch GT6), and 20 excellence awards (STU admission souvenir set).

## Submission

From June 28 to 30, follow the "STU Student Affairs" WeChat official account and submit via "Online College" - "Material Submission."
