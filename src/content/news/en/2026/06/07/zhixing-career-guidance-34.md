It is reported that the College of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, together with Zhixing College, held the 34th Career Guidance session on the evening of June 7, 2026. Themed "Academic Planning & Exam Preparation Sharing," the event targeted the 2023 cohort of undergraduate chemistry students.

**Session Schedule**

| Segment | Speaker | Topic |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Opening Address | Prof. Fang Yiwen (Party Secretary) | Encouraging students to set realistic academic goals |
| Policy Briefing | Prof. Zhang Hefeng (Chemistry Dept. Chair) | Postgraduate exam policies, school selection strategies, and common pitfalls |
| Academic Guidance | Ji Muwei (Class Advisor) | Study paths, balancing coursework with exam prep, daily planning |
| Experience Sharing | 2026 outstanding graduates | Year-long planning, review methods, time management, interviews, application materials |

The session aimed to help students better understand innovation, entrepreneurship, and further-education trends, strengthen planning awareness, and improve practical career-readiness skills. The four speakers provided systematic guidance from the perspectives of motivation, policy, academics, and hands-on experience.
