It is reported that Country Garden Group will hold an on-campus recruitment briefing for its 2027 "Super BIYE Student Preparatory Program" on June 9 at the East Coast Campus, targeting students from the Law School, Business School, and School of Mathematics and Computer Science.

**Time & Venue**

- **Date & Time**: Tuesday, June 9, 2026, 16:00
- **Venue**: Room 511, Building E, East Coast Campus

**Open Positions**

| Role | Slots | Target School | Location |
|------|-------|---------------|----------|
| Legal Intern | 5 | Law School | Dongguan (Nancheng) |
| Admin Management Intern | 5 | Business School | Foshan (Shunde) |
| HR Intern | 5 | Business School | Foshan (Shunde) |
| Digital Management Intern | 5 | Math & CS School | Foshan (Shunde) |

All positions offer a monthly salary of RMB 2,000–3,000. Legal interns will handle full-cycle legal risk management, contract review, and litigation. Admin interns will work on corporate culture, brand management, and party-union affairs. HR interns will be responsible for人力资源管理 related tasks. Digital management interns will work on AI digital product requirement analysis, feature design, system configuration, and project management.

**Fast-Track Offer**

Selected students can begin a 1–3 month summer internship from July. Those who excel in the end-of-internship assessment will receive a direct offer for the 2027 Country Garden "Super BIYE Student" program.
