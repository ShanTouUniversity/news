It is reported that the STU Party Building Research Association and the Party Organization Department conducted the 2026 "Good Party Building Cases, Good Party Secretaries' Lectures, Good Party Member Role Models" excellence campaign. Following recommendations from secondary-level party organizations, expert review, and deliberation, a total of **26 outstanding cases**, **10 lectures**, and **22 role models** were selected.

The campaign aims to thoroughly study and implement Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, summarize grassroots party building achievements, and foster a new pattern of "exemplary leadership, innovation-driven development, and holistic improvement" in grassroots party building.
