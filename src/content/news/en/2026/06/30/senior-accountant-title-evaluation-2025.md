It is reported that Li Danyan and Zheng Hui from the Finance Office have passed the 2025 senior accountant title evaluation conducted by the Guangdong Provincial Accounting Personnel Senior Title Evaluation Committee. The results are now subject to public notice.

## Public Notice Period

**July 1 to July 7, 2026**

## Objections

Any individual or organization with objections to the listed candidates may submit a written complaint during the notice period to either of the following:

**Guangdong Senior Evaluation Committee Office**
Address: 376 Beijing Road, Guangzhou
Tel: 020-33979254, 83170315

**STU Human Resources Office**
Address: Room 213 West, Administration Building, Sangpushan Campus
Tel: 0754-86502340

Individual complaints must bear the complainant's signature and contact number; organizational complaints must be stamped with the unit's official seal.
