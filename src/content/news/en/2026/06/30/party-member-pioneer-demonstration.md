It is reported that the STU Party Organization Department has issued a decision on establishing "Party Member Pioneer Demonstration Posts" and "Party Member Pioneer Demonstration Teams." Following recommendations from secondary-level party organizations and deliberation, 47 individuals including Wang Xiaojuan and 23 teams including the "Poetry Elegance" poetry cultureoutreach team were selected as demonstration models.

The selected posts and teams are expected to fulfill their commitments and lead by example, demonstrating party members' dedication and readiness to step up at critical moments. The full list is available in the attached document.
