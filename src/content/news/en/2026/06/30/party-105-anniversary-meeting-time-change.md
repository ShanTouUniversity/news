It is reported that due to scheduling adjustments, the celebration meeting for the 105th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China and the fourth work promotion meeting on establishing and practicing a correct view of performance, originally scheduled for **July 1, 2026 (Wednesday) at 15:00**, has been rescheduled to **9:00 on the same morning**.

For any changes to the attendee list, please contact the Party and Administration Office at bgshw@stu.edu.cn. Contact: Zhuang Shaoyang, 86502877.
