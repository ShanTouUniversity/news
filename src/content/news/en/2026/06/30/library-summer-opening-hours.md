It is reported that the library has announced its summer opening hours. From July 4 to September 3, 2026, both the Sangpushan Campus and East Coast Campus libraries will be open on **Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays**, from **9:00 to 17:00**.

## Closed Areas

During the summer period, the following areas will be closed: the Special Collections Room, Reading Hall, and Seminar Rooms at the Sangpushan Campus library; and the Seminar Rooms, Quiet Pods, and first-floor Self-Study Room at the East Coast Campus library. The cross-campus book reservation service will also be suspended. The 24-hour reading area on the first-floor mezzanine terrace of the Sangpushan Campus library will remain open until 8:00 on July 18, after which it will be closed until September 3.

## Regular Hours Resume

Both campus libraries will resume normal operations on **September 4**.
