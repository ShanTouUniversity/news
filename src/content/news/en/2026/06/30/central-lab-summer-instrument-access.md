It is reported that the Central Laboratory (Analysis and Testing Center) will offer full access to all large instruments during the 2026 summer vacation to ensure uninterrupted research activities.

## Schedule

- **Service period**: July 6 to August 29, 2026 (weekdays 8:30-11:30, 15:00-17:00)
- **Maintenance**: August 31 to September 3, during which the center will be closed for instrument maintenance

## Reservations

Reservations can be made online via the STU Large Instrument Sharing Platform at [http://zxsys.stu.edu.cn](http://zxsys.stu.edu.cn), with applications submitted at least 3 working days in advance. Testing fees during the summer remain the same as during the semester.

## Notes

Users who fail to show up as scheduled without notice will be charged the full reserved time and will not be allowed to reserve the same instrument for the remainder of the summer. In case of power outages, instrument failures, or other emergencies, the center will notify affected users promptly and coordinate schedule adjustments.
