It is reported that to promote high-quality development of higher education and digital education strategies, the Center for Faculty Development is organizing teacher recommendations for the 8th National Mixed Teaching Design Innovation Competition. The competition aims to "promote construction, application, and reform through competition" for teachers conducting mixed teaching reform.

## Requirements

Teachers must have taught the selected course for at least 2 rounds in the past 5 years. Teams may consist of 1 lead teacher and up to 4 members. Courses that previously won grand or first prizes are not eligible.

## Structure

The competition has three stages: campus preliminary, national semi-finals, and national finals. STU may recommend 3 courses for the semi-finals.

## Submission

Teaching units should send the teacher information form to **o_fdea@stu.edu.cn** by **July 3**. Participants should submit materials at [http://5261sja9.mh.chaoxing.com](http://5261sja9.mh.chaoxing.com) by **August 3**. Video recording support is available from the center.
