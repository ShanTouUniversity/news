It is reported that in response to the central government's决策部署 on preventing and mitigating financial risks, the Guangdong Financial Youth Working Committee has launched an anti-illegal financial activities volunteer project, with the STU Youth League Committee relaying the notice to all students.

## Reporting Channel

Illegal financial activity leads can be reported via the WeChat mini-program "金鹰投诉举报中心" (Golden Eagle Complaint Center).

## Volunteer Participation

**Registered iVolunteer users**: Search for "防非" on the iVolunteer website ([www.izyz.org](http://www.izyz.org)) or via its WeChat Official Account, select tasks tagged "防非" published by Guangdong banking and insurance institutions, and register to participate.

**New users**: Complete registration first on the iVolunteer platform before signing up.
