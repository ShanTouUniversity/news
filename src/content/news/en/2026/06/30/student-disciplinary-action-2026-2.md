It is reported that the Student Affairs Department has issued the second disciplinary bulletin of 2026, covering two cases:

A student from the International College, AKYYEV, was found using a mobile phone to access exam-related materials during the *Chinese Writing A* exam. In accordance with the STU Student Disciplinary Regulations, the student received a **Warning**.

A student (surname Liu) from the Business School, after a dispute over coursework, published a classmate's personal information on social media platforms, causing negative impact. The student received a **Serious Warning**.

Both penalties have a duration of six months.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Department)
