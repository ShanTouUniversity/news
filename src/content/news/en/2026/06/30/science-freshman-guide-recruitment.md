It is reported that the College of Science is recruiting undergraduate orientation guides for the incoming 2026 freshmen, aimed at helping new students quickly adapt to university life through peer support.

## Requirements

- Overall GPA ranking in the top 50% of the major, with no retaken courses
- Strong communication, coordination, and teamwork skills
- Under equal conditions, priority given to Party members, Youth League members, student leaders, and active volunteers

## Responsibilities

Contact and assist incoming freshmen before registration; help with material distribution and check-in guidance during orientation; support new students in adapting to campus life; and assist counselors with other tasks.

## Registration

Interested students should join the freshman guide consultation group by **July 7**. Guide work hours will be counted toward iVolunteer service hours.
