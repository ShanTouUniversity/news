It is reported that the Business School will host the second session of the Management Science and Engineering Discipline Building Series, featuring Professor Guan Xu from the School of Management, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, who will speak on *Data Opacity and Privacy Regulation*.

## Lecture Details

- **Speaker**: Professor Guan Xu (HUST)
- **Host**: Professor Li Baixun
- **Time**: July 1, 2026, 9:00
- **Platform**: Tencent Meeting 805-934-3787

## Abstract

The growing reliance on data collection and monetization in business models has heightened public privacy concerns and regulatory scrutiny. The lecture focuses on a fundamental yet underexplored issue—information opacity, where consumers cannot observe or verify firms' data handling practices. By comparing three regulatory regimes—no regulation, transparency regulation, and prohibition regulation—the study explores the economic effects and policy implications of privacy regulation.

## About the Speaker

Professor Guan Xu is a chair professor at HUST's School of Management and a recipient of the National Outstanding Youth Science Fund. His research focuses on supply chain management, operations-marketing interface, information design, and platform economy. He has published in leading journals including *Management Science*, *Marketing Science*, *Manufacturing and Service Operations Management*, *Information Systems Research*, and *Production and Operations Management*.
