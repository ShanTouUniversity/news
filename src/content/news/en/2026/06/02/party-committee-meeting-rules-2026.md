## Overview

Approved at the Party Committee meeting on May 19, 2026, the **Meeting Rules of Procedure** (Document No. 2026-3) have been formally issued, comprising 19 articles.

## Key Provisions

Based on the CPC Constitution and relevant regulations for higher education Party organizations, the rules include:

**Decision-Making Principle**
- Collective leadership combined with individual division of responsibility; major issues require "collective leadership, democratic centralism, individual deliberation, and meeting decision."

**Scope (13 Categories)**
- Implementing higher-level directives, comprehensive Party governance, Party-building planning, grassroots organization setup/election, member development/management, cadre work, internal awards, anti-corruption, and trade union/youth league affairs.

**Meeting Requirements**
- Typically monthly (or as needed), convened by the secretary (or deputy).
- **Majority** of members present for regular meetings; **two-thirds** for major decisions.

**Voting**
- Oral, show of hands, or secret ballot; passage requires **majority** of all members.
- Recusal required when议题 involves the member or their relatives.

**Implementation**
- Decisions enforced by designated members or units, with oversight and evaluation mechanisms; non-compliance may result in accountability measures.
