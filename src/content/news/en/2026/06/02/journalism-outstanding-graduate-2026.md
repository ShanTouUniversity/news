## Overview

The Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication has launched the **Outstanding Graduate** selection for the class of 2026, aimed at recognizing graduates with excellent academic and personal qualities.

## Eligibility

- Good conduct, no disciplinary violations
- Excellent academic record: **two or more** second-class or above academic scholarships, or two or more university-level专项 scholarships
- **No course retakes**, excellent thesis score
- Active participation in social practice, public welfare, and volunteer activities

## How to Apply

- **Voluntary application**; submit the completed form
- **Deadline**: June 7, 2026, 24:00
- Send to o_xwxytw@stu.edu.cn with subject "Outstanding Graduate + Major + Name"
- Reviewed by the college selection committee

## Awards

- Quota: within **3%** of the year's graduates
- Prize: **1,000 RMB** per recipient, along with honorary title and certificate
