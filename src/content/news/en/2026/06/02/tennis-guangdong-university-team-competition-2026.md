The 2026 Guangdong University Tennis Team Competition was held in Zhongshan from May 23 to 29. Coached by Geng Ruiyuan and Ou Yuanping, the STU tennis team placed **7th in the Men's Team A Division A** among top university teams from across the province.

Team members included students from the College of Engineering, the Business School, and the School of Mathematics and Computer Science.
