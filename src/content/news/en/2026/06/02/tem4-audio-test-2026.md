## Audio Test Arrangement

To ensure TEM4 examinees can receive listening signals properly, the Academic Affairs Office will hold an audio testing session:

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Time | **June 10 (Wednesday), 14:30 – 17:00** |
| Venue | **Building E** (at each examinee's assigned exam room) |
| Frequency | **FM 73.2** (same as CET-4/6 audio test sessions) |

Examinees must bring their own headphones and test their equipment at the designated location.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Academic Affairs Office)
