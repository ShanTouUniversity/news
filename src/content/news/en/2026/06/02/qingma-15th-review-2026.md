## Overview

The 15th session of STU's Young Marxists (Qingma) Training Program for Party applicants has completed all training modules. The program kicked off on April 11 simultaneously at Sangpu Campus and Donghai'an Campus.

## Closing Ceremony

On May 23 at 15:00, the closing ceremony was held at Donghai'an E105 (main venue) and Sangpu Campus (simultaneous venue). Temporary Party branches presented their learning outcomes through red songs, stage plays, and poetry readings on themes including national security, Two Sessions spirit, and Party history.

Deputy Party Secretary Li Liting praised participants' dedication and urged them to strengthen ideals and convictions, becoming well-rounded young leaders for the new era.
