## Overview

The Law School and Gezhi College are co-hosting the 5th "**Youth Voice**" (青声) Law Student Party Member Forum under the theme "**Legal Foundation for the 15th Five-Year Plan, Youth Voices for Good Policies**," aimed at guiding students to understand the national plan and explore paths in legal practice.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Time | **June 3, 2026 (Wednesday), 15:00 – 16:30** |
| Venue | **Building E, Room 325, Donghai'an Campus** |
| Participants | Law undergrad party branch members, applicants, development targets, and student representatives |

## Program

1. Opening remarks
2. Guided theory study & roundtable discussion
3. Rule-of-law practice micro-interviews
4. Mock CPPCC proposal exercise
5. Group photo

## Registration & Notes

- Register via the law undergrad party branch (see branch group for details)
- The forum is part of the party member development evaluation system
- Arrive **15 minutes early** for sign-in; keep phones on silent; observe political discipline
