## Exam Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date & Time | **June 14, 2026 (Sunday), 8:30 AM** (approx. 130 min) |
| Entry | From **8:00 AM**; no entry after **8:25 AM** |
| Audio test | No separate session; use CET-4/6 audio test instead |
| Required documents | **Exam admission ticket + ID card + Student card** |
| Listening equipment | Self-provided headphones |

## Test Structure

| Part | Component | Time | Answer Sheet |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|
| I | Dictation | ~10 min | Sheet 1 |
| II | Listening Comprehension | ~20 min | Section A → Sheet 1; Section B → Sheet 2 |
| III | Language Usage | 10 min | Sheet 2 |
| IV | Cloze | 10 min | Sheet 2 |
| V | Reading Comprehension | 35 min | Sheet 2 |
| VI | Writing | 45 min | Sheet 3 |

**Sheet 1** is collected immediately after Part II; **Sheet 2** is collected after Part V.

## Key Rules

- **Multiple-choice**: use 2B pencil; **Dictation, short-answer, writing**: use black pen (0.5mm or above)
- Answers must stay within the designated frames
- No mobile phones, dictionaries (including electronic), or correction fluid allowed
- May leave exam hall after **60 minutes**; leavers are supervised in a designated rest area (no phone use)
- **No leaving** during the final 30 minutes
- Late submission results in score cancellation
