## Overview

The College of Science Party Committee is conducting the 2026 selection of **Outstanding Party Members** and **Outstanding Party Workers** among student party branch formal members to recognize exemplary conduct. Recipients from the past two years are generally ineligible.

## Quota & Eligibility

| Award | Quota | Key Conditions |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| Outstanding Party Member | Within **15%** of formal party members | Undergrads: top 30% in comprehensive evaluation; Grads: at least one second-class scholarship in 2 years |
| Outstanding Party Worker | **1 per branch** | Strong party affairs capability, notable contributions, high student recognition |

## Timeline

- **June 8**: Submit recommendation summary to yding@stu.edu.cn
- Review by the College Party Committee; shortlist published for 5 working days

Contact: Ding Yuan, 0754-86502919.

> ⚠️ The recommendation summary form is available on the OA system.
