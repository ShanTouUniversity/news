With five days to go until the 2026 Gaokao, the STU Medical College Admissions Office posted a mindset adjustment guide on its WeChat official account to encourage exam candidates.

The guide emphasizes that "steadying your mindset is the key to success — more important than burning the midnight oil," helping students relieve anxiety and stay calm before the exam.

The college closed with a message for test-takers: "We wish every candidate a victorious start and a fulfilled outcome. STU Medical College is here waiting for you!"
