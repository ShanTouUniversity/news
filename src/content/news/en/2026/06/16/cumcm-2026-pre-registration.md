It is reported that the Mathematical Modeling Competition Coaching Team of the School of Mathematics and Computer Science has opened pre-registration for the 2026 China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling (CUMCM) for all undergraduate students.

**Registration Requirements**

- Teams of 3 members, each specializing in mathematical modeling, software programming, or academic writing
- No team leader designation; certificate order is independent of contribution level

**Schedule**

- Pre-registration deadline: July 17
- Training: August 17 – September 4
- Competition: September 10 (18:00) – September 13 (20:00)

**How to Register**

Download the registration form and send it to Professor Fang Rui at rfang@stu.edu.cn. One representative per team should submit. A confirmation reply will be sent within one working day. Instructors will be assigned after registration.
