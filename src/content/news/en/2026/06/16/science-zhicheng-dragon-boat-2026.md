It is reported that the School of Science and Zhicheng College will host a Dragon Boat Festival themed event on June 17 from 15:00 to 17:00 at the Zhicheng College 1F Lobby.

**Old Times, New Home**

Graduates can donate clean books, electronics, and stationery items. Attendees can pick up items for free and write thank-you cards or give handmade gifts in return.

**Festival Garden Booths**

- **Blessing Sachet Making**: Hand-sew traditional Dragon Boat Festival sachets
- **Fan Painting**: Paint Dragon Boat patterns or graduation messages on classical round fans
- **Five Poisons Archery**: Toss arrows representing the "five poisons" into a container; 5+ hits wins a zongzi
- **Bracelet Weaving**: Weave colorful silk threads into lucky bracelets
- **Bamboo Wind Chimes**: Craft bamboo wind chimes with message tags
- **Message Board**: Write blessings on bookmarks and display them
