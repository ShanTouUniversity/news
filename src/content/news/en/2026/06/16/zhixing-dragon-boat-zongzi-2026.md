It is reported that Zhixing College and the School of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering will host a Dragon Boat Festival themed event on June 19, offering blessings to students facing final exams and graduates preparing for career or further studies.

**Cultural Lecture**

Time: 16:30–17:00
Venue: Zhixing College, 2F Multi-function Hall
Content: The origins of Dragon Boat Festival, historical development, Qu Yuan's story, and traditional customs across northern and southern China.

**Zongzi Tasting**

Time: 17:00–18:00
Venue: Zhixing College, 1F Lobby
Content: A variety of freshly made zongzi for students to enjoy.

**Registration**

Limited spots available. Scan the QR code to join the activity group by June 18 at 18:00. Participants will receive activity credits.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Chemistry and Chemical Engineering College, Zhixing College)
