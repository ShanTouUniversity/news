It is reported that the Institute of Mathematics will host the 951st session of the "Towards Modern Mathematics" academic lecture series on June 25 at 10:30 at East Coast Campus D-209, featuring Dr. Liao Qianfen from Guangdong University of Education.

**Lecture Details**

- Topic: Generalized Cayley Graphs of Complete Groups
- Time: June 25, 10:30
- Venue: East Coast Campus, D-209
- Invited by: Dr. Lin Qingze

**Abstract**

This talk focuses on the generalized Cayley graphs of complete groups. It characterizes two types of isomorphisms between such generalized Cayley graphs and establishes necessary and sufficient conditions for a complete group to be a restricted GCI group, completing the classification of restricted GCI groups within the class of symmetric groups.

**About the Speaker**

Liao Qianfen earned her PhD from the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Central South University in 2024. She is currently a faculty member at Guangdong University of Education, specializing in group theory and combinatorics. Her research has been published in journals including *Scientia Sinica*, *Advances in Mathematics*, *Bull. Malays. Math. Sci. Soc.*, and *Appl. Math. Comput.*
