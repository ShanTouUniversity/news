It is reported that the Guangdong Education Science Planning Project (Higher Education Special) for 2026 is now accepting applications. STU has a quota of 20 projects, each with minimum funding of 10,000 yuan.

**Research Topics**

- Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics
- National Education Conference
- High-Quality Education Development
- Higher Education Science

**Quota Distribution**

Marxism School: 5 projects; other humanities schools: 2 each; natural science schools: 1 each.

**Requirements**

- Applicants must be under 55 (born after June 30, 1971)
- Hold a mid-level title or higher, or a doctoral degree
- One application per person; no concurrent applications for active provincial education science projects

**Timeline**

- Jun 22: Submit materials to o_wkb@stu.edu.cn
- Jun 25: Submit paper application to Research Office
- Jun 26: Upload to online system

Contact: Huang Yumei, Wang Ruimin, 86502563.
