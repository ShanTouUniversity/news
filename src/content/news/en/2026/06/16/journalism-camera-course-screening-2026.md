It is reported that the Cheung Kong School of Journalism and Communication will host a public screening of works from the Video Production course on June 18 from 16:00 to 17:40 at East Coast Campus E133, themed "A Dialogue Across Time and Space."

The featured works are documentary explorations centered on "a profession," capturing the dedication found in ordinary jobs. All students and faculty are welcome. Register via the "到梦空间" APP for activity credits.
