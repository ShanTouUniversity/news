It is reported that the STU Labor Union and Youth League Committee have jointly launched the 2026 "6·30" fundraising campaign to support rural revitalization, open to all faculty and students on a voluntary basis.

**2025 Fund Usage**

The 2025 campaign raised 49,534 yuan, which was fully allocated to the "Second Nan'ao Public Art Season and the 9th STU Public Art Exhibition," a key project in Nan'ao County covered by national media.

**Donation Arrangements**

- Faculty: Donations to department labor unions by June 30
- Students: Collected by colleges, submitted to the Youth League Committee by June 30

Contact: Teacher Ye, 13729296166.
