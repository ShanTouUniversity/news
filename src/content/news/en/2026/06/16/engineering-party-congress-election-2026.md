It is reported that the Engineering College will hold a Party Congress on June 17 at 14:00 in the Sangpu Mountain Campus Science and Technology Lecture Hall to elect a new committee, following approval from the university Party Committee.

**Participants**

All Engineering College party members (probationary members and non-party representatives attend as non-voting observers).

**Requirements**

- A quorum of at least four-fifths of eligible voters is required for the election
- Attendees must wear party badges, dress formally, and arrive 15 minutes early for check-in
- Absences require prior written approval

Party branches must submit absence reports to yinhaoli@stu.edu.cn by June 17 at 11:00.
