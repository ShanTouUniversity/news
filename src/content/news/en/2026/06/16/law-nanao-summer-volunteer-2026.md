It is reported that the Law School and Gezhi College, together with the Nan'ao County Youth League Committee, will launch a summer youth companionship social practice program, recruiting volunteers from the Law School.

**Program Details**

- Time: July 13–17 (tentative)
- Location: Houzhai Town Primary School, Nan'ao County
- Recruitment: 8 students, preference for Chaoshan natives
- Content: Learning support, legal education, and safety education for youth

**Notes**

- Round-trip transportation and accommodation will be provided
- Team members must form a project突击队 and partner with Nan'ao County
- All activities must be conducted under teacher guidance
