It is reported that the School of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering and Zhixing College will launch the 11th session of the Zhixing Education Peer Tutoring series. This session focuses on final exam preparation, with senior students offering guidance before exams.

**Session Details**

- Time: June 2026 (see attachment for detailed schedule)
- Venue: Zhixing College, 1F Academic Development Support Room, and online
- Participants: Chemistry College and Zhixing College students

**Subjects Covered**

Organic Chemistry I, Calculus B-II, Polymer Chemistry and Physics, Air Pollution Control Engineering, Physical Chemistry I, Fluid Mechanics, Materials Science Research and Testing Methods, Environmental Engineering Principles II, Fundamentals of Materials Science

**Registration**

Scan the QR code to join the WeChat group by June 17. Suggestions can be sent to o_zxsy@stu.edu.cn.
