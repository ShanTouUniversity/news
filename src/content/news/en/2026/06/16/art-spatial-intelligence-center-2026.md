It is reported that the Cheung Kong School of Art and Design has officially established the Spatial Intelligence Research Center, following a decision by the college's Party and administrative joint meeting.

The center aims to strengthen the college's support for national strategies, serve the university's new liberal arts construction and high-quality development of design disciplines, and promote integration of art design, artificial intelligence, urban spatial governance, and local services. It will build on the college's strengths in spatial twin, spatial perception, behavior analysis, and long-term empirical research in real-world scenarios.
