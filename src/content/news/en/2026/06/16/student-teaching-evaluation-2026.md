It is reported that the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Assessment has launched the end-of-semester undergraduate student teaching evaluation. All students enrolled this semester must complete the evaluation by July 3 via the teaching quality platform (desktop: http://quality.stu.edu.cn, mobile: http://quality.stu.edu.cn:81).

The evaluation is anonymous. Students who do not complete the evaluation will be unable to view course grades. Service-learning courses require multiple evaluation types.
