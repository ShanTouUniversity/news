It is reported that the municipal flood control headquarters downgraded the emergency response from Level III to Level IV as of 21:00 on June 15. While the rain-bearing cloud system affecting the city has weakened, precipitation and severe convective weather are still expected in the next three days.

24-hour emergency contact: Sangpu Campus 0754-86504110, East Coast Campus 0754-86500110. Property service hotline: Sangpu Campus 0754-86502661, East Coast Campus 0754-86500113.
