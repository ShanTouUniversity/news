It is reported that the Ministry of Education's Social Science Department has launched the 2026 Humanities and Social Sciences Special Task Project on the Theory of Socialism with Chinese Characteristics. Each project receives 100,000 yuan in funding with a 2-year research period.

**Requirements**

- Applicants must hold a doctoral degree or mid-level professional title
- One application per person
- Not eligible: those with active MOE humanities projects, NSFC, or NSSFC projects; those who applied for MOE general projects in both 2024 and 2025 without success

**Expected Outcomes**

- At least 2 academic articles in major newspapers or high-level journals
- Research reports adopted by provincial/ministerial authorities or recognized teaching cases
