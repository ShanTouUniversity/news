It is reported that the Cheung Kong Art & Design College and Dexin College will host a Dragon Boat Festival themed event on June 18 evening at the East Coast Campus, featuring two activities.

**Activity 1: Sachet-Making Workshop**

Time: June 18, 19:00
Location: East Coast Campus, E325 Round Table Discussion Room
Content: Make traditional Dragon Boat Festival sachets by hand, experiencing material selection, filling, stitching, and tassel tying
Registration: Sign up via shared document, limited to 45 on a first-come basis

**Activity 2: Dragon Boat Themed Werewolf Game**

Time: June 18, 19:30
Location: East Coast Campus, E325 Round Table Discussion Room
Content: A Werewolf game adapted with Qu Yuan's story and Dragon Boat Festival folklore elements
Registration: Scan QR code to join the group

> Source: STU OA Notice (Cheung Kong Art & Design College, Dexin College)
