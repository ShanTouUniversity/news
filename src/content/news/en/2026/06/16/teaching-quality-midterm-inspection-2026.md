It is reported that the Academic Affairs Office will conduct mid-term inspections on provincial and university-level undergraduate teaching quality and reform projects that were initiated in 2024 and are still in progress.

**Scope**

Provincial-level teaching quality and reform projects initiated in 2024, and university-level quality projects initiated in 2024 that are still ongoing.

**Materials Required**

- Mid-term inspection form (PDF)
- Supporting evidence of project成果 (consolidated into one PDF)

**Deadline**

Project leaders must submit materials by September 11 via the teaching project management platform (http://project.stu.edu.cn). College platform administrators must complete their review by the same date.

Contact: Zhou Ziyang, zyzhou@stu.edu.cn, 86502736.
