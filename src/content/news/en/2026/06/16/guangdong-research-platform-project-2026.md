It is reported that the 2026 Guangdong University research platform and project application process has opened. The Research Office has released the notice covering 6 subcategories across research platforms and projects.

**Research Platforms**

| Category | Quota | Funding |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Key Laboratory | 1 | ≥500,000 yuan |
| New Think Tank | 1 | ≥300,000 yuan |

**Research Projects**

| Category | Quota | Funding |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Innovation Team (Natural Sciences) | 1 | ≥300,000 yuan |
| Innovation Team (Humanities) | 1 | ≥150,000 yuan |
| Key Areas (Basic Research/Future Tech) | ≤8 | ≥300,000 yuan |
| Key Areas (Humanities) | ≤1 | ≥50,000 yuan |
| Key Areas (百千万工程) | ≤2 | ≥100,000 yuan |
| Featured Innovation / Youth Talent (Humanities) | ≤10 total | ≥5,000 yuan |

**Timeline**

- Jun 24: Submit applications to college
- Jun 29: University selection
- Jul 6: Submit paper applications
- Jul 7: Upload signed documents

Contacts: Zhu Changsheng, Chen Jinshan (86502813, natural sciences); Huang Yumei, Wang Ruimin (86502563, humanities).
