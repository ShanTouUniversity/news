It is reported that the Admissions and Employment Office has announced the centralized file archiving schedule for 2026 undergraduate graduates.

**Schedule by College**

| Date | Session | College |
|------|---------|---------|
| Jun 29 | AM | School of Science |
| Jun 29 | PM | College of Arts |
| Jun 30 | AM | Business School |
| Jun 30 | PM | Journalism, Public Health |
| Jul 1 | AM | Math & CS |
| Jul 1 | PM | Chemistry & Chemical Engineering |
| Jul 2 | AM | Law School |
| Jul 2 | PM | Art & Design |
| Jul 3 | All day | Engineering College |

**Location:** Admin Center 233, Admissions and Employment Office

Colleges must submit archival materials by June 23 and report staff assignments by June 18.
