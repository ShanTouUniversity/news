According to the School of Journalism and Communication, the 2025-2026 "Changhua Practice Scholarship" selection process has been completed. After student applications, college-level review, and a public posting period, 20 students were selected as recipients.

Each award carries a subsidy of 3,000 RMB, with an additional 2,000 RMB for students from economically disadvantaged families.
