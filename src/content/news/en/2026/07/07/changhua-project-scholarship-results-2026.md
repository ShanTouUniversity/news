According to the School of Journalism and Communication, the 2025-2026 "Changhua Project Scholarship" selection has been completed. Following applications from project leaders, review by the college selection committee, and a public posting period, three projects were selected for funding. Each project receives a subsidy of 7,000 RMB.

| No. | Project Title | Subsidy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | "Retracing the Route of Northwest China" (Sichuan) | 7,000 RMB |
| 2 | "Retracing the Route of Northwest China" (Inner Mongolia) | 7,000 RMB |
| 3 | "Survey on Fertility Concepts of Young Women in the Chaoshan Region" | 7,000 RMB |
