According to the Party Committee of the School of Arts and Design at Shantou University, 12 comrades — Yin Xueting, Yuan XM, Zhang Yue, Du XQ, Gao WX, Huang ZX, Wang ML, Luo XY, Wu QQ, Fang ZY, Lyu XY, and Zhao BZ — have been approved for regularization as full members of the Communist Party of China, following the completion of their probationary periods.
