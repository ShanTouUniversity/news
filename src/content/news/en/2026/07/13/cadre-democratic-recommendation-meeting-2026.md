It is reported that the Shantou University Party Committee will hold a democratic recommendation meeting for cadres at 3:30 p.m. on Tuesday, July 14, 2026, to conduct research interviews and meeting recommendations for deputy (deputy division-level) positions at the Medical College's affiliated hospitals. The meeting will be held at the First Affiliated Hospital of the Medical College and the Cancer Hospital Affiliated to the Medical College.

Attendees will include the liaison university leaders; members of the Medical College Party Committee and Discipline Inspection Committee; members of the Medical College's leadership team; and members of the leadership teams of the First Affiliated Hospital and the affiliated Cancer Hospital, along with mid-level cadres at the rank of principal officer or above.

The Party Committee Organization Department has asked the relevant hospitals to submit the list of attendees to the Organization Department (email: o_zzb@stu.edu.cn) by 5:00 p.m. on Monday, July 13. Attendees are asked to arrive on time and, given the recommendation tasks, to bring their own fountain pen, gel pen, or ballpoint pen.
