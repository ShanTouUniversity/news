The **Centre for Arts Education** at Shantou University will present the 2026 **Mountain and Sea Music Festival** — the **"Yin Wei Ai" Faculty-Student Concert** — on Sunday evening, July 26, at the university's **Science Lecture Hall** on the Sangpu Shan campus. Piano instructor **Sun Yumin** and violin instructor **Shang Yibin** will perform alongside students, covering a diverse range of musical styles.

## Performance Highlights

Audiences will be transported through varied musical landscapes by the interplay of piano and strings, encountering intimate solo performances as well as ensemble pieces. Against the backdrop of a clear summer night sky, the concert offers an opportunity to experience classic compositions, feel the warmth contained within music, and witness each performer's passion for artistic growth.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Date & Time** | Sunday, July 26, 2026, 19:30 |
| **Venue** | Science Lecture Hall, Shantou University Sangpu Shan Campus |
| **Admission** | Open to all faculty, staff, and students. Please queue orderly at the entrance. |

## Concert Etiquette

1. Arrive at least **30 minutes before the concert begins**; latecomers must wait until a suitable intermission between pieces.
2. After the concert starts, switch mobile phones and other electronic devices to silent or off mode.
3. Do not talk, walk around, or make unnecessary noise during performances.
4. Refrain from leaving your seat mid-performance unless there is an emergency.
5. Food, drinks, and smoking are strictly prohibited inside the lecture hall.

All are welcome to attend.
