It is reported that the Student Affairs Office has released Study Atmosphere Inspection Bulletin No. 628. During week 13 of the Spring 2026 semester, the inspection team of the Sangpushan Campus checked the Medical College Teaching Center and Teaching Building D on May 25 (Monday) at 14:00 and May 26 (Tuesday) at 10:00.

The results showed no violations of school rules on the Sangpushan Campus.

The Student Affairs Office urged all colleges to continue strengthening study atmosphere management. Details are available in the attachment.
