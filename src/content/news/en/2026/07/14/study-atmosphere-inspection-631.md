It is reported that the Student Affairs Office has released Study Atmosphere Inspection Bulletin No. 631. During week 16 of the Spring 2026 semester, the inspection teams of the Donghai'an Campus and the Sangpushan Campus each checked Teaching Building E on their respective campuses at 14:00 on June 15, 2026 (Monday).

The results showed that one student arrived late within 5 minutes at Teaching Building E on the Donghai'an Campus, while no violations of school rules were found at Teaching Building E on the Sangpushan Campus.

The Student Affairs Office bulletined the list of students who failed to observe school rules and urged all colleges to continue strengthening study atmosphere management. The detailed list is available in the attachment.
