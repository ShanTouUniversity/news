It is reported that the Student Affairs Office has released Study Atmosphere Inspection Bulletin No. 629. During week 14 of the Spring 2026 semester, the inspection team of the Donghai'an Campus checked Teaching Building E on June 1 (Monday) and June 3 (Wednesday) at 14:00.

The results showed that two students arrived more than 5 minutes late at Teaching Building E on the Donghai'an Campus.

The Student Affairs Office bulletined the list of students who failed to observe school rules and urged all colleges to continue strengthening study atmosphere management. The detailed list is available in the attachment.
