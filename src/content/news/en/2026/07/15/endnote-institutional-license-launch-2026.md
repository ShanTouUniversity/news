It is reported that the Shantou University Library has officially launched the institutional edition of the EndNote reference management software, which is now available for download by all faculty and students to support teaching and research.

Developed by Clarivate, EndNote is a professional reference management tool that integrates literature search, analysis, management, paper writing, and journal submission. It is an important tool for researchers to organize references, standardize citations, and efficiently complete academic writing.

## Key Features

- Seamless integration with Web of Science for quickly retrieving literature and building a personal reference database
- Effective management and analysis of references
- Citing references while writing, with one-click adjustment of citation formats
- Cross-platform integration and synchronization of references
- Sharing references with multiple users for team collaboration

## How to Download

Faculty and students can obtain the software via the path "STU Library Homepage / Services / Equipment and Software / Software Download," logging in with their campus network account and password. For the Windows version, the installation package must first be extracted into a single folder, and Office-related programs should be closed during installation; users may refer to the attached user guide when choosing between the Chinese and English versions.

The software is subscribed to and managed by the STU Medical College Library. For any questions during use, contact Ms./Mr. Chen at the Medical College Library at 0754-88900471-803 or chenruixiong@stu.edu.cn.

The library reminds users that the software is for teaching and research use by the university's faculty and students only. Distributing the software or related license information to any individual or organization outside the university is strictly prohibited; any economic loss or legal dispute arising from unauthorized distribution shall be borne by the violator.
