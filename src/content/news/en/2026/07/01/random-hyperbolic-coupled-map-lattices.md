It is reported that the Institute of Mathematics will host the 957th session of the "Towards Modern Mathematics" academic report series, featuring Dr. Zhang Ziyao from Xiamen University, who will speak on equilibrium states of random hyperbolic coupled map lattices.

## Lecture Details

- **Speaker**: Dr. Zhang Ziyao (Xiamen University)
- **Invited by**: Prof. Li Jian
- **Time**: July 2, 2026, 15:00
- **Venue**: East Coast Campus, D Building, Room 209

## Abstract

The report introduces equilibrium states of random hyperbolic coupled map lattices, covering: structural stability of such systems on random hyperbolic sets and equicontinuous Hölder continuity of topological conjugacies; properties of action-based equilibrium states in random dynamical systems, particularly the equivalence between equilibrium states and Gibbs states; and sufficient conditions for the existence and uniqueness of equilibrium states under specific conditions.

## About the Speaker

Dr. Zhang Ziyao earned his PhD from Xiamen University and specializes in differential dynamical systems and ergodic theory. His research has been published in *Acta Mathematica Sinica-English Series* and *Discrete and Continuous Dynamical Systems, Series S*, among other journals.
