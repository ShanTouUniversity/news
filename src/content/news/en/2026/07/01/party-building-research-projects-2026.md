The STU Party Building Research Association organized the 2026 Party building research project application under the theme "Using the Correct Performance Concept to Improve the Quality and Efficiency of Grassroots Party Building in Universities." A total of 58 applications were received. Following expert review and discussion by the Party Organization Department, **10 projects** were approved for funding.

## Funding and Administration

- Each approved project receives **3,000 yuan** in research funding
- Approved projects are managed as university-level humanities and social science research projects
- They will be recommended as key research projects of the Shantou City Party Building Research Association for 2026

## Submission Requirements

Each project team must submit the completion form and research report **by June 30, 2027** to the STU Party Building Research Association (email: o_dxb@stu.edu.cn).
