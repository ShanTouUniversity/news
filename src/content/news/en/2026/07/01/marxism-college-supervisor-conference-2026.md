To advance the quality of graduate education, the School of Marxism will hold its first Master's Supervisor Conference on **July 4, 2026 (Saturday) at 9:00 AM** at the **Sangpu Campus Academic Exchange Center**.

## Key Agenda

- **Degree Program Development**: Review current foundation, goals, and planning for master's degree programs; analyze opportunities and challenges; outline next-stage priorities
- **Curriculum Development**: Assess full-time academic and part-time professional master's curricula; discuss course system optimization, content updates, and practical teaching quality improvement
- **Graduate Admissions**: Analyze the current admissions landscape; deploy recruitment and promotional tasks; mobilize supervisors to enhance student quality

## Attendance

All master's supervisors of the School of Marxism are required to attend. Written leave applications must be submitted to the Graduate Office **by July 3**. Participants should prepare relevant suggestions in advance for discussion.
