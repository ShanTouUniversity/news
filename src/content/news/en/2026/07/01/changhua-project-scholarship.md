It is reported that the College of Journalism and Communication has launched the selection for the 2025-2026 academic year Changhua Project Scholarship. The scholarship was established by Changzhou Huaren Chemical Co., Ltd. to enhance teaching and student practice levels and encourage diligent study. In 2026, the company added an additional 100,000 yuan in donations to reward outstanding students at the college.

## Scholarship Categories

The Changhua Scholarship consists of three categories:
- **Changhua Encouragement Scholarship**: For financially disadvantaged students who demonstrate diligence and all-round development
- **Changhua Practice Scholarship**: Supporting the Beijing internship program's practical teaching projects
- **Changhua Project Scholarship**: Supporting students in college project cultivation, the "Challenge Cup," and the China International College Students' Innovation Competition

This selection is specifically for the Changhua Project Scholarship.

## Eligibility

Projects must be recognized as key cultivation projects in the college's development plan, with satisfactory progress and achieved outcomes or milestones. Applications must be submitted by the student project leader on behalf of the project.

Students are disqualified if they: violate laws or university regulations; engage in wasteful behavior or violate social ethics with negative impact; or submit false or fabricated application materials.

## How to Apply

Student project leaders should complete the Changhua Project Scholarship application form and send it to **o_xwxytw@stu.edu.cn** by **12:00, July 2, 2026**. The email subject and attachment archive should be named "Name + Changhua Project Scholarship Application Materials."
