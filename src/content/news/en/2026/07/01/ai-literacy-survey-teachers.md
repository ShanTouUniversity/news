It is reported that the Center for Faculty Development and Educational Evaluation is conducting a survey on teachers' artificial intelligence (AI) literacy and their training needs for AI-empowered education and teaching. The survey aims to assess teachers' overall AI literacy level and the challenges they face, helping the university build a more tailored training system.

## Scope and Timeline

The survey covers all faculty members and runs from **July 1 to July 10, 2026**.

## How to Participate

The survey is conducted online. Teachers can complete the questionnaire via the following link:

[https://v.wjx.cn/vm/YrLksEZ.asp](https://v.wjx.cn/vm/YrLksEZ.asp)

## Notes

The survey is anonymous and does not collect any personal information beyond the response content. All teaching units are encouraged to mobilize their faculty to actively participate, ensuring the reliability and validity of the results.
