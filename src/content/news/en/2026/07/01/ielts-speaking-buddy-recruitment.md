It is reported that the Language Self-Access Center (CILL) is recruiting tutors for its IELTS Speaking Buddy service for the 2026-2027 Fall semester. The service aims to provide personalized IELTS speaking diagnosis and mock practice for fellow students.

## Position Details

Tutors are required to conduct one 2-hour IELTS speaking tutoring session per week at CILL (Sangpushan Campus), including one-on-one mock practice, test-taking tips, and personalized learning advice. Compensation follows the university's work-study standards and is paid monthly. Tutors who successfully complete one semester of service will receive an official certificate from CILL.

## Eligibility

Applicants who meet any of the following criteria are welcome:
- Scored 6 or above on the IELTS speaking section in recent years
- English major with TEM-4 "Excellent" or TEM-8 "Good" or higher
- Previously reached the provincial or national stage of the "FLTRP·ETIC Cup" or "Understanding Contemporary China" national foreign language proficiency competition

## How to Apply

Complete the registration questionnaire at [https://v.wjx.cn/vm/eISWI8L.aspx](https://v.wjx.cn/vm/eISWI8L.aspx) by **July 9, 2026**. Applications will be pre-screened, and shortlisted candidates will be notified for interviews.

For inquiries, contact Ms. Bai at xianbai@stu.edu.cn.
