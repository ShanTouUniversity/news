It is reported that the English Language Center of the College of Arts held the STU campus selection for the 9th SFLEP Cup National College Students' Intercultural Competence Competition from May 10 to June 28. The results have been announced following a rigorous evaluation by the organizing committee.

## Award Winners

**Grand Prize**
- Law, Sun JL Team (Sun JL, Xu L, Liu ZY)

**First Prize**
- Arts, Yuan YL
- Mathematics and Computer Science, He JR

**Second Prize**
- Arts, Wu KY Team (Wu KY, Zhang YZ, Zhou YJ)
- Engineering, Chen X Team (Chen X, Meng DP, Jiang DZ)
- Engineering, Luo CD
- Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, Pan YT

**Third Prize**
- Business, Ji YC Team (Ji YC, Jia BX)
- Law, Wu ZX Team (Wu ZX, Li Y, Liu XY)
- Arts, Chen S
- Arts, Ke YQ
- Arts, Chen JY
- Arts, Li JM

Sun JL (Law), Yuan YL (Arts), and He JR (Mathematics and Computer Science) will represent Shantou University at the provincial competition.

## Certificate Collection

Award winners may collect their certificates from **September 10 to September 30, 2026** at Room 102 of the English Language Center, College of Arts.
