It is reported that the College of Journalism and Communication has launched the selection for the 2025-2026 academic year Changhua Practice Scholarship. This practice-oriented award is part of the Changhua Scholarship established by Changzhou Huaren Chemical Co., Ltd. to support the Beijing internship program's practical teaching projects. In 2026, the company added a total of 100,000 yuan in donations to reward outstanding students at the college.

## Eligibility

Applicants must be members of the Beijing internship program, demonstrate diligent study with satisfactory grades and no failed subjects, abide by university regulations, and actively participate in college activities and social practice with a spirit of service and dedication.

Students are disqualified if they: violate laws or university regulations; engage in wasteful behavior or violate social ethics with negative impact; or submit false or fabricated application materials.

## Selection Notes

This selection is open to members of the 2026 Beijing internship program. Under equal conditions, the evaluation committee will prioritize students from financially disadvantaged families and offer them a relatively higher scholarship amount.

## How to Apply

Interested applicants should complete the application form and send it to **o_xwxytw@stu.edu.cn** by **12:00, July 2, 2026**. The email subject and attachment archive should be named "Name + Changhua Practice Scholarship Application Materials."
