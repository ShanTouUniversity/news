According to the STU Graduate School, in accordance with the *Shantou University Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Evaluation Measures*, five outstanding doctoral dissertations for the 2025-2026 academic year have been selected following blind peer review, defense committee evaluation, recommendations from degree authorization disciplines and colleges, and Graduate School verification.

All five selected dissertations are from the Mathematics discipline.

| No. | Author | Dissertation Title | Supervisor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Chen Huanjie | Classification of Symmetry Groups of Geometric Nonlinear Partial Differential Equations | Du Shizhong |
| 2 | Chen Tiantian | Isometric Composition Operators on BMOA and Bloch Spaces | Ulanhas |
| 3 | Ding Wenxu | Fast Algorithms for Dual Quaternion Matrix Decomposition and Their Applications in Color Image Processing | Lin Furong |
| 4 | Tian Liu | Hilbert Matrix Operators and Cesàro-type Operators on Some Spaces of Analytic Functions | Ulanhas |
| 5 | Zheng Chenyou | Cohomological Characterization of the Self-homeomorphic Trivial Moduli Group | Xu Fei |
