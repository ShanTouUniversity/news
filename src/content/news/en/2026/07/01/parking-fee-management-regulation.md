It is reported that the Security Department has drafted the *Shantou University Motor Vehicle Parking Fee Management Regulations (Draft for Comments)* and is now soliciting opinions and suggestions from all university units.

Based on the *Shantou Special Economic Zone Parking Facility Management Measures* and the *Shantou City Motor Vehicle Parking Service Fee Management Implementation Rules*, and taking into account the actual conditions of the university, the Security Department formulated the draft regulation to strengthen campus motor vehicle management, maintain orderly campus traffic, and effectively manage campus parking resources.

All units are requested to submit their feedback and suggestions to the Security Department email at **o_bwk@stu.edu.cn** by **July 8, 2026**. Details of the draft regulation are available in the attached document.
