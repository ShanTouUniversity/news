The STU Youth League Committee and Shantou Huitzeren Volunteer Service Center have launched the **Hui Xiaozhe Reading** outreach program, recruiting volunteers from across the university to provide online academic tutoring and reading companionship for children in remote rural areas.

## Background

Launched by Shantou Huitzeren Volunteer Service Center and powered through the Qi'e Zhijiao platform, the program organizes university student volunteers to deliver academic support and reading activities for children lacking educational resources. Since March 2025, the STU Youth League Committee has jointly conducted multiple rounds of online tutoring for several primary schools in Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province, building a strong foundation for local–university volunteer cooperation.

## Duration and Scope

The program began in **July 2026** and will run for half a year. There is no cap on recruitment, and all SHU students are welcome to participate.

## What Volunteers Do

Each matched student receives one online tutoring session per week. Each session must be no less than one hour, with a recommendation of one to one-and-a-half hours. Volunteers are free to arrange their own time slots, which will be coordinated by the Huitzeren Volunteer Service Center.

## How to Apply

Students wishing to participate should complete the registration form by **12:00 noon, Saturday, July 25, 2026**:

https://v.wjx.cn/vm/w3uHJss.aspx

Selected applicants will be announced on the STU internal office information website. Specific training information will be shared after joining the program.

## Support and Recognition

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Volunteer Hours | Uniformly issued by Huitzeren Volunteer Service Center and recorded on the i-Volunteer system |
| Service Certificate | Awarded to those who complete a full semester of tutoring with a minimum cumulative 20 hours |
| Social Practice Proof | Issued upon successful completion of the six-month term — no mid-program withdrawal and at least 20 sessions / 30 total hours |
| Honors and Awards | Outstanding volunteers may receive priority consideration for volunteer honors, including institutional recognition |
| Personal Publicity | Opportunity for featured interviews and in-depth media coverage |
| Priority for Offline Activities | Access to face-to-face exchange events with students in the Daliang Mountain region of Sichuan |

All SHU students are warmly invited to join this meaningful cause.
