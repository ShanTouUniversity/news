It is reported that the University Party Committee will hold a cadre democratic recommendation meeting on **July 3, 2026 (Friday) at 9:30 AM** in **Room 349, College of Science** to conduct individual discussions and group recommendations for the Vice Dean position of the College of Science.

Attendees include the university leader overseeing the college, the college's leadership team, heads of departments and institutes, faculty members with senior professional titles, and relevant faculty representatives. The College of Science must submit the attendee list to the Party Organization Department (email: o_zzb@stu.edu.cn) **before 17:00 on July 2**. Participants are requested to arrive 15 minutes early and bring their own pens for the recommendation task.
