The Computer Teaching Center has issued a notice on computer public course enrollment changes.

## Discontinued Courses

The following courses will be discontinued starting **Fall 2026**:
- Fundamentals and Frontiers of Computer Applications
- Python and AI (Science & Engineering)
- Python and AI (Humanities & Social Sciences)

## Replacement Course

**(COM1026A) AI Fundamentals** will be offered from Fall 2026. Students **enrolled in 2025 or later** who have not yet completed their computer public course requirement must take this replacement course to fulfill their credits.

## Course Availability

To ensure enrollment capacity, **2 sections** of AI Fundamentals have been arranged at the Donghai'an Campus and **1 section** at the Sangpu Campus.
