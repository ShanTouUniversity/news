To commemorate the 90th anniversary of the Red Army's Long March victory, the Student Affairs Office and the Youth League Committee will jointly organize the "Crossing the Liupan Mountains, Joining Forces at Wuqi" Long March reenactment and Party history education project during the 2026 summer break.

From May 21 to June 30, the project conducted candidate reviews (resume and research plan evaluation) and physical fitness tests. The selection has resulted in **18 regular members** and **5 alternate members**. The full list is available in the attached document.

> ⚠️ The selected member list is available on the OA system.
> Source: STU OA Notice (Student Affairs Office and Youth League Committee)
