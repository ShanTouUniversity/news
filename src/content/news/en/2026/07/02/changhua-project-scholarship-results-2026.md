Following applications by project leaders and review by the college selection committee, the Yangtze River Journalism and Communication College has completed the evaluation for the 2025-2026 Changhua Project Scholarship, awarding **7,000 yuan** to each of the following three projects:

- Retracing China's Northwest Corner (Sichuan)
- Retracing China's Northwest Corner (Inner Mongolia)
- Survey on Fertility Attitudes of Young Women in the Chaoshan Region

## Public Notice

Objections may be raised in writing or by phone **before 17:00 on July 6, 2026** to the Yangtze River Journalism and Communication College (personal objections must include the real name of the complainant).
