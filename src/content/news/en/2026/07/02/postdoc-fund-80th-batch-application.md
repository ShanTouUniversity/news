The China Postdoctoral Science Foundation has launched the 80th batch of general funding applications, and STU's Research Office has issued the relevant notice.

## Funding and Eligibility

- **Natural sciences**: 80,000 yuan; **Social sciences**: 50,000 yuan
- Male postdocs may apply within **18 months** of entry; female postdocs within **24 months**
- Each person can receive funding only once per station
- In-service postdocs (except active military) are not eligible

## Application Process

1. Log into the [China Postdoctoral Science Foundation Management System](https://jj.chinapostdoctor.org.cn/auth/login.html?usertype=bsh) **after August 1, 2026** using your account
2. Complete online submission **by August 24**, and notify the Research Office's Science Project and Platform Section by email for formal review
3. Submit one printed copy of the application **by September 4** after passing the formal review

## Important Notes

- Under "科研成果和奖励" in the Personal Info section, list up to **5 items** (papers, monographs, patents, awards, etc.)
- The Project Info section (except "研究基础") **must not contain personal information** — including the applicant's name, institution, or supervisor's name — or it will be scored 0
- Funded成果 must acknowledge "中国博士后科学基金资助项目" (Supported by the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation under Grant Number XX)
