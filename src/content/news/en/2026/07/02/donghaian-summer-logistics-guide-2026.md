| Facility | Hours | Lighting |

## Shuttle Bus

- 7:30 Sangpu → Donghai'an
- 17:15 Donghai'an → Sangpu

- Trip 1: 7:30 Sangpu→Donghai'an, 8:45 Donghai'an→Sangpu
- Trip 2: 16:00 Sangpu→Donghai'an, 17:15 Donghai'an→Sangpu

Normal duty during summer. Repair hotline: 0754-86500112
