The Donghai'an Campus Management Committee and Resource Management Office have adjusted the inter-campus shuttle bus schedule for the summer period.

## Regular Summer Schedule (Jul 6 – Sep 3)

**One trip** per day, Monday–Friday (no weekend service):
- 7:30 Sangpu → Donghai'an
- 17:15 Donghai'an → Sangpu

## Transition Periods (Jul 3–5, Sep 4–5)

**Two trips** per day:
- Trip 1: 7:30 Sangpu→Donghai'an, 8:45 Donghai'an→Sangpu
- Trip 2: 16:00 Sangpu→Donghai'an, 17:15 Donghai'an→Sangpu

The fall semester shuttle schedule will be announced separately starting September 6.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Donghai'an Campus Management Committee, Resource Management Office)
