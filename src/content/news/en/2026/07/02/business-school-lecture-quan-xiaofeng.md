The Business School will host an academic lecture on **July 3, 2026 at 10:00 AM** in **Room C333, Administration Building, Donghai'an Campus**, featuring Prof. Quan Xiaofeng, a Distinguished Professor from Suzhou University.

## Talks

**Talk 1**: "Do Rumors Stop with the Wise: Evidence from Analysts' Earnings Forecast Performance" — examining the impact of rumor propagation on analyst earnings forecasts

**Talk 2**: "Experience in Applying for National-Level High-Quality Research Projects" — sharing insights on securing major grants such as the National Social Science Fund Key Project

## About the Speaker

Prof. Quan Xiaofeng is a Distinguished Professor and doctoral supervisor at Suzhou University, Director of the Capital Operations and Risk Control Research Center, and Chief Expert of the National Social Science Fund Major Project. He has been recognized as a Changjiang Scholar Young Scholar and a National Accounting Advanced Talent by the Ministry of Finance. He has published over 100 papers in leading journals including *Economic Research Journal*, *Journal of Business Ethics*, and *Financial Management*, and has received 7 provincial and ministerial-level awards.
