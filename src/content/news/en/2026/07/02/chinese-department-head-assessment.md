In accordance with the Regulations on the Selection and Appointment of Party and Government Leading Cadres, the inspection team of the College of Liberal Arts will conduct an organizational assessment of Chen Miaodan, the candidate for Head of the Chinese Language and Literature Department, on **July 3, 2026**.

Individuals and organizations may report on the candidate by letter, phone, or in-person visit. Personal reports should include the reporter's real name; organizational reports must bear the organization's official seal. All reports must be factual and free from defamation.
