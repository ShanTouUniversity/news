The Resources Management Office has published the June 2026 water and electricity bills for faculty transit housing, and relevant faculty members are advised to check and verify them promptly.

The current billing period covers June 1 to 30. Faculty can log in to the STU utility management system (http://power.stu.edu.cn/) using their campus network account to view the charges. Those with questions or disputes should visit the Water and Electricity Center during work hours before the 15th of this month. Inquiry hotline: 86502489, 86502400.

After the deadline, the Finance Office will deduct the corresponding amount from the individual's salary account. Tenants who cannot be charged through the salary account should obtain a payment notice from the Water and Electricity Center and then complete the payment at the Finance Office.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Resources Management Office)
