The Communist Party of China Business School Committee of Shantou University has issued a decision approving the transition to full Party membership for 20 comrades.

Following deliberation by the Business School Party Committee, 20 comrades—Wang SX, Wang TY, Lu XW, Liu YP, Liu XL, Liu JF, Liu ZH, Chen WC, Chen BW, Chen XL, Chen YL, Chen JA, Zheng YL, Luo HH, Hou RQ, Tang F, Huang RH, Wen Y, Zou LY, and Wei XX—have been approved to become full members of the Communist Party of China on schedule.
