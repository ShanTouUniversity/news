It is reported that, due to relocation and renovation, the gym in the Building B Complex on the Shantou University Donghai'an Campus has been temporarily closed with immediate effect.

According to the Donghai'an Campus Management Committee and the Physical Education and Research Department, once the relocation and renovation are complete, the gym will reopen to all faculty and students in the fall semester of this year. The university reminded faculty and students to take note and inform one another.

> Source: STU OA Notice (Donghai'an Campus Management Committee; Physical Education and Research Department)
