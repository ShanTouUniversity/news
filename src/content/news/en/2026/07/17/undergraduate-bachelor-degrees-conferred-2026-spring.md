It is reported that the Shantou University Academic Degrees Committee recently decided to confer bachelor's degrees on 917 qualified undergraduate graduates, comprising 837 regular higher education graduates and 80 continuing higher education graduates.

The 11th Academic Degrees Committee of Shantou University reviewed the graduates who met the conferral requirements. Following the review, all 917 students were found to have completed every requirement of their respective program curricula, obtained the prescribed credits, and met the conditions for the award of a bachelor's degree. The decision was made in accordance with the Degrees Law of the People's Republic of China, the Shantou University Measures for the Administration of Degree Work, and the relevant detailed rules governing bachelor's degree work.
