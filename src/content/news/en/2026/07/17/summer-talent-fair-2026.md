It is reported that the "Million Talents Gather in Southern Guangdong" 2026 Shantou Summer Talent Exchange Fair and College Graduate Employment Negotiation Fair will be held on the evening of Friday, July 24, from 19:00 to 22:00 at MixC Plaza in Longhu District, Shantou City. Organized by the Shantou Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, the event aims to facilitate connections between employers and college graduates.

The fair focuses on advancing the "Million Talents Gather in Southern Guangdong" action plan, creating a supply-and-demand matchmaking platform to promote efficient employer-candidate matching and assist key groups, especially graduating students, in securing employment.

## Event Overview

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | "Million Talents Gather in Southern Guangdong" 2026 Shantou Summer Talent Exchange Fair & College Graduate Employment Negotiation Fair |
| Organizer | Shantou Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau |
| Date & Time | Friday, July 24, 2026, 19:00-22:00 |
| Venue | MixC Plaza, Longhu District, Shantou City (intersection of Jinhuan Road and Changping Road) |

## Participating Employers and Positions

According to the organizers, approximately 50 employers will participate in the event, spanning industries including manufacturing, information technology, financial services, education, and healthcare, offering a wide range of job opportunities.

## Supporting Employment Services

In addition to on-site recruitment, the fair will feature multiple supporting employment services:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Policy Consultation | Information on current employment and entrepreneurship policies |
| Live Streaming Job Showcase | Real-time job presentations via online live streaming platforms |
| Career Guidance | Professional career planning advice |
| Resume Review | One-on-one resume optimization sessions with experts |

Attendees are encouraged to review participating employer information and job requirements in advance, bring printed resumes, and prepare for face-to-face interviews with recruiters.

> Source: Shantou University Admissions and Employment Office
