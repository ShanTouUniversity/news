It is reported that the Shantou University College of Liberal Arts has issued its decision recognizing outstanding student cadres and others for the 2025-2026 academic year. Following review and public notice by the selection panel, the college honored 69 students with the title of "Outstanding Student Cadre," 16 students as "Active Contributors to Student Work," and 10 students as "Outstanding Volunteers."

The college expressed the hope that all students will follow the example of these role models, study diligently, forge ahead, and make new contributions to the college's development. The complete list of honorees is available in the attachment.
