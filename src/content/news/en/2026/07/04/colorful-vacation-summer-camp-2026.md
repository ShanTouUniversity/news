In response to the "Colorful Vacation" volunteer service initiative to care for minors and deepen university-local community partnerships, the Engineering College Youth League and Jingyi College, in collaboration with the Dahua Subdistrict Youth League Working Committee, will host the "Dream Begins in Dahua · Starlight Workshop" Colorful Vacation summer camp from July 13 to 19.

The camp is open to young people in Dahua Subdistrict, running daily from 15:00 to 17:30 at the Fuchang Community Party-Mass Service Center, Jicheng Community Residents' Committee, and Shantou Science Museum.

The event is organized by the "Xinghua" University Student Practice Team and the "Huameng" Colorful Vacation Series Course Teaching Assault Team. Participants follow a "star navigation mission challenge" format, collecting "starlight stamps" upon completing daily tasks. Those who collect all stamps will receive a graduation certificate and an exclusive gift pack.

Each day's program includes knowledge sessions, hands-on practice, and interactive games.

| Date | Course Title |
| --- | --- |
| July 13 | Setting Sail Among the Stars — Interstellar Exploration and Team Building |
| July 14 | Passing the Torch — Red Spirit and Storytelling Session |
| July 15 | Blue and White Illusion — Tie-Dye Magic and Color Challenge |
| July 16 | Secrets of Light — Circuit Exploration and Starlight Greeting Cards |
| July 17 | Flowers and Plants in Poetry — Botanical Paper Lamps and Nature Crafts |
| July 18 | Elegance of Bamboo and Shells — Bamboo Weaving and Mother-of-Pearl Inlay |
| July 19 | Starlight Bloom — Tech Exploration and Starlight Showcase |
