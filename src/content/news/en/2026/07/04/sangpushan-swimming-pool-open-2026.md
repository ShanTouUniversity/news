According to the Teaching and Research Division of Physical Education at Shantou University, the outdoor swimming pool at the Sangpushan Campus will open to faculty and students starting July 6.

Access will be managed via campus card swipes, with a fee of 5 RMB per session. Daily usage limits by card type are as follows:

| Card Type | Daily Limit |
| --- | --- |
| Faculty/Staff Card | 3 uses per day |
| Student Card | 2 uses per day |
| Temporary/Family Card | 1 use per day |

Operating hours are from 17:30 to 19:30 daily. Due to changing summer weather, any temporary adjustments to opening hours will be posted at the pool entrance without additional OA notifications.

The division reminds users not to lend campus cards to non-university individuals, to follow all safety and hygiene regulations, and to obey the directions of pool attendants and lifeguards. Entry is prohibited outside of designated hours. Vehicles should be parked neatly to avoid obstructing campus traffic.

> Source: Teaching and Research Division of Physical Education OA Notice
