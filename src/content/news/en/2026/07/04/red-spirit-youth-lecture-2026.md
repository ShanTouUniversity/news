To pass on the red legacy and carry forward the spirit of the Red Boat, the Engineering College Youth League and Jingyi College, in collaboration with the Dahua Subdistrict Youth League Working Committee, will hold the fourth session of the "Party and League Join Hands to Pass the Torch, Red Spirit Nourishes Children" themed lecture series on July 14.

The theme of this session is "Red Boat Guiding the Way, Passing the Torch." The event is open to primary and middle school students in Dahua Subdistrict and will take place on July 14 (Tuesday), from 14:30 to 17:30, at the Fuchang Community Party-Mass Service Center.

The "Red Star Navigation Team" and "Red Heritage Voice" Assault Team serve as organizers, with the "Huameng" Colorful Vacation Series Course Teaching Assault Team as co-organizer.

The event features two main segments:

| Segment | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Lecture | Through historical storytelling and interactive Q&A centered on the "spirit of the Red Boat," students will explore the founding journey of the Communist Party of China and grasp the core connotations of the Red Boat spirit |
| Interactive Games | Includes the "Red Boat Memories" story detail quiz and a "Historical Facts" true-or-judgment game |
