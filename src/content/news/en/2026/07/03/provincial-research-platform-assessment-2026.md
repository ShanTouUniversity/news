According to the Research Affairs Office at Shantou University, to standardize the operation and management of provincial-level research platforms and reinforce institutional responsibilities, an annual assessment was conducted in accordance with the *Shantou University Research Platform Management Measures*.

Following internal expert review and verification by the Research Affairs Office, the 2025 assessment results for provincial platforms have been finalized. The results are now published for review by the university community.

The public notice period is 10 working days, from July 3 to July 17, 2026. Any objections must be submitted in writing with real-name identification, contact information, and supporting materials to the Research Affairs Office via email: o_kyc@stu.edu.cn. Submissions after the deadline will not be accepted. Reports must be objective and truthful; fabrication of false information is prohibited.
