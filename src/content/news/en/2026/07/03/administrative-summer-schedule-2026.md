It is reported that the Party and Administrative Office has compiled the summer duty schedules submitted by various units for administrative, teaching support, and logistics personnel, and published them as Document No. 2026-02.

Following the university's earlier summer vacation notice, all units submitted their summer staffing arrangements to the Party and Administrative Office by June 22. The consolidated schedule has now been finalized, and all units are required to strictly comply with the relevant provisions to ensure timely and effective service to faculty, staff, and students throughout the summer period.

> ⚠️ The full summer 2026 duty schedule is available as an attachment on the OA system.
> Source: STU OA Notice (Party and Administrative Office)
