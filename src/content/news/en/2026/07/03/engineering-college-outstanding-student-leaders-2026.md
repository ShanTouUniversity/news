According to the Engineering College, following student applications and review by the college evaluation committee, 75 students including Jiang Zheng and 50 students including Chen Lin have been selected as the 2025-2026 Outstanding Student Leaders and "Five-Color" Navigators, respectively.

Those with objections to the selection results may submit written concerns to the Engineering College Youth League Committee Office (Room 413, West Building 4) by 12:00 on July 8, 2026. Personal submissions must include the reviewer's real name.
