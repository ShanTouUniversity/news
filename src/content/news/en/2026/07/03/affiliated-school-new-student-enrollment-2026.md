According to the STU Affiliated School, to prepare for the start of the fall 2026 semester, registration and orientation for first-year junior high and primary school students are scheduled as follows:

| Grade | Time | Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Junior High Grade 1 | August 29 (Saturday), 8:00 AM | Grade 1 Classrooms, 2nd Floor, Affiliated Middle School |
| Primary School Grade 1 | August 29 (Saturday), 3:00 PM | Grade 1 Classrooms, 2nd Floor, Affiliated Primary School |

The school reminds families that students who have completed registration procedures are considered to have secured a place. Students who also participated in computer lotteries at other schools should notify the school by email as soon as possible if they decide to decline their place after lottery results are announced.
