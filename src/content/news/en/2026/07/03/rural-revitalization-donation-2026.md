According to the STU Labor Union, in accordance with the *Guangdong Provincial Department of Education Notice on Conducting the 2026 "June 30" Rural Revitalization Assistance Activity*, the university organized the 2026 "June 30" Rural Revitalization Charity Donation Drive.

Faculty and students responded enthusiastically to the call, actively contributing to support rural revitalization efforts. The donation drive has now concluded, with a total of 50,518.69 RMB raised across the university.

The full amount was transferred to the Guangdong Education Foundation account on July 3, 2026.
