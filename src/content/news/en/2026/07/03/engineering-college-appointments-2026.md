According to the Engineering College, based on probationary period assessments and decisions made at the 9th meeting of the College Party Committee in 2026, the following appointments have been made:

| Name | Position |
| --- | --- |
| Zhao Lei | Head, Electronic Engineering Department |
| Ling Siying | Head, Mechanical Engineering Department |
| Xu Biao | Deputy Head, Electronic Engineering Department |
| Zhang Jiayang | Deputy Head, Mechanical Engineering Department |

The effective date of appointment is calculated from the start of the probationary period.
