According to the Research Affairs Office at Shantou University, in accordance with the *Several Provisions of the State Council on Implementing the Law of the People's Republic of China on Promoting the Transformation of Scientific and Technological Achievements*, the following technological achievement (technical secret) is proposed for public notice and transfer:

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Technical Secret Name | Micro Reclaimer Test Platform and Digital Twin Intelligent O&M System |
| Achievement Completers | Wang Fengtao, Peng Shitong, Guo Jianan |
| Rights Holder | Shantou University |
| Transaction Method | Technical Secret Usage License (Agreement Pricing) |
| Proposed Price | 530,000 RMB |
| Licensee | Nanjing Kelora Information Technology Co., Ltd. |
| Implementation Period | 3 years |

The public notice period runs from July 3 to July 17, 2026. Objections during this period should be submitted in writing with real-name identification to the Research Affairs Office. The office will investigate and respond within 10 working days of receiving an objection.
