Following student applications and review by the college selection committee, the Yangtze River Journalism and Communication College has completed the evaluation for the 2025-2026 Changhua Practice Scholarship, selecting 20 students as recipients.

## Award Amounts

Each recipient will receive **3,000 yuan**, with an additional **2,000 yuan** for students facing financial difficulties.

## Recipients

Bi ZQ, Qi JH, Liang YY, Luo CJ, Cao ST, Ye KX, Zhang JM, Huang ZQ, Zhu ZH, Lin HX, Zhang SX, Xu RL, Yang YX, Lai YM, Li ZH, Lin YR, Zeng YQ, Song CY, Zhang ZW, Feng M.
