It is reported that the Guangdong Provincial Education Department held the 11th Guangdong University Counselor Quality and Ability Competition in June 2026 and has recently announced the results. According to the department's notice (Document No. 2026-13), the competition awarded 10 first prizes, 20 second prizes, and 30 third prizes.

Wang Le, a counselor at the School of Law and Gezhi College at Shantou University, delivered an outstanding performance and won the second prize.
