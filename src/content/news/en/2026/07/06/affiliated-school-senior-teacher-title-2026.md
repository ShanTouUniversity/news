According to the STU Human Resources Department, Li Shaona from Shantou University Affiliated Middle School has passed the 2025 Shantou City Primary and Secondary School Senior Teacher Title Review, with the qualified qualification being Junior High History Senior Teacher.

The result is now subject to public review from July 6 to July 10, 2026. Any individual or organization with objections to the list of approved candidates may submit a written statement to the Personnel Section of the Shantou Education Bureau or the University Human Resources Department during the public notice period.
