It is reported that the Student Affairs Office at Shantou University offered introductory software classes for the autumn 2014 semester, covering Photoshop image processing, Illustrator graphic design, poster design, and integrated software learning. A total of 31 students were selected across the four classes.

## Schedule

| Course | Time | Location | Student Coordinator |
|--------|------|----------|---------------------|
| Photoshop Image Processing | Tue 19:30-21:30 (Weeks 7-10) | SLC 105 | Shen Tong (Interior Design, Class of 2013) |
| Illustrator Graphic Design | Thu 19:30-21:30 (Weeks 7-10) | SLC 105 | Lin Jiayuan (Environmental Design, Class of 2013) |
| Poster Design Intro | Fri 15:00-17:00 (Weeks 6-9) | SLC 105 | Zhang Jiangnan (Literature, Class of 2013) |
| Integrated Software Learning | Wed & Sat 15:00-17:00 (Weeks 6-9) | SLC 105 | Chen Yanpei (Industrial Management, Class of 2012) |
