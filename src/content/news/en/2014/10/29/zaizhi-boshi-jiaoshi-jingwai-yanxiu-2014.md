To further enhance the academic level and research capabilities of its teaching staff, the Human Resources Office of Shantou University has launched the 2015 selection for the "Overseas Study Program for In-Service Faculty Pursuing Doctoral Degrees." The program aims to send outstanding faculty members to universities or research institutions abroad for advanced study.

## Program Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Eligibility | In-service faculty members pursuing doctoral degrees |
| Funding Method | Tuition subsidy provided by the university |
| Subsidy Amount | Up to 30,000 RMB per person |
| Study Duration | One to two years |

Faculty members interested in applying should complete the "Shantou University Overseas Study Application Form for In-Service Faculty Pursuing Doctoral Degrees," obtain review signatures and official seals from their respective colleges or departments, and submit the form along with supporting materials to the Faculty Development Section of the HR Office. The application deadline is November 7, 2014, at 5:00 p.m.

For questions, please contact Ms. Huang Yumei or Ms. Zhuo Yanchun at the Faculty Development Section of the HR Office, telephone: 8650-2562.
