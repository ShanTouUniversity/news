It is reported that the Global Center for Studies (CGS) at Shantou University's School of Liberal Arts is making available a collection of textbooks and reading materials from previous CGS courses for students to take free of charge.

## Pickup Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Materials | Textbooks and reading materials from past CGS courses |
| Pickup Date | Friday, October 31, 2014 |
| Pickup Location | Outside Room 202, Wendong Building, along the wall |
| Organizer | Global Center for Studies (CGS) |

Students interested in these materials are encouraged to visit the pickup location outside Room 202 of the Wendong Building on Friday, October 31, and select the books they would like to take.
