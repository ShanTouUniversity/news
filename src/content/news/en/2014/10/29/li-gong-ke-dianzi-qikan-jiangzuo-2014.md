It is reported that the University Library of Shantou University will host a lecture titled "Retrieval and Access to STEM E-Journals" on the evening of October 30, 2014 (Thursday), from 19:30 to 20:30, at the Subject Services Department (second-floor reference desk). The lecture is part of the 2014 "Subject Literature Services" series and will be presented by Zeng Zhaohong. Faculty and students are welcome to attend.

## Lecture Content

The lecture covers two parts: Chinese and foreign e-journals in science, technology, and engineering disciplines, introducing commonly used academic databases.

### Chinese E-Journals for STEM Fields

The session will introduce methods for retrieving and accessing Chinese-language e-journals in STEM fields, with a focus on advanced search techniques in CNKI's China Academic Journals Full-Text Database, helping faculty and students locate and utilize academic resources more efficiently.

### Foreign E-Journals for STEM Fields

The foreign-language portion will cover the following topics:

| Topic | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Ei Compendex | Introduction and retrieval methods for the Engineering Index database |
| Professional Society E-Journals | Overview of library-subscribed databases including ACM, ACS, AIP, APS, ASCE, IEL, IOP, RSC, and MathSciNet |
| Full-Text Download Rankings | Sharing download statistics across e-journal databases to highlight the journals most frequently read by STU faculty and students |
| Open Access Journals | Introduction to Open Access Journal publishing |
