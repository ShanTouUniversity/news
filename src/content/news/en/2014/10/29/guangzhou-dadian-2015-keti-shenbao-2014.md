It is reported that the Research Affairs Office of Shantou University recently circulated a notice on organizing applications for the "Guangzhou Dadian and Guangzhou Historical Culture Research" 2015 annual research project, inviting faculty members who are interested in applying for the "Guangzhou Dadian and Guangzhou Historical Culture Special Research Project."

## Application Schedule

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Project Name | Guangzhou Dadian and Guangzhou Historical Culture Special Research Project |
| Form Review Deadline | November 26, 2014 |
| Final Submission Deadline | November 28, 2014 |
| Submission Method | Application form sent to yczhuo@stu.edu.cn for form review; six printed copies submitted to the Research Affairs Office |

Faculty members interested in applying should prepare their application materials according to the notice requirements. The completed application form should be emailed to yczhuo@stu.edu.cn for form review by November 26, 2014, followed by submission of six printed copies to the Research Affairs Office by November 28, 2014.

For questions, please contact Ms. Huang Yumei or Ms. Zhuo Yanchun at 8650-2563.
