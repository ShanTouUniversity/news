It is reported that the 2014 campaign to search for "Chinese College Student Self-Improvement Stars" and the recommendation process for the "New Oriental Western China Hardship Scholarship" were jointly launched by the Communist Youth League Central Committee and the National Student Union in September 2014. The Youth League Committee of Shantou University has circulated the relevant notice and is organizing all students to participate.

## Application Process

| Item | Requirements |
|------|-------------|
| Online Registration | Register on the official website: star.xiaomei.cc |
| Deadline | Before 24:00 on November 21, 2014 |
| Paper Materials | Download application form from the website homepage; prepare personal achievement materials |
| Submission Location | Youth League Committee Office |

## Program Overview

The "Chinese College Student Self-Improvement Star" campaign aims to identify and promote outstanding role models among contemporary college students who demonstrate independence, resilience, and ambition, showcasing the spirit of new-era university students. The "New Oriental Western China Hardship Scholarship" provides financial assistance to disadvantaged students from western China regions, helping academically promising students from low-income families complete their studies.
