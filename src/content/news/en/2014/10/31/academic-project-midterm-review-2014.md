The Graduate School has completed the mid-term review of the 2014 undergraduate extracurricular academic and technological projects. According to the results, 31 projects passed the mid-term review, while 7 were cancelled for failing to proceed as scheduled.

## Review Results

| Category | Count | Details |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Passed Mid-Term Review | 31 | Additional funding determined based on completion status, project difficulty, and funding needs |
| Cancelled | 7 | Failed to proceed as scheduled and did not submit mid-term review reports |

## Next Steps

Project leaders from the passing teams are required to follow the schedule in the attached document for startup fund reimbursement procedures.

The university plans to conduct the final review for the 2014 projects in mid-March 2015. Participants are encouraged to continue their efforts with guidance from faculty advisors, aiming to achieve stronger results and potentially become candidate projects for the National Undergraduate Challenge Cup competition.
