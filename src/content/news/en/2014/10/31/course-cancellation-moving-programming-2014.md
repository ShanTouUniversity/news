It is reported that due to a faculty member's business trip, the Academic Affairs Office at Shantou University has issued a notice cancelling the "Introduction to Mobile Programming" (Class No. 72993) session scheduled for November 6 (Thursday), periods 6-7, at Computer Lab E101. A make-up session will be scheduled at a later date.

Students enrolled in the course are advised to inform one another.
