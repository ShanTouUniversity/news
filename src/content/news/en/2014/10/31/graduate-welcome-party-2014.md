The Graduate School will host the "Bright Youth, Graduate Excellence" 2014 Graduate Welcome Party on the evening of Saturday, November 1, at 19:00, at the university gymnasium (adjacent to the track and field stadium). All faculty and students are welcome to attend.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | "Bright Youth, Graduate Excellence" 2014 Graduate Welcome Party |
| Date & Time | Saturday, November 1, 2014, 19:00 |
| Venue | University Gymnasium (next to the track and field stadium) |
| Organizer | Graduate School |

## Highlights

The evening will feature entertaining cultural performances and interactive segments, along with generous prizes for attendees. Organizers encourage students and faculty to come together to celebrate the start of the new academic year.

> Source: Shantou University Graduate School
