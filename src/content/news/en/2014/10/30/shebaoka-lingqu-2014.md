It is reported that the Resource Management Division of Shantou University recently issued a notice arranging for selected staff members to collect their social security cards. According to the notice, Qiu Yumin, Li Rujin, Zhang Minmin, Gu Yangyang, Liu Jinfei, and Zhang Hao are required to visit the staff medical insurance office on the second floor of the university hospital during working hours to complete the collection process.

The social security card serves as a key credential for beneficiaries to access social insurance services. Cardholders may use it to handle various social security matters, including medical care, pension, and unemployment benefits. The university reminds the relevant staff members to collect their cards promptly. For questions, please contact the staff medical insurance office at the university hospital.
