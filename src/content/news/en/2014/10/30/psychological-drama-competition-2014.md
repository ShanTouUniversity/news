The Student Affairs Office Mental Health Education and Counseling Center has announced the fifth Shantou University Psychological Drama Competition, themed "Seeking the Mind Catcher, Finding the Self's Refuge." The competition invites all enrolled students to submit original psychological drama works.

## Competition Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | Fifth Shantou University Student Psychological Drama Competition |
| Theme | Seeking the Mind Catcher, Finding the Self's Refuge |
| Eligibility | All currently enrolled STU students |
| Registration | Free-form teams, maximum 15 members per team |
| Organizer | Student Affairs Office Mental Health Education and Counseling Center |

## Work Requirements

Participants are encouraged to draw on common psychological challenges faced by college students, creating and performing original psychological dramas. Works must incorporate psychological knowledge and counseling skills, featuring engaging plots, well-developed characters, and aesthetically appealing performances.

Performance formats are open-ended and may include musicals, plays, skits, mime, song-and-dance dramas, and more. Each piece should run approximately 10 to 15 minutes. Dialogue and narration should primarily be in Mandarin, with optional use of English or dialects.

## Schedule

| Stage | Date | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Registration & Script Submission | November 3 – November 23 | Submit registration form and script |
| Preliminary Round | November 29 | On-site performance preliminaries |
| Final Round | December 26 | Final performance |

## Prizes

| Prize | Winners | Reward |
|-------|---------|--------|
| First Prize | 1 | 1,000 RMB bonus + certificate |
| Second Prize | 2 | 600 RMB bonus + certificate |
| Third Prize | 2 | 300 RMB bonus + certificate |

Teams advancing to the finals will receive a proportional subsidy for props and stage materials.
