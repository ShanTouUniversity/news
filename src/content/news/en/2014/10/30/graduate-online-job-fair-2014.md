The Graduate School has announced an online job fair for the 2015 graduating class, running from November 1 to 30, 2014. The event is open to all graduate students on the main campus.

## Event Overview

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | Shantou University 2015 Graduate Online Job Fair |
| Dates | November 1–30, 2014 |
| Eligibility | All graduate students on the main campus |
| Website | http://www.stzp.cn/adv/StUniversityGetJob/ |

## Participating Employers

Nearly 70 companies have already registered for the online job fair, including:

| Company | Location |
|---------|----------|
| Guangdong Xianle Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. | Guangdong |
| Nanjing Chaozhao New Materials Co., Ltd. | Nanjing, Jiangsu |
| Foshan Nanfang Packaging Group Co., Ltd. | Foshan, Guangdong |
| Guangdong Yatu Chemical Co., Ltd. | Guangdong |
| Guangdong Guanghua Technology Co., Ltd. | Guangdong |

The Graduate School noted that participating companies and job postings will continue to be added and updated throughout the fair period. Students are encouraged to check the website regularly for new opportunities.

> Source: Shantou University Graduate School
