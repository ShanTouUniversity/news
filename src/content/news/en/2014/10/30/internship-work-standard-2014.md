The Academic Affairs Office has directed all teaching units to submit signed paper copies of their internship work standards by November 7, 2014, for review by the relevant sub-committees of the Teaching Committee. Approved standards will be published across the university.

Internship work standards serve as the foundational framework and guiding document for organizing internship programs within each teaching unit. Under the provisions of the "Shantou University Undergraduate Internship Management Regulations" (Shan Fa [2014] No. 80), all units are required to develop and submit their respective internship work standards.

On June 18, 2014, the Academic Affairs Office had already emailed the revised regulations and associated template materials to the leadership of each teaching unit, instructing them to prepare and submit their internship work standards.
