The Student Affairs Office and the Student Dormitory Self-Management Committee of Shantou University are organizing the ninth dormitory committee leadership training and team-building activity, aimed at improving members' work and planning capabilities while strengthening team cohesion and promoting student self-management within residential communities.

The event is scheduled for November 1–2, 2014, and will be held at Building E lecture hall and Building D activity room. All members of the ninth Student Dormitory Self-Management Committee are invited to participate.

|------|---------|
| Event Name | Ninth Student Dormitory Committee Leadership Training and Team-Building Activity |
