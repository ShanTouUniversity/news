The Academic Affairs Office will conduct a comprehensive inspection of internship work materials across all teaching units from November 10 to 14, 2014, to assess the implementation of internship programs and ensure compliance with the "Shantou University Undergraduate Internship Management Regulations (2014 Revision)."

## Scope

The inspection covers all internship courses offered during the 2014 summer vacation and summer term.

## Schedule

Each department must select a specific date within the November 10–14 window for the inspection and submit it by email to the Academic Affairs Office by November 7. Contact: Zhou Mengyi, email myzhou@stu.edu.cn.

Departments are also required to complete a self-assessment of their internship materials by November 7.

## Inspection Content

The inspection covers three levels of documentation:

### Department Level

| Material | Quantity Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| Internship Work Standards | Two copies; one submitted during on-site inspection |
| Internship Course Syllabus | One copy |
| Internship Schedule | Two copies; one submitted during on-site inspection |
| Internship Preparatory Meeting Minutes | One copy |
| Internship Summary Meeting Minutes | One copy |
| Internship Status Summary Form | Two copies; one submitted during on-site inspection |

### Instructor Level

| Internship Type | Requirement |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Non-continuous supervision | One work record per internship site |
| Full-time continuous supervision | One work journal per instructor |
| Student-self-arranged internships | One work record per instructor |

### Student Level

| Material | Quantity Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| Student Internship Diary | One per student |
| Student Internship Report | One per student |
| Student Attendance Record | One per student |
| Student Internship Evaluation Form | One per student |
| Self-Arranged Internship Application Form | One per self-arranged student |
| Internship Site Acceptance Letter | One per self-arranged student |

## Results Publication

The Academic Affairs Office will publish the inspection results on the internal office system and in the Teaching Newsletter.
