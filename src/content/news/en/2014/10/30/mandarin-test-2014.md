The second half of 2014 Mandarin proficiency test will be held on Saturday, November 8, at the Mandarin Training and Testing Station of Shantou Vocational and Technical College. The Academic Affairs Office has reminded students taking the test to note the following details.

## Test Arrangements

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Test Date | November 8, 2014 (Saturday); exact time per admission ticket |
| Venue | 6th Floor, Shantou Teachers College Teaching Building, No. 23 Jinyuan Road |
| Required Documents | Admission ticket and ID card |
| Ticket Collection | One student per class may collect from the Academic Affairs Office service counter |

## Transportation Guide

There is no direct bus route from Shantou University to the testing venue. Students may take the following options:

| Route | Stop |
|-------|------|
| Bus 27 | Jindong Huayuan Station |
| Bus 21 | Shantou Daily Press Station |
| Bus 6 | International Grand Hotel Station |

From any of these stops, walk to Shantou Vocational and Technical College (Shantou Teachers College) to reach the testing venue.

## Safety Reminder

As the test is conducted in segments, the university will not arrange shuttle buses. Students are advised to travel in groups, remain aware of personal safety and belongings. In case of emergencies, call campus security at 8290-4110 or contact the relevant instructor.

> Source: Shantou University Academic Affairs Office
