The Yangtze River College of Journalism and Communication will host a lecture on Tuesday, November 4, from 14:00 to 16:00 in Classroom E-Jie 203, featuring Jiang Zhigao, executive editor of Southern Energy Observer magazine, who will speak on the topic "Training Industry Journalists."

## Lecture Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Topic | Training Industry Journalists |
| Speaker | Jiang Zhigao (Executive Editor, Southern Energy Observer Magazine) |
| Date & Time | Tuesday, November 4, 2014, 14:00-16:00 |
| Venue | Classroom E-Jie 203 |
| Organizer | Yangtze River College of Journalism and Communication |

## About the Speaker

Jiang holds a master's degree from the School of Journalism and Communication at Jinan University. He has worked for prominent publications including *Times Person Weekly*, *New Century Weekly*, and *Southern People Weekly*, where he served as senior editor. He previously held the position of executive editor at *New Century Weekly* and currently serves as executive editor of *Southern Energy Observer* magazine.

The college noted that graduating students interested in interning at Southern Energy Observer should not miss this opportunity, and students interested in journalism and industry reporting are also welcome to attend.
