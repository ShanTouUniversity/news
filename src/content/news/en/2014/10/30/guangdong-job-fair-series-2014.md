The Guangdong Provincial Department of Education, under the guidance of the Guangdong Provincial University Graduate Employment Guidance Center, has completed the itinerary planning for the "Guangdong Province 2015 Graduate Series Supply and Demand Meeting Activities." Job fairs will be held across multiple cities, providing employment opportunities for the 2015 graduating class province-wide.

## Event Overview

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | Guangdong Province 2015 Graduate Series Supply and Demand Meeting Activities |
| Organizer | Guangdong Provincial Department of Education |
| Guiding Body | Guangdong Provincial University Graduate Employment Guidance Center |
| Target Audience | 2015 graduating class |

Specific dates, venues, and participating employers for each session are detailed in the attached schedule. Graduates are encouraged to review the fair itinerary, identify events matching their majors and career interests, and participate actively.

> Source: Shantou University Student Affairs Office
